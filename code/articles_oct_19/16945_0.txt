LYNCH LAW.- The negro who lately murd Mr.
Anderson, of Arkansas, has been hung by the citizens
of Bentonville, without a trial. He made a full confession
of his guilt, pointing with his finger at the
same time to a white man, who had, he said, instigated
him to commit the murder. There was a num
ber of persons present who objected to the application
Lyuch law, but upon its being put to vote, a large
of majority were in favor of it.
