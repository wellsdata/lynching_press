Lynched Man Was
Ernest Everetts
Says His Landlady
Centralia, Wash., Nov. 12. The man
who was lynched here Tuesday night was
identified, tonight, as Ernest Everetts.
The identification was made by landlady
of a rooming house where Everetts had
a room for several months. He was
working here as an I. W. W. organizer
the landlady said.,
