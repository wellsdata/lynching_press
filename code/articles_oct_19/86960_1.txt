No Truth in It.
ST. LOUIS, July 24.- A very brief
special
to the Post Dispatch says that one of
the negro county officials of Crittenden
county, Ark., was hanged by a mob at
Marion last night; that the race war has
broken out afresh, and that a terrible
state of affairs prevails.
MEMPHIS, July 24.- The report of the
lynching at Marion, Ark., last night and
further race troubles in Crittenden
is unfounded. county
