George Kennedy, Colored, Victim of
Wrath of a Mob.
Shreveport, La., Oct. 20.- George
Kennedy, a negro, was lynched at Tay
lortown, near here, Sunday night by a
mob of 100 men. It is said Kennedy
had made a murderous assault on Conductor
Brittain of the Valley road, and
that afterward he was found lying in
wait, armed with a pistol, for the confessed
purpose of killing the
or. The mob dispersed quietly conduct.
