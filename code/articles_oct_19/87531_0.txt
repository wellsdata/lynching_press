So Mississippi Mob Lynched His
Brother.
Clarkedale, Miss., Sept. 7.- Unable
to get their hands upon Nathan Mc
Daniels, a negro who is alleged to
have shot and killed Policeman Wal
ter Marshall when the latter attempt
ed to raid a crap game here on Saturday
night, a mob of hundreds of
citizens caught McDaniels' brother,
Hiram McDaniels, and lynched him
from a telegraph pole on the
where Marshall fell dead. spot
