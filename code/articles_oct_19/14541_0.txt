Negroes Burning Bessemer,
BIRMINGHAM, ALA., April 25.
There is considerable excitement
here. A negro was lynched to- day
at Bessemer and the negroes in revenge
have taken possession of the
town and have started to burn it
down. A telegram was received by
the sheriff asking for 100 men and
arms. They have left here armed
with Winchester rifles to protect the
residents of Bessemer. Special trains
will be held all night to carry
