MURDERER LYNCHED IN COLORADO.
DENVER, Nov. 11.- A Canyon City special
says that passengers on the coach from
Leadville last night brought word that a
freighter named J. C. Bailey had been murdered
a few miles this side of Cleora and
about one mile from the line of Chaffee
County, in Freemont County, and that the
murderer was lynched. He was found hang
ing, in his shirt and drawers, to a tree
