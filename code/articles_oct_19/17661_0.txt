TWO LYNCHERS FATALLY WOUNDED.
A Mob Makes An Attack Upon the Jail at
Asheville.
Birmingham, Ala., Aug. 22.- The
News staff corespondent at Asheville
wires that Sheriff North and his deputies
fired on a mob seeking to lynch
Jim Brown, a negro rapist, who was
today convicted, and that Walter
Blankenship and Jamie Blankenship,
two members of the mob, were fatally
wounded. A hundred shots were fired
when the mob retreated, but were reforming
to make another attack. The
sheriff is trying to get communication
with the governor to secure
protection. military
