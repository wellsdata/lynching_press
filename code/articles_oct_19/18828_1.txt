Negro Lynched in Mississippi.
NEW ORLEANS, June 18.- Alexander or
Abraham Barrett, colored, was lynched today
at Nugent station. Misa, on the Gulf
and Ship Island Railroad, a few miles from
Gulfport Barrett a week ago attempted
criminal assault on Anna Ladnier of ancock
county While on her way to school she was
attacked by the negro and hit over the head
with an axe handle. He was carrying her
into a canabrake when the school teacher
saw him and rescued child. He was
captured this morning the and shot to death.
