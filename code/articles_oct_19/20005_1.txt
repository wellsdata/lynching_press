Warned to Leave by Bald- Knobbers.
FORSYTHE, Mo., July 26. When Justice of
the Peace Johns entered his office this morn
ing he found his books and papers had been
removed and thrown into the muddy street.
On his desk he found this notice, written in
pencil:
" If you don't leave the county in ten days
will be lynched. BALD KNOBBERS."
