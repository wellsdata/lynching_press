MOBERLY, Mo., November 17.- A
negro, said to be one of four who on
Thursday night held up and robbed Ed
ward Thompson, a farmer, on the out
skirts of Moberly, was lynched in For
est Park here early Sunday morning.
The negro's three companions, who had
been taken with him from the Macon
Jail and spirited to Moberly by the
mob, escaped from the lynchers. Two
of them later were captured by the
Randolph County officers and are now
in jail at a point unknown. One negro
still is at large, but posses are scouring
the country for him and his capture
momentarily expected. is
