counter part of the recent lynching
of Robert P. Prager in Collinsville, Ill,
was checked here tonight when Chas.
Miller, assistant chief of police, was
able to convince a crowd of fifty men
that it would be better to give Henry
Rheimer, alleged pro- German, whom
they had hanged, a chance before the
council of defense tomorrow morning
rather than to take his life without
unprejudiced hearing. Rheimer was
