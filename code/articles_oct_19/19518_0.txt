VERY ORDERLY
NEGRO LYNCHING
Austin, Texas, March 10.- Governor
Campbell was informed today of
a" very orderly" lynching in Nava
sota county. A negro who attempted
to kill and rob a white man was captured
after a chase of several miles
and lodged in the Navasota county
jail.
According to the sheriff, a crowd
of citizens took the negro from the
jail and hanged him to the nearest
telegraph pole. Not a word was
spoken after the negro was dead.
crowd silently dispersed and
The went to their homes.
