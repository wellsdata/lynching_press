A Negro Brute Lynched.
Nashville, Tenn., Aug. 30.- A negro
named George Johnson was hanged by
a mob at Murfreesboro, 30 miles south
of here, about nine o'clock Friday
night. The negro Friday morning at
tempted an assault upon a Miss Morrival
ris, seven miles from Murfreesboro.
He was captured and taken to Mur
freesboro, where young lady identified
him, and he the confessed.
