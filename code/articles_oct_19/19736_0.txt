WIFE MURDERER LYNCHED.
Hung to a Tree and Henry Wildman
Body Riddled With Bullets.
MONTICELLO, III., Oct. 15.- About 1 o'clock
In the morning about 200 disguised men over
powered the sheriff and broke into the jail,
and took therefrom Henry Wildman, the
wife murderer, and hung him to a
hade tree near the jail. small
