FIVE NEGROES LYNCHED,
INCLUDING TWO WOMEN.
Gainesville, Fla., Aug. 19.- Five
Negroes, three men and two women,
were taken from the jail at Newberry,
Fla., early today and hanged by a mob
and another Negro was shot by deputy
sheriffs near Johnsville, Fla., as the
result of the killing yesterday of
Constable S. G. Wynne and the shoot-
ing of Dr. L. G. Harris by Boisey
Long, Negro. The lynched Negroes
were accused a of aiding Long to escape.
