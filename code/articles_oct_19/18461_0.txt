Negro Lynched in West Virginia.
HINTON, W. Va., May 11.- William Lee,
colored, was lynched here to- night by a
masked crowd of 100 men. He was taken from
the jail at 11:15 o'clock. On Wednesday he
attempted to assault Mrs. H. H. Diefenbach, a
Chesapeake and Ohio telegraph operator at
Sandstone seven miles west of this city. He
confessed the, crime.
