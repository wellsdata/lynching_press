Hanged and Shot to Death,
AUGUSTA, Ga., April 26.- John Patterson
, a negro, was lynched at
Denmark, S. C., to- night by a mob.
He was convicted of rape on a young
white girl by a vigilance committee
organized for the purpose- He was
hanged on a pine tree near the scene
of the outrage and shot to death.
Five hundred citizens participated
the lynching. in
