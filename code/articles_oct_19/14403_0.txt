lo FOUR NEGROES LYNCHED
BY HAMILTON, GA., MOB
O Hamilton, Ga., January 22.- A
mob of 100 men tonight broke into
the Harris county jail here, over
powered Jailer E. M. Robinson and
took four negroes, three men and
one woman, out and hung them to
trees one mile from town. They
then riddled them with builets. It O
is estimated that 300 shots were
fired. The negroes were charged
with the murder of Norman Had
ley, a young farmer.
