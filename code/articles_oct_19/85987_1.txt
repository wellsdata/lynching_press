Negroes Disperse a Lynching Party.
CINCINNATI, Sept. 6.
Last Tuesday evening a young lady was
raped by a negro in Jessamine county, and
Lewis Franklin was arrested as the perpe-
trator of the crime. He was taken to
Frankfort for safe keeping and brought
back to Nicholasville yesterday, Saturday,
for trial. The negroes of Nicholasville,
expecting him to be lynched, armed themselves
last night, and lay in wait near the
jail. About midnight some fifty armed
men approached the jail. They were fired
upon by the negroes, and Joel W. Single.
ton was instantly killed. The remainder
of the party fted. The sheriff has summoned
a large posse to guard the jail
