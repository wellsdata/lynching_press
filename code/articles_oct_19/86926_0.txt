TWO NEGROES LYNCHED
Had Confessed to Burning a Barn and
Are Taken From Custody
of Sheriff.
Monroeville, Ala., Dec. 8.- Calvin
Exele and Sylvester Peyton, negroes,
were found hanging to a tree at
Double Branches, two miles from here.
They had confessed to robbing Edgar
Bass' barn and outhouses. A deputy
sheriff had them in charge and was
bringing them to this place for safe
keeping when he overpowered by
