Offer Reward for
Negroe's Lynchers
ATLANTA, Ga., July 26.- Fifteen
today for the arrest and conviction of
members of the mob that lynched
Marry Washington, a negro, 72 years
old, near Milan, May 26. Governor
Dorsey offered$ 1000 and Dr. Floyd
W. McRae, an Atlanta physician, added
$ 500. On the night of May 24, two
white men are alleged to have invaded
the negro section of Milan and threat-|
ened occupants of several houses.
Washington, armed with a shot gun,
came out of his home and shots were
exchanged, one of the white men was
killed and Washington surrendered.
