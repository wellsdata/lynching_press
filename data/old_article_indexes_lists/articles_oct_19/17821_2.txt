Negro and His Friend Lynched.
Lake Charles, La., Feb. 21- Thomas
Vital, a negro who criminally assaulted
Nora, the 13 year old daughter of Estiev
Miller, was taken from his home near
Fenton last night by a mob and lynch
ed. Samuel Maddox, who attempted to
defend Vital was shot to death. Vital
had been previously shot in the leg by
the girl's father and was not under
arrest yet
