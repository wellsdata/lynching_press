Three More Negroes Lynched.
By Telegraph to the State Chronicle.
BIRMINGHAM, Ala., May 17.- Ber
ry Bowden, Jim Bowlen and Will
Carter, colored, were lynched at
Childersburg, Ala., early yesterday
morning because of an assault upon Mrs.
D. C. Bryant, the wife of a prominent
farmer, last Saturday night. The
men were hung to a railroad
over the Goosa river. bridge
