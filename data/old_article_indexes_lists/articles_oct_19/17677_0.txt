SEARCHING
For Texas Negro Suspected of
Assault.
Ft. Worth, Tex., Aug. 17 .-( Spl.) --
Mrs. J. M. Caldwell, aged 19, a bride
of a few months, was found dead at
her home near Shoman this morning.
She had been crimnally assaulted.
Her head was split open with an ax
and her throat cut from ear to ear.
A party is searching for a suspected
and he will be lynched if
caught negro.
