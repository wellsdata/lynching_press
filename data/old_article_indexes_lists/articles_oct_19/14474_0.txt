Lynch Law in Texas.
GALVESTRN, July 25.- The New's
special from Waco states that Bill
Statworth, arrested last evening in a
neighboring county for threatening the
life of a resident, while asleep under a
tree to- day under guard, a party of
thirty rode up and asked for Statworth.
He answered, when the mob fired, killing
and wounding one of the
guards Statworth.
