RAPE AND LYNCH LAW.- Cincinnati, Ohio,
January 31.- In Frankfort, Ky .. yesterday, a
negro committed a rape on an Irish girl 15
years old, and afterwards threw her over at
embankment at the railroad tunnel, breaking
her shoulder blade and otherwise injuring her.
The negro was arrested and lodged in jait. At
night a crowd of infuriated citizens assembled
and forced the jail, taking the negro and hang
him to a tree Several shots were fired
into the body while. hanging.
