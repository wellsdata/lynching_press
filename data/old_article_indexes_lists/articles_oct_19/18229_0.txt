NEW YORK- Following successful
meetings held in various parts of
New York City during the past few
days, Mrs. Ada Wright, mother of
Roy and Andy Wright, two of the
nine framed up Scottsboro boys, will
speak in Yonkers tonight( Saturday
) at a protest meeting that will
demand a halt to the mass lynching
plans of the southern courts. She
will speak at 8 p m. at 252 Warbur-
ton.
