A Negro Lynched.
ten LEXINGTON, GA., July 10.- A mob of 3,500
, people took Ross Griffith, colored, from
jail a little after midnight, and, marching
fes to the old Jones gallows, swiftly adjusted
the the rope around his neck and hanged him,
warning the negroes not to cut the body
for down. Griffith had assaulted Miss Sallie
Fannie Shackleford in her room in the
residence of her brother, the editor of the
, local paper here, on Friday night.
