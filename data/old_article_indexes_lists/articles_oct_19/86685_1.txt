FEAR REPETITION
OMAHA, Neb., Sept. 29.-( AP) -Despite the quiet
which now prevails here, local police officials fear that to
night will witness repetition of the rioting experienced last
night. Only a few troops are available at present for duty
and it is feared that they may not be able to maintain order
However, General Leonard Wood, commander of the central
department, is expected to arrive late tonight with addition
al troops.

turned over to the mob by other negroes confined in the jail,
