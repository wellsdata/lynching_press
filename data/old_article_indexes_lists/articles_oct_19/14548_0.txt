Coatsville
LOSES LIQUOR LICENSES ON AC-
COUNT OF NEGRO LYNCHING.
( By Associated Press.)
WESTCHESTER, Pa., March 14.
The borough of Coatsville today lost
all its liquor licenses as an indirect
result of the lynching of a negro in
August, 1911, for which crime no per
son has yet been punished.
Of 14 prominent hotels in the county
, 11 were today ordered to close
their bars at and three were giv
en a further hearing once.
