A FIEND'S ACT.
An Eight- Year- Old Child Enticed
Away To Be Outraged and
Murdered.
Alleged Vindication of Ellis, Neal and
Craft, the Ashland Fiends ...
Hoosier Barn- Burners.
DETROIT, Mich., June 13.- Nettie Lyons,
aged 8 year, was enticed away from home at
Cheboygan, last evening, by some fiend, and
was found this morning in a swamp a mile
from home by her father, who had been
searching for her all night. The child bad
been stabbed in the left lung and her body
was badly bruised. Her recovery is doubt
ful. neighborhood is greatly excited
and the The fiend will be lynched if caught.
