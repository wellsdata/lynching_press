Attempt to Lynch lowa Prisoner
Perry, Iowa, Jan. 20.- Three thousand men gathered at the
jail last night and attempted to lynch Stephen Crandall, ar
rested as a suspect in connection with the shooting of Harry
Patee, a bank cashier, Saturday morning. Ropes were secured
and preparations made to hang Crandall. At the request
of the mayor a local company of militia was called out to guard
the jail. The sheriff spirited the prisoner away and took him
to Adel, where he was placed in jail.
Attempts have been made to secure a confession, but Crandall
protests his innocence, saying he was at home all Friday
night. He being held to serve out an unexpired jail sentence
pending is further investigation.
