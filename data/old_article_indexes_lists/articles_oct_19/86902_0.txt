Deputy Sheriff Shot.
Memphis, January 13.- Near Friar's
Point, Miss., Friday night, Deputy
Sheriff James Lucas was shot from
ambush and instantly killed by Andrew
Caylor. The officerad a warrant for
Caylor's arrest for illicit whisky selling,
and was on his way to serve it when
waylaid. Caylor was captured and a
mob organized to lynch him, but the
officers hid him out and thus averted
violence. Caylor's preliminary exam
will be held at Friar's Point to
day ination, and it is feared he will be lynched.
