A War of Races Feared.
NEW ORLEANS, May 14.- Governor
O'Neal, of Alabama, has received advices
from home indicating his presence is need
ed there on account of an apprehended war
of races in Bibb and Chilton counties. The
trouble grew out of the recent assault on a
white woman by a negro, and the lynching
of the negro's father in consequence thereof.
