DISCREDIT TO THE ARMY.
The President Orders a Court of Inquiry
In the Walla Walla Outrage.
SAN FRANCISCO, April 26.- The President
had a conference with Brigadier
Geneneral Ruger this afternoon in regard
to the lynching of the murderer
Hunt at Walla Walla, Wash., last Fri-
day night by a party of soldiers. Subsequently
the President sent the fol:
lowing telegram to the Secretary of
War at Washington:" The affair at
Walla Walla is very discreditable to the
army discipline. If there is reason to
believe, as there appears to be, that the
mob was composed of soldiers, you
will order a court of inquiry, and see
that the responsibility for the disgrace
is fixed, and that the guilty are
to prompt trial. brought
