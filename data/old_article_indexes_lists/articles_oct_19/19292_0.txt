A Psalm- Singing Murderer.
BALTIMORE, September 9.- Ross, the
negro murderer of Emily Brown
( white), was hanged this afternoon. He
went to the gallows singing a hymn.
The crime of Ross was particularly
revolting. The victim was an old white
woman named Emily Brown, who was
beaten to death by Ross, December 10th
last, and her body sold to the medical
college for$ 15. He acknowledged the
killing, but threw the blame upon
Anderson Perry, a colored janitor of
the college, who he said, had made him
drunk and induced him to kill the old
woman. Perry was acquitted of complicity
