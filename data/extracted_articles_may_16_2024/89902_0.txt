NEGRO THIEF LYNCHED
Body Dragged Through Streets After
He Wounded Business Man.
By United Press
HOT SPRINGS, Ark., Aug. 1.-
" Punk" Harris, negro, was lynched
by a mob in front of the Como Hote
here today and his body dragged
through the streets.
Harris was said to have shot and
mortally wounded Maurice Conley,
young business man, last night wher
