SHERIFF JIM WRIGHT MAKES
ANSWER TO THE GOVERNOR
By Associated Press.
Montgomery, Ala., April 4- Sherif
P. W. Jinright, of Bullock county, has
issued a statement in reply to the intimation
of Governor O'Neal that there
existed collusion between the sheriff
and the mob in the lynching of Aber
deen Johnson, the negro, near Union.
Springs, Ala., Sunday afternoon.
In setting forth the facts of the
lynching, the sheriff says that he did
everything in his power to prevent
mob violence, deputizing responsible
citizens to aid him in this endeavor.
He adds that before he was aware of
the move of the mob, he had been dis
armed in the jail yard," forced down
and carried into the court room in
most brutal manner." a
