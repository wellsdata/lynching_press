The People Not Satisfied.
MORRILLTON, ARK., April 19.- The grand
jury yesterday indicted Flannagan Thorn-
ton for murder in the second degree for
killing Constable Pate. The public, disappointed
at the result, took Thornton out
of jail about midnight and strung him up
on Main street and fired a few bullets into
his body The District Attorney is pushing
an inquiry..
