DOUBLE LYNCHING.
Man and Woman Lashed Back to
Back and Hanged.
FT. SMITH, Ark., Jan. 18.- Deputy U. S.
Marshal Liggitt has just come in from the
northern part of the state, where he has
been chasing moonshiners, and reports a
double lynching which occurred in the
mountains. Peter Baker, a farmer, was
found murdered in his house. His wife
and a man named Johnson left the country
, but both returned a few days ago.
The neighbors suspected them of putting
Baker out of the way and an assault was
at once made on the house and the couple
captured. They were lashed together
back to back and then hanged on the
