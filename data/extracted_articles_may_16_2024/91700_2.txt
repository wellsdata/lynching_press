Negro Murderer Lynched- True Bills
Found.
JACKSONVILLE( Fla.), September 30th.- A
negro employed on the St. Johns and Lake
Eustice Railroad killed his white boss. He
was carried to Fort Mason, but at night a
band of men overpowered the guard and
took the negro out and lynched him.
The Grand Jury, investigating the robbery
of the trunks and bodies which came ashore
from the wreck of the Vera Cruz, have found
three bills against Edward Moody and E.
Allen- latter a hotel keeper at Mata-
