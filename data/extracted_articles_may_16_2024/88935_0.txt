MOB LYNCHES NEGRO.
der, Taken From Kentucky Jail.
London, Ky., Oct. 17.- Virgil Bowers
, a negro, was taken out of the
county jail here last night and hung
to a tree. The negro shot and killed
George Farris, a prominent and
wealthy Knox county lumber dealer
on August 26. He was tried by the
Laurel county jury last week. The
jury disagreed, ten for the death
penalty and two being for a life sentence.
