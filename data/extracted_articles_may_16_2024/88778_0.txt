Lynching in Tennessee.
Chattanooga( Tenn.), January
18 .-- At Lynchburg, the county
seat of Moore county, a mob lynch
ek John Toney and Jim Newman,
both colored, early this morning.
The negroes were charged with
numerous robberies in the neighborhood
. Both were sentenced to
the penitentiary for two years for
robbery, but were pardoned by
the Governor.
Returning to
Lynchburg, they inaugurated a
reign of terror by holding up people
and entering and robbing
farmhouses. Monday night they
entered the house of an elderly
white woman, beat her to insensibility
