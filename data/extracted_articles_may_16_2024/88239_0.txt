12- YEAR- OLD NEGRO
ESCAPES LYNCHING.
Boy Accused of Attempted Assault on
Four- Year- Old White Girl.
New Orleans, Oct. 13.- To escape
lynching a 12- year- old negro boy,
known as Jesse James, was brought
here last night from Amite, La., and
placed in the parish prison. The boy
was accused of attempted assault
upon a 4- year- old white girl and it
was necessary for the sheriff at Amite
to take him secretly aboard a train
to escape from lynching. Immediately
after the crime he had been dragged
into the woods by the friends of
the family of the outragde child and
a rope had been placed around his
