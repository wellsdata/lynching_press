IN OKLAHOMA
NEGRO IS LYNCHED
ADEBEL, Okla., April 3.- Oscar
Martin, a negro, was taken from
a justice of the peace court room
here today and hanged by a mob
at the rear of the court house
from which his preliminary trial
had been in progress.
The mob overpowered the officers
in the court room, placed a
rope around the negro's neck,
rushed him out onto a porch, tied
the end of a rope to a porch
railing and pushed the negro off.
He fell about 12 feet and
within a few minutes. died
