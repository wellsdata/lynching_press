Murder and Prospective Lynching.
Special to the Indianapolis Journal

character shot and killed Constable
William Cressin,, at a picnic, this after-
companion when Cressin interfered and
was fatally shot. McCully was arrested,
and to- night the dead constable's friends
gathering in large numbers, and it is
teared are the murderer will be lynched.
