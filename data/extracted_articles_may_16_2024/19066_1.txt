Taken From Court Room and Lynched
( By the Associated Press.)
Smithville, Tenn., August 2.- Charles
Davis, on trial today for criminal assault
, was taken from the court room
and lynched by a mob composed of
friends and relatives of the girl in the
case. Davis tried to jump from the
second story window but was captured.
