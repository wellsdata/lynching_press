NEGROES AS LYNCHERS
Mississippi Blacks Hang a Colored
Uxorcide.
Vicksburg, Miss., Oct. 23.-G. Lesler
Barnes, colored, was lynched by a mob
of his own people in the northern part
of this county last night. In drunken
fury Barnes murdered his wife, stabbed
and badly wounded a negro who interfered
, and engaged in a rifle duel with
white man who attempted to arrest
a him.
