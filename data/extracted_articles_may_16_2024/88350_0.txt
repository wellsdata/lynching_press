Texan Kills Texan in Illinois.
Waterloo, Ill., Nov. 3.-J. S. Rabb
and DeWitt Wilcoxson, who shot and
killed John Maher of Tyler, Texas,
early today, were captured near the
scene of the crime this afternoon, after
a pitched battle in the woods. Wil-
Coxson was shot four times and is
dying. Capt. Herman Wagner of the
Waterloo police force
was shot
through the arm. Rabb, who shot
Maher without provocation, was res-
cued from a mob bent on
him. Rabb is from Beaumont lynching, Texas.
