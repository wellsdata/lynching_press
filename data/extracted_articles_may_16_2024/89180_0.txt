Mob Hangs a Negro.
Hearne, Tex., February 9.- As a result
of a dispute as to some land J. A. Kess-
ler, a section foreman, was shot and killed
today by Rolley Wyatt and Alexander
McKenzie, negroes. Wyatt was captured
and McKenzie escaped. Tonight a mob of
several hundred persons battered down the
doors of the jail and, securing the negro,
hanged him to a nearby tree. Posses are
searching for McKenzie a like fate
