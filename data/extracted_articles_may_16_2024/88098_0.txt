A NEGRO LYNCHED IN TEXAS
A Mob of 5,000 Men Puts an End to
Little Girl's Assaulter at
Dallas.
Dallas, Tex., Mar. 4.- Allen Brooks,
a negro charged with assaulting a 3-
year- old white girl last week, was
lynched here by a mob of 5,000 men.
At noon all the available militiamen
extra police and firemen were ordered,
to the jail. The mayor has issued an
order closing all the saloons. A number
