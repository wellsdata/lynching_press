How the Negroes Feel.
The negroes do not pretend to protect
some of the negroes lynched. The better
class of colored people are against that element
of their race implicated in the at
tempt to murder the officers of the law.
There are no signs of peace now. Black
mobs
are on every street
corner.
Negroes have threatened to break in the
jail and lynch Casper, the white man who
killed a negro at" The Curye" several days
ago. They are holding secret meetings.
