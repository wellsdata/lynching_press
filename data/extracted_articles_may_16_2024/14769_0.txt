TWO NEGROES LYNCHED.
One Attacked White Woman, and
the Other Approved of It,
Paducah, Ky, Oct. 16- Two ne-
groes were lynched by a mob here
today and their bodies burned.
One was charged with attacking
white woman and the other was accused
of voicing his approval of
the action of the other.m
The negroes were taken from the
jail by a mob of citizens, who overpowered
the tail guards, and were
hanged near the home of the
man attacked. wo-
