Soldiers Defied; 2 Lynched.
Greenville, Texas, Oct. 25.- With
three companies of militia on guard
this town is recovering from the excitement
that followed the lynching of
Frank and Louis Williams, negroes,
for an alleged assault on Mrs. Will
Lloyd. The mob which lynched the
negroes took them out of the custody
