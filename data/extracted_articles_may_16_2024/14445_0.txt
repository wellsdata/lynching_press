Sherreman, while arresting Charles
Cobb, alias Smith, at Udell Station, yesterday
, was shot. He died last night.
Cobb was captured by farmers and held
to await the arrival of officers. Before
their arrival an armed party of farmers
hung Smith to a tree. Cobb also killed
