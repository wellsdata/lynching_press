HANGED BY A MOB.
A California Murderer Lynched by a Mob.
FRESNO, Cal., July 27.- Word was
received here this afternoon that Vic
Lor Adam, who murdered Justice I. L.
Baker in cold blood at O'Neal's, Tues
day morning last, met death yesterday
afternoon at the hands of a mob of 60
citizens, friends of the dead justice.
Adam was captured about 30 miles
above O'Neal's yesterday morning by
a brother of the murdered man and two
other men, friends of the judge. They
were returning with their prisoner to
O'Neal's, and when within three miles
of their destination they were met by
