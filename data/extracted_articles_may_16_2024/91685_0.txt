PROSPECTIVE BLOODSHED.
A White Mob Trying to Lynch a Negro
Rape Fiend.
CARLTON, Mo., Sept. 18.- While May
Betzemberger, 15 years old, was returning
home from school yesterday, she
was accosted by Joseph Oliver, a
negro boy, who attempted to assault
her, and in the struggle which
ensued, he stabbed her in the shoulder.
Oliver was arrested, and after his examination
this afternoon a crowd of
white men attempted to lynch him, but
he was landed safely in jail. At nidnight
tonight a mob of whites assembled
about the jail to secure the negro and
lynch him, while opposing negroes are
gathering to protect the prisoner. A
fight between the two mobs is imminent
. Both sides are well armed, and it
is is feared there will be much
shed. blood-
