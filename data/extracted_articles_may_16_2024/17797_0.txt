Value of a Lynchable Negro.
Colored men, when lynched, are
worth$ 2 each, according to the verdict
of a Salina, Kan., jury. Henry Adams
sued the city for$ 5,000 damages for the
loss of his son, who was hanged by a
mob in April. He claimed that the
death of his son represented a pecuniary
loss of that amount.
The jury decided
there had been no actual damage sustained
by the plaintiff, but allowed$ 2
as" nominal" damages.-
Record. Philadelphia
