law previals in Lexington tonight,
Six hundred federal and state troops
are patrolling the streets to prevent
further rioting, which today resulted
in four deaths with 15 persons wounded
, with a mob bent upon lynching
William Lockett, a negro who con
fessed to the murder last week of 10
year- old Geneva Hardman, was fired
upon with rifles and machine guns by
state militiamen as the mob was
storming the courthouse to get the
negro during his trial.
The city was quiet early tonight
but authorities fear a recurrence of
violence. Five hundred additional
