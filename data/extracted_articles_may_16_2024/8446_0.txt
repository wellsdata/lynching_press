TEXAN LYNCH LAW- A man named Ainsworth'
charged with murder, was recently placed in jail in
De Witt county, Texas. A few nights after, a gang
of men proceeded to the prison, and broke open a
door which seeme to have been in the top of the
cell. Through this they then dropped an ignited
turpentine ball, by the light of which they could
plainly see the situation of the prisoner. They then
deliberately murdered Ainsworth, firing successively
four different volleys of buckshot until he was killed.
The cries of the victim for help, and his screams of
pain, were distinctly heard by persons close at hand,
not one of whom, however, would venture to his
