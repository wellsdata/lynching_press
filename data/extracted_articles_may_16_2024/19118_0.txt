ITALIANS KILLED.
Two Are Shot to Death in Mississippi
and Another Badly Wounded.
Vicksburg, July 13.- The reported
lynching of Italians at Avon, Washing.
ton county, Thursday, which was later
denied, turns out after all to be true, except
as to the location. It is now positively
known that John and Victor
Serio, brothers, were shot to death and
Salvator Serberto
probably fatally
wounded by a mob of marked men at
Erwin, shortly after 12 o'clock Wed
nesday night. There were four Italians
in the party which was attacked but
one succeeded in making his
unhurt. escape
