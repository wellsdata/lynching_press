SPRINGFIELD, Ill., Aug. 15.- The lynching of a second negro by
the white mob here occurred this afternoon within two blocks of the State
Capitol and more militia have been ordered out to preserve order. The
first lynching took place this morning, after which the troops engaged
the white rioters, firing into the mob. Two whites were killed and several
others were wounded, one of the latter dying later.
