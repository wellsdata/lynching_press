The attempt to kill the Vice President
was witnessed by a crowd which
filled every available inch of standing
room in Saginaw, and cries of" Lynch
him!"" Lynch him!" filled the air as
McConnell, fighting desperately, was
carried to a patrol wagon.
Even then the anger of the assemblage
could not be quieted, for several
infuriated citizens followed the wagon
to the station, where McConnell, trembling
like a leaf, begged the patrol
men to protect him from the mob.-
