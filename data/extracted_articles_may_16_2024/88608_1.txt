NEGROES LYNCH NEGRO.
Raped a Negro Woman and Is Cut
Into Shreds.
Lake Butler, Fla., July 18.- A negro
tramp named Adams, who assaulted a negro
woman near this place a day or so
ago, was captured last night by a mob
of negroes and lynched in Santa Fe
swamp.
His body was cut into shreds by the
mob and severely. The lynching
was carried out disfigured by negroes entirely.
