NO TRACE OF NEGRO
WHO DISAPPEARED
WHEN MOB ARRIVED
By Assoolated Press
Wilmington, N. C., February 6.- At a
late hour tonight no trace had been found
of the negro, Daniel Petteway, who dis-
alpeared from the Onslow county jail at
Jacksonville, N. C., at an early hour
this morning when a mob shot and
killed in his cell John Daniels, the negro
who last week killed Grover Dickey,
white, farm manager for the Rope Lumber
company, near Jacksonville.
The jailer declares he has not any
idea what became of Petteway in the excitement
attending the lynching of the
other negro, Sheriff Walton believes the
lynched Petteway but admits that
mob his body cannot be located.
