NEGRO LYNCHED.
RICHMOND, Va., Jan. 5.- Information
was received here from Amhurst Court
house of the lynching of a negro name
Columbus Miles near that place on Satur-
day. The sheriff of Amhurst county had
arrested the negro on the charge of outraging
a white lady, and was on the way to
jail with the prisoner when he was overtaken
by an armed body of forty citizens,
who took the negro from the officer
