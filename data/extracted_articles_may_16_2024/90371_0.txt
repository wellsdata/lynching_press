James Ray Gibson, a traveling salesman
, of Tucson, Ariz., was killed on
the Apache Trail near Roosevelt.
Star Daley, alias Ashmore and said to
be a deserter from the army, was arrested
at Mesa on complaint of Mrs.
Gibson who told the officers that
Daley had killed her husband and then
attacked her. Daley was taken to
Phoenix lodged in jail, and later
taken by and a mob of fifty and hanged.
