LYNCHED BY VIGILANTES.
Louisville, Ky., July 11 The Courier
Journal's Russellville special says A
:
inob of six armed and masked men
mounted on horseback came from Todd
county to this place last night between
11 and 12 o'clock, took Dick Henderson
colored, from jail and lynched him.
Henderson was charged with cutting the
throat of young Adams, a white boy, at
Pincheor, Todd county, last April .. He
was brought here from Elkton in the
latter part of May to escape mob violence
Adams is not dead and will
not
die from. his wounds.
