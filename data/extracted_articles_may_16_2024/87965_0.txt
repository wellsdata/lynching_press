NEGRO LYNCHED
TENNESSEE MOB OVERPOWERS
OFFICER, TAKES HIS PRISONER
, WHO CONFESSES.
Memphis, Tenn., Aug. 16.- Constable
Burris of Tiptonville, Tenn., was
overpowered by a mob last night at
Maple, Ky., and Will Clifford, a negro
prisoner, was taken from him,
carried to within one mile of
and hanged. Clifton
