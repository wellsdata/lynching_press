Swainsboro, Ga., May 22.- Ben Smith.
and old negro preacher, was lynched

his wife and fatally wounded Deputy
Marshal Neal Canady, who was called
to the scene. A crowd pursued the
negro, caught him a swamp, hanged
him and filled his body in with bullets.
