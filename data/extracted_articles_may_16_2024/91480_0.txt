NEGRUES ANGRY.
They Want to Lynch a White Prisoner In
Retaliation.
COLUMBIA, S. C., July 16.- The negroes
in Greenville are greatly excited over the
lynching of Ira Johnson, whose lawyer
says he could have established a clear case
of self defense. They want to lynch a
white prisoner in jail charged with murder
of a white man for retaliation.

jail, and another is under arms in its armory
