NEGRO MURDERER
LYNCHED BY MOB
BIRMINGHAM, Ala., May 27.- The
negro, J. Matson, who murdered Deputy
Sheriff Scott Taylor, was captured near
Oxmoor and lynched by a mob near
Pelham last night.
He was hanged to a tree and his body
literally shot to pieces.
Matson killed Taylor near Ac
ton, Ala., Wednesday following a diffi
over some work the negro had
culty promised to do.
