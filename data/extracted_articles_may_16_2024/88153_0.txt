Dead Body Found in the River.
PINE BLUFF, Ark., Sept. 2.- The dead
body of a Negro was found hanging to
a trot line in the Arkansas river, near
Rob Roy. A rope around the neck and
several gashes in the head indicate
that the Negro had been lynched and
thrown into the river. There was
nothing on the body by which the man
could be identified and no lynching
has been reported in that vicinity
