MURDERS IN VIRGINIA. We learn from the
Warrenton,( Va.) Flag of '95, that recently
three dreadful murders have been committed in
Virginia. The first one occurred in Loudon
county in which a man named Reid had his
throat cut from ear to ear in the presence of
his wife, by an individual named Ridgeway.
In Prince William a man killed his wife. The
last is the murder of a negro woman in
per county. Culpep
