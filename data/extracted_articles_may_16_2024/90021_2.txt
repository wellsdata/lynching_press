MEMPHIS, Sept. 9.- The grand jury
sprung a sensation tonight when, at
11:30 p. m., an indictment was returned
against William Cox, the assistant warden
at the county jail, charging him
with perjury in connection with the
lynching of six alleged negro incendiaries
near Millington, Tenn., a week ago,
It is alleged in the indictment that Cox
was cognizant of and a party to a
