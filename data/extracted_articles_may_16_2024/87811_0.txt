WASHINGTON LYNCHED.
Three Thousand People Witness His Taking
Off.
Nashville, Tenn., June 23.- A dispatch to
the Banner from Double Springs, Tenn.,
states that Charles Washington, colored,
who recently assaulted Mrs. Ward near
Mine Lick, Putnam county, was lynched
at Mine Lick to- day. He was captured
near Dayton and lodged in jail at Cooke-
ville, but was taken from prison by the
mob and hanged. The negro said he had
escaped from the New Jersey penitentiary
after serving only a short time. He confessed
his guilt. The lynching witnessed
