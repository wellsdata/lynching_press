Florida Negroes Lynched.
Tampa, Fla.- John Black and Wi
Ham Reagin, negroes, were lynched by
a mob of 200 men near Fort Gardner
The negroe killed Ed Granger, a
white turpentine operator, without
provocation, and were captured by
Sheriff Wiggins and three deputies
near the scene of the killing a few
hours afterward. The mob halted the
sheriff and posse and took possession
of the prisoners. Both confessed and
were hanged to a nearby tree belug
afterward riddled with bullets.,
