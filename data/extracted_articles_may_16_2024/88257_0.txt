NEGRO MURDERER
LYNCHED IN GEORGIA
( Hearst News Special to the Graphic.)
Talbottom, Ga., June 24.- William
Carrelxoa, a negro, was lynched
night for murder of
Tuesday the
William Leonard, a white man. A
blind negro preacher, living with
Carrelxoa, was taken out and whip
. He fell into a creek and was
ped drowned.
