Unsuccessful Attempt to Lynch a Negro.
LOUISVILLE, Nov. 3.- Last night a mob collected
about the jail in Jeffersonville, Ind., with
the intention of lynching a negro named Jno.
Washington, who attempted to outrage a little
white girl, and whose father died from excitement
produced by the outrage. The prisoner
was slipped out by a back way and conveyed to
the State prison learning which the mob dispersed
