LYNCHED A WOMAN.
A Kentucky Mob Hangs an Alleged Poisoner
.
CINCINNATI, March 16.- A Mt. Sterling,
Ky., dispatch says a mob went to the
house of Mrs. Wigginton, whose husband
and sons are charged with poisoning Mr.
Ferguson and Mr. Watts, and hanged her,
and now are searching for Mr. Wigginton,
who has flown. Two of Wigginton
boys are in jail, which is strongly the guarded.
