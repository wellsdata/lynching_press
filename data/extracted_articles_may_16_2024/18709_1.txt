Firemen Almost Lynch a Gambler.
Canton, Pa., Aug. 17.- A volunteer
firemen's convention here ended in a
lively riot and attempted lynching. A
large number of gamblers were attracted
to the place. The gamblers
did a flourishing business, and people
were robbed right and left. Soon after
the parade a mob of about a hundred
firemen got after the gamblers, who
were roughly handled and some of
them quite badly hurt. The mob got
the rope around the neck of one, but
he managed to throw it off, and a policeman
rushed him into a hotel, the
windows in which were broken by the
mob Another gambler took refuge in
a private. house.
