NEGRO LYNCHED FOR SLIGHTLY
WOUNDING AN OFFICER.
Lifeless Body. Riddled With Bullets,
Found Hanging to a Tree Near
Graceton, Tex., Thursday.
Gilmer, Tex., Oct. 5.- Will Spencer
, a negro, was lynched last night
near Graceton, Upshur county,
where his body was found this
morning hanging to a tree and riddled
with bullets.
Spencer, it is said, fought a pistol
duel with Constable Harrell when
the latter tried to serve a writ of
attachment on him Wednesday.
Harrell was slightly wounded.
It is supposed that the negro was
taken from jail last night by mob
of citizens after his arrest." a
