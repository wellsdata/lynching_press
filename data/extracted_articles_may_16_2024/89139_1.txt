No appeal having been taken in the
case of William Dennis, his life sentence
for murder was affirmed at the suggestion
of the attorney general. The affirmation
in the case of Will Horn was expected
. He is the negro who murdered
the boss of the chain gang. Smith, at
Hattiesburg, several months ago, and who
freely admitted the crime while in jail
in this city to save him from the vengeance
of the mob that lynched two of
companions. The case was tried in
Harrison his county on charge of venue,
