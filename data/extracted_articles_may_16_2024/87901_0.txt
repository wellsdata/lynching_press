The passage of the Toney bill preventing
mob violence and lynching,
the Rowland bill providing for the
establishment of a home for tuberculosis
sufferers and the Fletcher bill
extending jurisdiction over the Mis-
sissippi river, the defeat of the Keel
bill preventing corporations from giving
information in regard to destination
of shipments and the usual closing
exercises were the features of
Wednesday's session in the House.
The House was declared adjourned
sine die at 15 minutes of 1 o'clock,
closing exercises consuming more
the time than was anticipated.
