A Murderer Lynched.
ST. LOUIS, March 16.- A Kansas City special
says that Tom Gordon, tne desperado who
killed the City Marshal of Socorro in New
Mexico a few days ago, but escaped and fled
to Albuquerque, was arrested there, and on
Friday last taken back to Socorro, where he
was hanged to a tank by a mob of 200
or 300 men. water
