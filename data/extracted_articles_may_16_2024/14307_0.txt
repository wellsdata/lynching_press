Negro Lynched at Laramie.
Laramie, Wyo., Aug. 30.- Joe Martin
, a negro" trusty" in the county jail,
attempted to assault Miss Della
Krause in the jail kitchen yesterday
afternoon. In the struggle he slashed
her face and arms with a razor. Mrs.
Cook, wife of the sheriff, threw her
arms around the negro and prevented
him from killing the young woman.
He was subdued by jail officials and
placed in a cell.
Shortly after 8 o'clock last night sey-
eral hundred men went to the jail,
overpowered the sheriff, took his keys
and entered the cell. The negro was
dragged outside the jail and hanged to
a lamp post a short distance from the
jall. He fight and hurt several
men before showed being lynched.
