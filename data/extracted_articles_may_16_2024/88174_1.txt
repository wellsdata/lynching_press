Mob Lynches Murderer's Brother.
Citizens of Portland, Ark., lynched
Joseph Blakely, a colored man, the
brother of Sam Blakely, who shot and
killed Deputy Sheriff Cain. Unable to
catch the alleged murderer of Cain,
the mob lynched the brother, after he
declared that he intended to" get"
Harper, who had killed a dog belonging
Bud to the Blakely brothers.
