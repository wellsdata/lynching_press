MOB LYNCHES NEGRO.
Man Accused of Trying to Enter
Woman's Room.
ELKTON. Oct.
Ky .. 12. Shortly
after midnight a mob of about one
hundred men, supposed to be from
Trenton, took Frank Leavell. a one
legged negro, from jail here and
lynched him in a nearby woods.
Leavell was arrested on the charge
of attempting to enter the room of a
woman at Trenton last Saturday
young night. He confessed.
