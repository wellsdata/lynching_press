Ky., mobbed and lynched a Negro the
first of the week who was simply
charged with assaulting Mrs. Chester
Crawford, a white lady. To prove
that the white Christian gentlemen
were simply savages and not in favor
of law and order, they rushed into
the day coach, in which the Negro
