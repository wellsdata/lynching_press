HANGED TO A TREE.
Negro Ravisher Taken from Jail by
a Lousiana Mob and Lynched.
MONROE, La., Oct. 2.- Wash Ferren, the
negro who had criminally assaulted the two
Ladrum children, near Cadeville, was taken
from jail by a mob at 6 o'clock and hanged
to the limb of a tree in front of the courthouse
. From 4 o'clock until the time for
the hanging the street in front of the jail
crowded. There was no concealment
was about it.
