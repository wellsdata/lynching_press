AN AGED CRIMINAL.
Mob Anxious to Lynch Negro Who is
70 Years Old.
Dallas, Tex., July 20.- A special to the
News from Tyler, says that two companies
of militia have been ordered by
special trains to Rusk, Henderson county
, for the protection of a negro who
is upward of 70 years of age and who
is confined in the jail at Rusk charged
with criminal assault on the daughter
of a planter. Captain Bonner, with forty
of the State Guard left for Rusk at 10
o'clock tonight and another company has
been ordered from Marshall by the adjutant
general.
The victim of the negro's alleged assault
is highly connected and her relatives
