Passengers from San Francisco report, that
the supply of gold from the mines was great, and
that the city was quite recovering from the fire.
The best proof that the losses on the merchandise
have not been great, is, that the markets
have been unaffected.
Lynch Law seems now to be the law of the
land in all parts of California. Alas, that there
seems to be overwhelming necessity for it.
