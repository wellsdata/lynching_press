A Negro Fiend Lynched.
GRENADA( Miss.), March 17.- Alex-
ander Anderson, a young negro, attempted
to criminally assault a school
girl last night at 9 o'clock. Three hours
later he was captured by a mob, confessed
and was hanged. Anderson was
made to climb a cottonwood tree with a
rope around his neck. He was
pushed off a limb and strangled to death then.
