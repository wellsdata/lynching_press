They Said it Was Justifiable Homicide.
An unmarried daughter of Ben Brookins,
residing in Baldwin County, Ga., gave
birth to an illegitimate child a few days
ago, and upon being questioned by her
father confessed that a negro named Coop
er was the father of it. Mr. Brookins immediately
shot and killed the negro, and
Coroner's jury returned a verdict of
the justifiable homicide. He was released.
