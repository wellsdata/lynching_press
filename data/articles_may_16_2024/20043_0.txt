A Mob Lynches& Negro Who Shot a Policeman
.
FRANKLIN Tenn., April 80.- The city was
crowded with negroes yesterday, who came to
attend a 10- cent circus. Jim Taylor became
very troublesome and shot Policeman Charles
Cook, who was trying to arrest him. He was
afterward lodged in jail. About 10 o'clock last
night an organized mob attacked the jail. overpowered
the Sheriff, took the keys from him,
and escorted the negro down a side street to
the covered bridge and hung him up to a cross
beam. It was done in an orderly manner under
recognized leaders. The body was then
riddled with and left hanging. after
which the mob bullets dispersed.
