CROWD ANGRY FROM AN
SHERIFF SAVES NEGROES
Mob Cries Lynch" Them" When
Protected Boy
Gamblers Slash Man Who
SENACA, Kan., Sept. 12.- Jim and
Ollie Johnson, negroes. of Centralia,
are in jail here. lacking by only a narrow
margin being the victims of an infuriated
mob. The men had been betting
on the races at the Centralia log
rolling last evening and, being losers,
attacked a boy bystander. This called
for interference by Sam Murphy, a
white spectator. The negroes at once
Murphy fatally with knives
and slashed struck him after he fell.
A crowd with ropes quickly gathered,
crying" Lynch them! Hang them!" but
the negroes were spirited away to the
jail at this place, followed by an angry
throng which surrounded the prison
through, the night.
