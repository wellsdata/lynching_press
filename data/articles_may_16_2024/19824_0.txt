Three Murderers Lynched.
By Telegraph to the State Chronicle.
TOCOA, Ga., May 17. Three
negroes, Jim Pelt Nunly, Gus Roberts
and Bob Anderson, who murdered
Constable Carter several days ago,
were lynched at an early hour this
morning at Clarksville, the county
seat. The men were engaged in robbing
the Tocoa bank when the constable
, surprising them, they beat
