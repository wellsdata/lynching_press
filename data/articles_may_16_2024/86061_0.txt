Ashland Murderer Lynched.
CATTLETSBURG, Ky., June 3.- A masked
mob at 3 this morning came by rail from
Ashland, took George Ellis Jout of the jail at
Cattletsburg, took him to Ashland, near the
scene of the murder and outrage of the Gibbons
girls last December and hanged him to a
sycamore tree. The mob was masked. Geo.
Ellis was convicted of manslaughter yester
day on an indictment for the murder of these
girls It was his testimony that convicted
William Neal and Ellis Craw, who are now
in jail at Lexington, Ky. George Ellis and
these two were taken by river from Cattlets
burg under military escort last January
