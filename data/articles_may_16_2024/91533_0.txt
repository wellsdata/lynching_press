Georgians Jailed
For Negro Killing
Americus, Ga., April 14- Billy De-
van, Henry Harvey and Henry Lewis
Fulford, while residents of Schley
county, were sentenced yesterday to
serve from one to ten years on the
chain gang for their part in the
lynching of Will Jones, a negro, near
Ellaville, several weeks ago. The
men were convicted on ajoint trial
on the charge of shooting at another
man. The jury recommended misdemeanor
punishment, but this rec-
ommendation was disregarded by
Judge Littlejohn before whom the
men were tried.,
