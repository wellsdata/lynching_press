**
( By Federal Wireless Telegraph.)
NEW ORLEANS, May 27.
( Special to The Advertiser) -N.W.
Green, a wealthy negro, was lynched
at Milton Flat today because
he insisted on riding in Pullman
cars in States where the Jim Crow
law is effective. Green, who was
supreme chancellor of the Negro
Knights of Phythias, was removed
a train, taken to the woods
from by a mob and lynched.
