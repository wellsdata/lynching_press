NEGRO MURDERER LYNCHED.
Cairo, Ill, Nov. 11.- Will James, the
negro suspected of being the murder
er of Miss Annie Pelley, was killed
here tonight by a mob. James was
strung up to the public arch, the rope
broke and at least 500 shots were
poured into his body. He made a partial
confession and implicated the negro
