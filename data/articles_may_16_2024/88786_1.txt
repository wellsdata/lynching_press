In Dooly county, Georgia ,, Tony Sut-
ton and his son, who killed an officer
sent to arrest, were lynched.
A passenger them' ain ran into an open
jail, from which they were taken.
At 7:30 o'clock at night Governor
Turney received a telegram from the
sheriff, calling for troops. One hundred
of the state guard were speedily under
arms and at the station, ready to leave
at 9 o'clock. At 8:15 a telephone message
was received saying that the jail
had stormed and the prisoners had
been been lynched.
