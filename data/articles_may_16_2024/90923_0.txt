This is all the space the leading
white newspapers throughout the land
could devote to the brutish acts of
W. K. Beecher, and he will become a
prince among the ladies, for many of
them admire men like Beecher, who
has mastered the art of raping young
girls; but if he had been a Negro
these same papers would have urged
the Christian mob to lynch and burn
body of the" burly black brute at
the the stake."
