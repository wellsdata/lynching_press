Another Negro Lynched.
COURTLAND, Ala., Nov. 8.- Andrew
Sloss, a negro, was taken from jail here
by a mob and hanged to a tree on the
outskirts of the town. He was arrested
on a charge of attempting criminal assault
upon Mrs. E. W. Bussey near
Moulton, the victim having been choked
into insensibility in her room,
night. Tuesday
