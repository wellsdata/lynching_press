BATTLE IN A CHURCH.
Bloodthirsty Kentuckians Desecrate
Place of Worship to See Who Is
" Best Man."
Owingsville. Ky .. Jul 18.- Telegrams
from Beattyville, Ky., say John
Miller of Breathitt county shot and
killed James Crees and wounded James
M. Thomas during a fight in a church
on Fraley creek last night. Miller entered
the church, according to report,
and announced that he was the best
man in the house. Crees and Thomas
disputed this and the shooting fol,
lowed. A mob was formed to lynch
Miller but the sheriff landed the
oner safely in Beattyville jail. pris
