Miller, of Breathitt county shot and
killed James Crees and wounded James
M. Thomas during a fight in a church
on Fraley creek last night. Miller entered
the church, according to the report
, and announced that he was the
best man in the house. Crees and
Thomas disputed this and the shooting
followed. A mob formed to lynch Miller
but the sheriff landed the prisoner
safely, in the Beattyville jail.
