Negro Murderer Lynched.
FORT WORTH, Tex., June 29.-Mr. and
Mrs. Waite were fired upon by a discharged
negro, John Williams. Mrs. Waite
was instantly killed and Mr. Waite mortally
wounded. The negro was captured
by a sheriff's posse. An enraged mob
took Williams to the public square,
