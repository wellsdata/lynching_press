THEY STRUNG HIM UP.
A Negro Brute Lynched by Enraged Citizens
of Miller County.
Arlington, Ga., May 21 .-( Special.) -- News
has just reached here of a terrible crime
in Miller county. In three miles of Colquitt
live a family by the name of Smith and
they have a fourteen- year- old daughter
who attends a school four miles from their
home. It is customary for the father to
bring her home
meet his daughter and
every evening; but yesterday he was late
and the young lady started home
coming alone.
