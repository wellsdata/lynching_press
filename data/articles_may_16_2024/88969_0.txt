Mob's Anger Increases After Lynching
Negro at Crisfield, Md.
Crisfield, Md., July 29. Inflamed
with passion which seemed to increase
yesterday after lynching James Reed,
the negro murderer of Policeman
Daugherty, the mob which put the negro
to death and buried his body in a
swamp, returned to the spot early today
, dug up the body amid yells and
curses, and burned it, after riddling the
with bullets. It is feared by
many corpse that a race war will follow.
