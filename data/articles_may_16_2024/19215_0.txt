Watertown, N. D., Dec. 19.- August
Justunen, the Hamlin county farmer
who confessed to killing his wife and
baby, was brought back to Watertown
vesterday after having been taken to
Castlewood to face preliminary
hearing on a charge of a murder.
Justunen waived the hearing and
was held for trial in circuit court
without bail. He will be kept in the
Codington county jail to avoid the
possibility of a lynching.
Justunen said he did not want an
attorney when questioned by the ex-
Lamining magistrate. It is understood
will be provided and that the
counsel defense will be heard on insanity.
