MIDDLESBOROUGH, Ky., March 26.- At
Cumberland Gap, Tenn., last evening, J. A.
Burke, a telegraph operator, and Tom Hunter
( colored), had a row over some trifling
matter. The negro left and, procuring a shot
gun, laid in ambush for Burke and when the
latter came along shot and killed him. The
negro was captured after a long chase. This
evening Hunter was forcibly from the
authorities by a mob and lynched taken.
