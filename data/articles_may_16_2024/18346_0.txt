NEGRO LYNCHED.
Prominent Young Society Man
Of Augusta, Ga., Shot
Last Night.
[ News- Democrat Leased Wire Service.]
Augusta, Ga., May 14.- William Wil
son, a negro, about 25 years old, was
lynched this morning about 11:30 for
the murder of Aleck Whitney. Whit
ney was one of the most popular young
men in the city and a graduate
society of Cornell University.
