The Lynchings in Georgia.
Bainbridge, Ky., July 25.- Decatur
and Early counties, Ga., are still greatly
excited over the recent lynchings of
negroes. One negro has been lynched
by hanging after his body had been
mutilated, two have been found dead
and their sealps reported to be on exhibition
at Saffold, Ga., while the
bodies of two others were found lying
near a railroad track in the disturbed
district. The cause of their death is in
doubt. Crowds are still hunting for the
five other negroes who were implicated
