Hanged to Telegraph Pole.
Sullivan, Ind.( Special) .- James Dil
lard, the Kentucky negro who criminally
assaulted Mrs. Mary Davis, of Sullivan
county, and Mrs. John Lemon, of Knox
county was hanged to a telegraph pole
one mile east of John Lemon's farm by
a mob. Dillard was captured at Law
renceville, Ill .. after a battle with the
town marshal, during which the negro
shot three times and severely
was wounded.
