-Jack Reilly, who was accustomed to
drink heavily and then shoot off lamp chimneys
with a revolver, was placed in the calaboose
at Stevens Point, Wis., for a brutal
assault. The other night unknown parties
went to the jail and shot him dead. In Wayne
county, Tenn., a negro McLain,
under arrest for criminal assault upon a
young white woman, was riddled with bullets
, and the Sheriff was killed while de
fending his prisoner. In Kaufman county,
Texas two negroes were lynched for a
similar, crime.
