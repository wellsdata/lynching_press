Negro Murderer Lynched.
Washington, N. C., March 25.- The body
of James Walker, the negro who poisoned
the whole family of Dr. David T. Tayloe
of the state board of medical examiners,
was found dangling from a limb of tree
just outside of the town limits early a this
morning. The coroner's jury brought in a
verdict that the negro came to his dea
by lynching at the hands of unknown
parties The negro was about 19 years of
age..
