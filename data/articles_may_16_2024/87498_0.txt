LYNCHING. The two negroes who murdered
Gerand Hayton, near LaGrange, Texas, on the
5th ult., were hung on the 15th ultimo. A letter
in the Texas Presbyterian says:" The citizens
of the county assembled according to previous
notice, add upon taking the vote, about two
hundred were in favor of an immediate trial, and
four opposed. A jury of twelve men was then
selected, who, after hearing the evidence and
confession, sentenced the two murderers to be
hung immediately, which sentence was
the same evening." excuted
