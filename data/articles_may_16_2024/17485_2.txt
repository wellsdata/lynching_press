Harrison Grieved Over a Lynching.
WALLA, WALLA, WASH., April 28. At
the parade last evening the commanding
officer of this post had a dispatch read
from General Ruger, commanding the division
of the Pacific expressing a sense of
grief of President Harrison at the act of
soldiers in lynching Hunt on Friday after-
noon and declaring the necessity of better
conduct in the future and full explanation
of the crime. Private Miller, who was
by Hunt, died last night. shot
