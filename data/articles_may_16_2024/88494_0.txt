THE LAST OF LYNCHINGS.
From the New York World.
The Hempstead( Tex.) mob that took
two convicted negroes about to be sentenced
to death, and who would have been
legally executed within a month, and
lynched them in the public square, strikingly
illustrated the pure love of lynching
for its own sake which seems to be
gaining ground in too many parts of the
country, north and west as well as south.
There was no chance of these two criminals
escaping death by" due process of
law." The mob simply lusted for the
cruel sport of killing them in its own unceremonious
and brutal way. Wherever
lynch law prevails civilization is
