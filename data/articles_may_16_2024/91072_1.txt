Another incident that showed the
calibre of the man occurred when he
was mayor of Chicago. The Chicago
Woman's Club had invited Ben Till-
man, the notorious Negro baiter, to
lecture to its members. Governor
Dunne was asked to preside at that
meeting. He refused because Tillman
was an advocate of lynching. Tillman
was informed of the mayor's reason
