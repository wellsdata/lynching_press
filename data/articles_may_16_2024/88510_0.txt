City Marshall Killed
Fall City, Neb., October 28.- The city marshal
of the town of Rulo was shot and
killed by" Bill" Hunt last night. Hunt
was disturbing speakers at a political
meeting and the city marshal attempted to
arrest him, when Hunt shot him twice,
killing him instantly. An effort was made
to form a mob to lynch him, but he was
hurried away to the county jail at
