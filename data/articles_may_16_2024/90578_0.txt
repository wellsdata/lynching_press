MOB HANGS ALLEGED MURDERER
FORT SMITH, Ark., Nov. 20.- According
to a long distance telephone
message received here tonight from
Mountain Home, Ark., Baxter county,
a crowd of 25 citizens met members of
a posse who were en route to Cotter
with T. T. Lee, captured late today following
a hunt through the mountains
of Arkansas and southern Missouri for
several days, and hanged him to a tree.
Lee was arrested in connection with
the killing several days ago of V.
Loba, a farmer. K.
