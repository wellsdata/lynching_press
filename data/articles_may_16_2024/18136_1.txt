Second Negro Strung Up.
Monroe, Ga., June 27.- The same
mob which, early today lynched Tom
Allen, the negro accused of attacking
a white woman in this county,
stormed the jail here soon after noon
today and lynched Joe Watts, another
negro, who was being held on suspicion
. No charge had been made
against him. He had been arrested
prowling around the home of
while a white man.
