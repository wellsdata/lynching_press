MOB LYNCHES
SHOP OWNER
FOR MURDER
KEY WEST, Fla., Dec. 27.- Manuel
Head, a coffee shop owner, was taken
from jail here and lynched as a result
of the shooting of W. H. Decker, a
prominent resident. After Head had
been beated by masked men, he was
strung up to the telephone pole and
his body riddled with bullets.
Head stood off an armed mob Sun
day night for several hours, finally be
ng captured. Marings guarding the
jail relieved by deputies, who
surrendered were Head to the mob.
