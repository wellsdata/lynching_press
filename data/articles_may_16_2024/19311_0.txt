CAPTURE AND CONFESSION.
Escape From Lynching and Final
Plea and Sentence.
Special to the Globe.
LITTLE FALLS, July 18.- Bulow's
plan was to dispose of the team and es
cape to the wilds of Hubbard county,
and with that idea in view, after hiding
the body, he drove rapidly to the city.
Following the river, he took the road
back of town, crossed the bridge, and
directed the horses toward Wadena
county. Saturday night he spent with
a farmer near the county line, and on
Sunday, while the hue and cry was being
raised, he went leisurely toward
Verndale, where he arrived on Tuesday.
There he very ignorantly proceeded to
dispose of the team, and aroused suspicion
by expressing the desire to either
sell mortgage horses and wagon.
