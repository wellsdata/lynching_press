FUNERAL OF FIRST VICTIM.
Newark, July 9.- Thousands attended
the funeral today of William How-
ard, whose death on Friday resulted in
the lynching of Carl Etherington, an
anti- saloon raider, who shot him. There
was no demonstration. The officiating
clergyman did not refer to the incidents
surrounding his death.
Seward will on
Judge announce
whether he will call a special
Monday jury to investigate the lynching.
