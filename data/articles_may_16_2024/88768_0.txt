LYNCHING
LEXINGTON, Ky., Feb. 9- Four persons
were killed and several injured
when a mob, intent on lynching Will-
iam Lockett, a negro, slayer of a 10-
year- old girl, today charged the courthouse
during his trial.
As the mob approached the courthouse
the police and state troops
opened fire. It is reported that five
women were shot and wounded.
During the shooting authorities slipped
away with the negro. Great excitement
prevails and there are
ings of further trouble. mutter
