LYNCHERS ON THE MOVE.
A Tennessee Nezro Who Hid in a Lady's
Bedroom to Be Hanged.
NASHVILLE, May 18.- About 10 o'clock
last night a telegram was received in this
city saying that a mob of sixty citizens had
organized at Tullahoma, Tenn., seized an
engice and was preparing to go to Man
chester to take a negro from jail and lynch
him. At 2 o'clock yesterday morning a
negro was discovered in the bedroom of the
inspector. The young lady screamed and
the negro fled. It is supposed that his intention
was to commit a criminal assault
upon Miss Baird. Information is that the
negro is doomed. The jail can be easily
broken into, as it is only an ordinary
