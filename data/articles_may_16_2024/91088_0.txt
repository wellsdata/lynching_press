Lynched in Omaha Negro
Following an assault on a young
lady at Omaha, by a negro, a mob
dragged him from the top floor of the
jail Sunday hanged, shot and burned
him and dragged him through the
streets. The mayor urged them to let
the law take its course, when he was
struck several times and nearly was
mobbed. He was unconscious until
Monday morning and is still in a critical
condition. The court house and
were badly damaged by fire and
many jail documents were destroyed.
