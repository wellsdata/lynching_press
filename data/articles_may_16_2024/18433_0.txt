ANOTHER NEGRO LYNCHED.
He Was Charged With Attempt to
Murder a White Woman.
MERIDIAN, Miss., May 16.- Parties arriving
here tonight bring the news of the
lynching of a negro, Sam Hinson, by a
mob of white men near Cuchusta.
Hinson was employed on the farm of
Mrs. Eliza Adams, and attempted to
murder her. He was prevented by the
arrival of neighbors, who heard Mrs.
Adams scream. The negro fled, but
