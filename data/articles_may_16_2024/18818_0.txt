MOB LYNCHES NEGRO.
Troops Ordered from Atlanta Too
Late to Save the Black.
At Tallapoosa, Ga., Friday, Ben
Brown, a negro, charged with having at
tempted to criminally assault Mrs. Henry
Dees, a white woman, was taken from
the county jail here by a mob of 300
men and lynched.
Troops had been ordered from Atlanta,
but did not arrive until an hour and
half after the negro had been hanged a.
