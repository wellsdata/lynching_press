Prospects for Lynching Good.
Atlanta, Ga., Feb. 28.- An unknown
negro entered the residence of Mrs. Bu-
chanan in the heart of the city this
morning and compelled her to cook his
breakfast. Then tieing her feet and
hands he quietly ate his breakfast.
after which he set fire to the house by
placing a quantity of paper in a bed
where he had forced Mrs. Buchanan to
lie. The negro escaped without being
detected. Mrs. Buchanan screamed and
assistance came before the fire had
gained much headway. A search is
being made for the negro. now
