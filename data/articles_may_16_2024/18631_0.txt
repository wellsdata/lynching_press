THREE NEGROES LYNCHED.
An Arkansas Mob Hangs the Murderous
Assailants of a Merchant.
CAMDEN, Ark., May 10.- Three ne
groes were lynched at Bearden early
this morning for a murderous assault
Saturday upon Jesse Norman, a prominent
young business man of that
place, with an axe as he was
ing home from his store at midnight return.
