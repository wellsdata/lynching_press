NEGRO LYNCHED
For Outrage and Murder Committed a
Week Ago.
Raleigh, N. C .. August 25.- Tom
Jones, the negro charged with criminally
assaulting Mrs. William Smith
near Seven Springs last Friday, inflicting
injuries from which Mrs. Smith
died today, was lynched near the scene
his crime this afternoon by ten men.
of The negro was captured early today,
and after being positively identified as
Mrs. Smith's assailant was placed in a
barn to await the sheriff's arrival.

the barn and demanded Jones'
surrender. Upon being refused the men
battered down the doors, and placing
Jones on a hand car carried him into
