Negro Assailant of a Child Lynched.
COLUMBIA, S. C., May 12.- The third
lynching in this state within less than
three weeks occurred four miles west
of the town of Laurens about 5 o'clock
Wednesday evening, when a colored
man, having confessed to an assault,
was strung to a limb. His intended
victim was an 11- year- old daughter
