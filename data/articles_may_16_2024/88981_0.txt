Negro Murderer Is Lynched.
Baton Rouge, La., Dec. 24.- Simon
Cadors, negro, convicted of the murder
of James Norman, a wealthy plant-
, who had appealed his case to the
supreme court after having been sentenced
to be hanged, was taken from
West Baton Rouge jail during the
night and lynched. The body was
ound hanging to a telegraph
placarded" The inevitable penalty pole."
