INNOCENT NEGRO IS LYNCH
VICTIM OF FLORIDA MOB
Jacksonville, Fla., Aug. 26.- Arrests
were promised shortly by Henry
Lilienthal, county detective, tonight
after an investigation of the lynching
last midnight of Ben Hart, Negro,
whose bullet riddled body, with the
hands manacled, was found in a ditch.
Investigators today said the mob
had lynched innocent Negro.
