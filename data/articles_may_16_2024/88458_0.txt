Key West, Fla., June 25.- For forty-
eight hours this city has been on the
verge of a race war. Mrs. Maggie Atwell,
white, was ravished on Wednesday afternoon
by Sylvanus Johnson, colored,
who was promptly identified and jailed.
Two unsuccessful attempts were made
by a mob to get to him to lynch him and
these efforts greatly enraged the blacks.
As a measure of precaution against an
outbreak on either side the Island City
Guards, a local military company, slept
at their armory last night
