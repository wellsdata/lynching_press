Uncle of a Murderer Lynched,
NATCHEZ, Dec. 28.- From Black Water,
La., the place where T. J. Hart was murdered
Christmas eve, comes the news that
an uncle of the murderer was hanged last
night by the searching party. Developments
go to show that there was a plot to
murder young Hart in which several
negroes are implicated, and the citizens of
that neighborhood have to rid
themselves of the entire determined gang.
