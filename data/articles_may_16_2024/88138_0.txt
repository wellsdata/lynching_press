NEGRO SWUNG UP.
Insulter of Young Lady Taken Out by
Mob and Hanged.
Lofton, Ga., Aug. 11.- Charlie Lokie,
a negro boy, about eighteen years of
age, was lynched here for making insulting
remarks to a prominent young
lady of this place. The lynching was a
very quiet one. The negro was a desperate
character, and had been under
the eyes of the people for some time.

lynching. Several hunder negroes and
