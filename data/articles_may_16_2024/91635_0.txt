Murderer Lynched.
VICKSBURG, Miss., April 25.- News has
just been received of a murder and lynching
in Point Coupee Parish. A wealthy
storekeeper named Cotton was alone in his
store, when a tramp came in and demanded
$ 100. Cotton refused, and after some words
the tramp fatally wounded him with a pistol
. He was quickly captured and
up to the nearest tree. strung
