CAPTURED CHIAPAS
The Man Who Wiped Out the Tala-
mantes Family.
Bisbee, Aug. 19.- News was received
here tonight that Francisco Chiap-
as, slayer of the sons of Col. Tala-
mantes, was caught at Nogales on
Friday in a pullman car, by Captain
Escalante.
A mob tried to lynch him, it is
reported, but he was finally lodged
in jail. Chiapas that he will
be executed without fears trial.
