Attempt to Lynch Smith.
Raleigh, N. C., August 25.- Governor Rus-
sell today received a telegram from Solicitor
Ferguson of the Twelfth district,
dated Waynville, saying that a mob was
preparing to lynch Burt Smith, in jail
there charged with assaulting a woman,
and asking that the local militia be placed
under the sheriff's orders. The governor
ordered Captain Springfield, of Company
H, First regiment, to report to the sheriff
and protect the prisoner from mob
