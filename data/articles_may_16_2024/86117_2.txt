KILLED WHILE RESISTING ARREST.
SPRINGFIELD, Mo., June 13.- Arneas Ridge,
while resisting arrest, was shot and killed by
a sheriff's posse at Danforth's saw mill, fifteen
miles south of this city. Ridge was wanted
on a charge of assaulting two negroes. He
was grandson of John Ridge, chief of the
Cherokees a, and cousin of Col. Boudinot.
