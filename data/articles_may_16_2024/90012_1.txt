MOB LAW.
What Lynching Demonstrates.
[ Pittsburg Dispatch.]
The lynching fever has manifested itself
again, this time in Indiana, where white caps
and other phases of mob rule have manifested
themselves for some years. The lynching was
of the stereotyped variety, and has none of the
South Carolina features of hanging innocent
men for an example, or making a holiday by
torturing the victims after the fashion of
Apaches. A mob of 100 masked men took a
murderer out of jail and hanged him to a tree
in sight of the railroad track" in the most orderly
manner."
