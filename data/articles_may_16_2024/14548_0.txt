Coatsville
LOSES LIQUOR LICENSES ON ACCOUNT
OF NEGRO LYNCHING.
( By Associated Press.)
WESTCHESTER, Pa., March 14.-
The borough of Coatsville today lost
all its liquor licenses as an indirect
result of the lynching of a negro in
August, 1911, for which crime no person
has yet been punished.
