NEGRO LYNCHED.
An Attempted Outrage on a White
Woman the Cause.
MERIDIAN, Miss., May 6.- A lynching
occurred at Butler. Choctaw county, Ala.,
last night. A negro named George H.
Hoes attempted an outrage upon a white
lady living some distance from Melvin in
the country and was apprehended by
officers and lodged in jail at Butler. Last
night a crowd of determined white men
went quietly and took the brute from the
jail and hanged him to a tree. There was
unmistakable evidences of his guilt
