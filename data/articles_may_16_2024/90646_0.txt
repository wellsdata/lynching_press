Indianapolis, Ind., Nov. 11.- The
Scottsburg lynching case, which promises
to be a cause celebre in the criminal
annals of Indiana, was called for
trial today before Judge Baker, of the
United States court. This is the case
in which the father of Marion T. Tyler
brought suit against Sheriff James F.
Gobin of Scottsburg and his bondsmen
for not properly protecting his
from a mob that lynched young Tyler son.
