NORTH CAROLINA
COURT'S VERDICT
Sends Fifteen White Men to Prison
For Attempted Lynching
Winston- Salem, N. C.- Fifteen of the
sixteen men tried for breaking into
the city jail November 17th and attempting
to seize Russell High, colored
, who had attacked a white woman,
were found guilty today by a jury in
the Surry County Court, and sentenced
terms ranging from fourteen months
to to six years on the county roads.
