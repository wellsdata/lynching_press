Beaten Over the
Head With a Hatchet
DYERRBURG, Tenn, Feb. 18.- Miss Eliza Arnold, daughter of
prominent physician was beaten over the head with a hatchet in the
hands of a man who had broken into her room. Bloodhounds followed
the trail to the house of a negro named King where the hatchet was
found in a bnreau. King has been arrested and lynching is
ened. Miss Arnold will recover. threat-
