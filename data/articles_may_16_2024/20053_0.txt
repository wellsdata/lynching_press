The Leaders Arrested.
OMAJA, Neb., Oct. 10.- Seven members
of the moh which lynched the negro
rapist last night have been arrested,
charged with murder in the first degree.
County Attorney Mahoney refused to allow
the prisoners bail, and by 7 P. M. a
large crowd surrounded the county jail,
and threatened that if the prisoners were
not given their liberty the jail would be
attacked and the men liberated. The
men under arrest are Ed. Neuchaefer,
Patrick O'Herne, John Fritz, R. G.
Bloom, H. H. Bradies, Ed. Fitzgerald
and John O'Monoghue.
