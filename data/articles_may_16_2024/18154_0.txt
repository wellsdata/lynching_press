Troops Guard Alabama Jail.
Gadsden, Ala., July 17.- The Soldiers
are still guarding the jail to prevent
ynching of the five negroes who are
suspected of the murder of Mrs. S. J.
Smith, a white woman. The mob
which surrounded the jail during the
aight had not entirely dispersed Mon-
lay but had been driven back, and
those who remained stood off at some
listance in groups. No clash OCcurred
during the night and no trouble
expected at present. The authori-
ies regard that they have the situation
fully in hand.-
