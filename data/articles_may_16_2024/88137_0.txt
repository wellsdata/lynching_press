" This Is How We Pay For Lynching of
Shaw," Says Negro.
Pensacola, Fla., Aug. 15.- Mrs. Ed
Moclair, residing four miles from this
city, was assaulted by an unknown negro
.
" This is how we pay for lynching
Shaw," said the negro as he overpow.
ered the woman and choked her into
insensibility. When Mrs. Moclair re
covered enough to let the neighbors
know her plight the county officials
were notified and a sheriff's posse
started immediately upon the track of
the assailant.
In Pensacola a crowd has gathered
about the jail awaiting the return of
the officers. Meanwhile excitement
is at white heat, following so closely
the lynching of the negro, Shaw,
two upon weesk ago.
