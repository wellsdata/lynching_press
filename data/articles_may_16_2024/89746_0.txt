Probably Lynched.
EUFALA, Ala., Aug. 5.- At Harris this
morning, at 1 o'clock, a negro gained entrance
into the room of a young woman, it
is believed, with intent to rape. Before
achieving his purpose he was scared
away. He was arrested in a short time
and guarded in a store. When the train
passed the station the crowd had determined
to hang the man, and the latest
news is that the determination was carried
out. No telegraphic communication can
