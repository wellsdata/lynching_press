MORMON ELDERS ESCAPE.
Jackson, Ga., July 29.- The three
Mormon elders who were taken away
from the home of William Cunnard
Wednesday night by a mob escaped
from their captors and are now safe
near here. They are badly. bruised.
They swam down the creek, hotly pursued
by the mob, finally escaping to
the home of friends. It is not thought
the mob intended lynch them, but
only escort them out to of the country.
