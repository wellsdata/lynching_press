Acting on information presented by
Attorney General Moody, the Sepreme
Court has summoned Sheriff Shipp, his
deputy, and twenty- five alleged members
of the Tennessee mob which lynched
Ed Johnson, March 19, to appear and
show cause why they should not be
punished for contempt. The lynching
of Johnson occurred after he had been
sentenced to be hanged and after an
application for a writ of error had been
granted by the Supreme Court. The jail
officers are accused acting in collusion
with the mob. of
