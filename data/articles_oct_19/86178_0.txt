Mob Thins Out and Sheriff Does Not
Expect Further Trouble.
La Crosse, Wis., June 9.- A report
received from Sparta says that the
danger of Samuel Montgomery, alleged
wife murderer, being lynched,
has passed. The mob has thinned out
and is dispersing. Montgomery is well
guarded by a squad of deputies, and
Sheriff Cassels says that he does
anticipate any further trouble. not
