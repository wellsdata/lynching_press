A villain giving the name of George
Williams and hailing from Lexington, Ky.
enticed away two girls from Richmond,
O., one on Thursday, and the other Fri
day, last week, on s plea of hiring them
to work for him, ravished both, murdered
the first, and attempted to the second,
but she escaped, gave the alarm, and he
was arrested Friday night. Saturday
mob took him from and hung him to
the nearest free. jail
