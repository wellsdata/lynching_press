MOB LYNCHED A MINSTREL.
Said He Was a" Yankee Nigger and
Took No Impudence."
Shreveport, La. 17.- A mem
ber of a traveling colored minstrel
troupe who gave his name as
Homer Rogers, was hanged by a
mob in Morehouse parish last
night and his body riddled with
bullets.
So far as can be ascertained
when
resulted
the lynching
Rogers resented a remark made
by a white man, declaring he was
a Yankee nigger and did not
