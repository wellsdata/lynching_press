A WHITE MAN SOUGHT
BY MOB IN CAROLINA
Augusta, Aug. 28.- A special from
Aikin count by by telephone says Sher
iff Raybun has been notified that a
mob is being formed near Granite
ville, where Mrs. Bryant says she.
was criminaly assaulted by a white
man named Barton Saturday, with
the determination to take the man
from the Aiken county jail.
Sheriff has taken every pre
caution. Raybun
