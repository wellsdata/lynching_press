MURDER AND LYNCH LAW.- Ac respondent of
the North Carolica Christian Advocate, from Hyde,
writes, that on the 16th June, a negro belonging
to Rev. J. M. Watson, stabbed
Mr. Micajah P.
Thomas, so that he died immediately. On Mon
day night the boy was caught; and on Tuesday,
a number of the citizens assembled, chose twelve
men as a jury, who decided that the negro should
be hung. The sentence was carried into execution
forthworth; and the criminal was buried
