MOB LYNCHES NEGRO
Mississippi Farmers Use Rope and
Torch on Girl's Assailant,
Ellisville, Miss., June 27.- John
Hartfield, negro, confessed assallant
of a young white woman, was lynched
here by a mob.
Hartfield, who had been wounded by
farmers in effecting his capture, was
taken to the railroad trestle, where he
committed the crime to which he con
fessed and was hanged to a girder.
His body, was then burned.
