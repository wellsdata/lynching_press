WAR ON NEGROES.
A Mob Attempts a Lynching and Thes
Beats Negroes at Kansas City.
Kansas City, July 13.- A crowd of
2500 excited men and boys surrounded
the jail in this city last night clamoring
for vengeance against three negroes who
criminally assaulted Miss Grace Davis
Wednesday night in a populous and respectable
neighborhood after knocking
her escort. Vernie Newton, insensible.
Eight men, two of whom have been
identified, are held at the jail. Mise
Davis and Newton are both in a critical
1 condition.
