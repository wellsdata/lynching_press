ONE LYNCHED IN JASPER
COUNTY, GA.
Macon, Ga., Oct. 7- Eugene
Hamilton, a Negro, under ten
years' penitentiary sentence for an
attempt on the life of Charles Tin-
gle. Jasper County farmer, was
taken from Sheriff Middlebrooks
of Jones County near here early to-
day and shot to death at daybreak
near Monticello, in
County. Jasper
