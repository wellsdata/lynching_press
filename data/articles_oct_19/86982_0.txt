Wholesale Hanging,
MANSFIELD, La .. Nov. 27.- Gus
Simmonds and Frank Garrett were hung
here today for the murder of an unknown
white man near Gioster last spring. Both
were laughing and talking cheerfully up
to the time the caps were placed on them.
