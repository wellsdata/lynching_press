FOR ROBBERY AND MURDER
Victim and Mob as Well as
Culprits of Same
Race.
Cairo, Ill., Sept. 12.- News reached
here to the effect that last night a
moh broke into the jail at Wickliffe,
Ky., across the river from this city,
and lynched three negroes accused of
robbing and murdering the old negro,
Thomas, last Saturday. The
murderers Wash confessed the crime.
