NEGRO LYNCHED IN VIRGINIA
For Killing Harry Draper, An Unknown
Man is Sent Into Eternity
Without Trial.
Bramwell, Va., Feb. 1.- An unknown
negro, who was refused the privilege of
walking through a tunnel on the Norfolk
& Western railway near here last
night, shot Harry Draper, the watch-
man, killing him instantly. A posse
captured the negro, and at midnight 50
men took the prisoner from jail
