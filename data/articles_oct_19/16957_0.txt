MOB VIOLENCE- MAN HUNG.- A dis
patch frout Baltimore of the 23d ult.,
says that in Culpepper County, Va., a
mob of 300 persons including justices of
the peace and others, resisted the Sheriff
, and took from jail Wm. Grayson and
hung him till he was dead. The cause
assigned, is that Grayson had twice
been convicted of the murder of David
W. Miller, a white man, but had been
granted a new trial on the ground that
the testimony hardly justified his guilt.
Great excitement prevailed and the papers
entreat the Government arrest
