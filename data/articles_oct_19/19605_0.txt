Shooting in a Sunday School.
SEDAN, Kan., Sept. 29.- At Sunday- school
last Sunday in this place, Simon Smith, co
ored, shot and wounded William Andrews,
colored, against whom he had an old grudge.
He shot at him again, but the bullet struck
and killed an old negro named Ben Williams.
A mob of colored people tried to lynch
murderer, but he was safely lodged in jail the.
