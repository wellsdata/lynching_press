A Murderer Lynched.
NASHVILLE, April 28.- An American special
from Florence, Ala., says an immense
crowd there to- day overpowered the jailer
and hung George Ware, a prisoner, who murdered
a boy named Robert Bethune, at Muscle
Shoals, last week. Before hanging, Ware
confessed that he murdered the boy for$ 5
and plug of tobacco, and threw the body in
the river a.
