REQUEST CAME TOO LATE.
Governor Could Not Get Troops to
Hempstead in Time.
Austin, Tex., Oct. 21.- This afternoon
Gov. Sayers received a telephone mes
sage from the sheriff at Hempstead
asking for state troops post haste as a
mob was trying to get the prisoners
away from him.
At 4:15 o'clock this was followed by
a telegram from the district asking for
troops by special train. Orders were
issued for Houston and Bryan companies
to respond, but before they
could be assembled the mob had lynch
ed the negroes and the order for
was countermanded. troops
