Aiken, S. C., Feb. 19.- Will Burts, a
negro, was lynched at Burr Basket Mills,
eight miles north of Aiken, by a mob of
250 men. Last week Burts attempted to
assault Mrs. C. L. Weeks, a planter's wife.
When Burts was strung up the rope broke.
The rope was tied and Burts again swung
up, the crowd emptying their guns into
his body. Burts confessed to
