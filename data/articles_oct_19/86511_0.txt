One Negro Lyncher Indicted.
NEWARK, Ohio, Dec. 19. Mon
tello Wathla, a negro, has been indicted
by the grand jury on a
charge of manslaughter, in connection
with the lynching last July of
Carl Etherington, a detective employed
by the anti- saloon league.
Etherington was lynched after he
had shot and killed the former chief
of police of this city, whose
saloon the he was raiding.
