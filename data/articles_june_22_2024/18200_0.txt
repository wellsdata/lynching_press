CHICAGO, Aug. 10.- A shocking
child murder is reported to have occurred
at Naples, Illinois; a little
nephew of a man named Hale, was
found buried in the yard of his uncle's
house, the body bearing evidence
of its having been whipped to death.
Hale has disappeared.
The murderer of young Mrs. Mat-
thews, near Yates City, Illinois,
has been discovered. John S. Os-
borne, a young married man, was
arrested but waiyed a hearing and
was taken to jail. The proofs of his
guilt are so strong that the sheriff had
great difficulty in rescuing him from
a mob which had gathered to
