Negro Woman Escaped Lynching.
ST. LOUIS, Aug. 11.- A dispatch
from Clarendon, Ark., says that Susie
Jacobs, the negro woman escaped and
was not hanged by the mob. Mrs.
Orr was in the jail when the mob entered
. She had attempted to poison
herself, and was left alone. She was
alive the morning. If she lives she
will probably in be lynched.
