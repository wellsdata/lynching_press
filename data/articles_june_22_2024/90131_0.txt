A TEXAS MURDER.
Victim a Woman Homesteader, Slayer
A Mexican Youth.
El Paso, July 26.- News reached
here tonight of the murder of Miss
Emma Brown, formerly of Austin,
Texas, who had taken up a homestead
in Reeves county Texas. A
sixteen year old Mexican boy named
Martinez is under arrest and is said
to have confessed to the murder.
He was spirited out of town to
Midland when it became known that
a mob was forming to lynch him. It
is reported that the Mexican citizens
of Reeves county had planned to get
possession Martinez and burn him
at the stake of.
