ASKS FOR TROOPS.
Sheriff Smith Wants Protection i
A Rapist in Florida.
Pensacola, Fla., August 27.- Sheriff
Smith has wired to Governor Jennings
asking for a detail of the state troops
to protect the negro Thompson, who assaulted
aged Mrs. Dixon on Sunday.
A mob formed last night and at 3
o'clock this morning sent a message to
Sheriff Smith that if Thompson was not
tried today he would be lynched.
Judge Reeves is out of the state and
it is impossible to have the trial now.
The mob is thoroughly organized and
it is feared will attack the jail tonight.
Sheriff Smith has several special deputies
, thoroughly armed, on guard at the
