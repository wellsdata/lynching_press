A Negro Pastor Lynched by His Flock.
ATLANTA, Ga., Oct. 13.-Dr. Alfred Will-
iams( colored) is the pastor of the Antioch
colored church in Edgewood. Some time age
he formed a co- operative store among his
parishioners, with himself as storekeeper.
Dissensions soon arose. The store was put
into the hands of two receivers. Thursday
morning the preacher was found in the woods
with over a hundred buckshot wounds in his
body made by members of his congregation.
He will, die.
