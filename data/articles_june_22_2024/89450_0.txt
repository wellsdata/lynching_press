NEGRO LYNCHED AT EUFAULA
Eufaula, Okla., August 6. Armed
with shotguns and revolvers, a mob
of masked men of McIntosh county
early Saturday morning entered the
county jail, forced Jailor W. D. Hogan
to turn over the keys, and taking
ing Crockett Williams ,, a negro,
from a cell, led him to the of
Eufaula and hanged him to edge a tree.
