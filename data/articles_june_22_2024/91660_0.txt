A Negro Murderer Lynched by an Angry
Mob.
PETERSBURG, Va., Feb. 24. -Some
days ago Hugh Hammock, a prominent
farmer residing in Belleville, Ottawa
county, was assailed and robbed while
passing along the highway by a negro
named Scott Bishop. Bishop was arrested
Saturday night. Hammock died
Friday from the effects of the assault.
This morning at 4 o'clock a mob took
Bishop from the officers and hanged him
to a tree near town. The negro begged
piteously for his life, but entreaties
