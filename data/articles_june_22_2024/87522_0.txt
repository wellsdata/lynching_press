SALT LAKE CITY, Dec. 28.- Lawrence
Leberg. the man who was lynched at Las
Animas, Colo., last night, was taken into
custody here in June last, was adjudged
insane and was committed to the state
hospital at Provo, where he was detained
until August 18. That the mob lynched
a lunatic is the opinion of Dr. Calder, superintendent
of the Utah asylum: Leberg
drank to excess and while under the influence
of liquor had a mania to set fire to
anything within his reach. The man was
known here as Lars Leberg. He is said
