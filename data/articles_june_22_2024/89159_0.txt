Negro Hanged by Mob.
Brokenshire, Tex., September 13.- Dave
Newton, a negro, charged with complicity
in the murder of John Buchtrin, a white
man, who was shot and killed at his home
yesterday, was taken from jail late last
night by a mob and hanged. The father
and brother of the negro lynched are under
arrest in connection with the killing
and have been placed in the jail at Hemp-
stead to prevent further violence. Intense
excitement prevails here and all idle ne-
groes have received instructions to
the community. leave
