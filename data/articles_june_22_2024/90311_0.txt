ANOTHER BRUTE LYNCHED.
A South Carolina Mob Takes Summary
Vengeance on a Negro Ravisher.
BLACKVILLE, S. C., March 2.- Mel-
ville Kennedy, colored, was lynched by
a mob of 30 men at Windsor, near here.
Eight months ago Kennedy and an-
other negro named Wade forcibly carried
Miss Sallie Webb to the home of a
colored woman and assaulted her.
They forced her to keep the matter
quiet by threats of death, and the
young woman kept the secret until
yesterday. The mob is now scouring
the country for Wade, and he will
lynched if caught. be
