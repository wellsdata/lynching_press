SOMERSET, Ky., Sept. 15- About 1 o'clock this
morning a crowd went to the jail where the
Gilliland brothers were confined on the charge
the murder of Sheriff McCargoe, and overpowered
the guard. The Gilliland brothers
were then taken to a ravine a short distance
of the city and hanged. No confession
could be procured. The boys declared to the
that they were innocent.
