WE ARE ALL SICK of hearing so
much talk about segregation- discrimination
- disfranchisement- and
lynch law. Negro editors continu-
ally write about it- Negro
ers always preaching about it- Negro
orators deliver long orations
about it- everybody is weary.
weary. But sick and weary though
we be segregation, discrimination
and lynch law is keeping us crowded
in the worst districts- holding
us out of decent jobs, keeping our
living standards low and crushing
the spirit of our youth. What are
to do about it?
we ***
