DIED FACE TO FACE.
Lynching of Two Tennessee Negroes
Last Night.
Newbern, Tenn., October 8.- Garfield
Burley and Curtis Brown, negroes, were
lynched here tonight by a mob of 500
people. Burley on Saturday last shot
and killed D. Flatt, a young farmer,
near Dyersburg. Burley confessed to
the killing, implicating Curtis Brown as
an accomplice. Both men were lodged
in jail at Dyersburg today. A mob took
the two men from jail tonight, strung
them up on a telephone pole and securely
tied them face to face
