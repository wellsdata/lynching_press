Negro Lynched
Manchester, Ga .. October 19.- Jerry
Lovelace, a negro brakeman, charged
with assaulting Yardmaster W. F. Ker-
nan on Tuesday night, was taken from
Marshal Collier here early today by a
mob of about 30 men and lynched. The
mob first secured Collier, taking his keys
and gun from him, after which they went
to the jail for the negro. After the lynching
the mob went back to the jail, re-
the marshal and
turned the keys
dispersed. Not a to shot was fired.
