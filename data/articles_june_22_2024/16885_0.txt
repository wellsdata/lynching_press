NEGRO LYNCHED.
Danville, Va., July 7.- Red Roach,
negro, arrested late yesterday afternoon
attempt to attack the 13
year alleged old daughter of a farmer, was
taken from the Person county jail
last night by a mob and hanged near
the town of Roxboro. A number of
shots were fired into his body. According
to sheriff Robert Thompson
of Roxboro, the negro was identified
by the girl after his arrest
Roxboro was quiet today and
was no indication of race feeling there.
