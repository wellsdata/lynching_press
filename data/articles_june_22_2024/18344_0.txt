DOUBLE MURDERER LYNCHED.
Florida Mob Hanged a Negro Who
Killed White Men.
Valdosta, Ga., March 11.- Word was
ceived here to- night of a double murder
and lynching near Jennings, Fla. Two
white men named Carver were killed by a
negro whose name could not be learned.
