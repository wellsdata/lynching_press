A Deputy Sheriff Killed by Lynchers.
SPRINGFIELD, Mo., March 15.- News
has just reached this city of the lynching
in Forsythe, Taney county, of John
Wesley Bright, who was charged with
murdering his wife. Bright was being
examined when the mob demanded his
surrender. Deputy Sheriff Williams refused
to give him up, and was shot dead
The mob hanged Bright and escaped,.
