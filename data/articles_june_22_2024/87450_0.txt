" DAKOTA SLIM."
Ruffian Had a Preliminary Examination
Yesterday.
Colfax, Wash., Feb. 15.- Robert Mc
Donald, otherwise known as" Dakota
Slim," was brought before Justice Kirk
land this morning for preliminary examination
for the murder of Orville Yaden in
Farmington, Oct. 22, 1897. McDonald is
the man whom a mob attempted to lynch
last month. The principal witness was
Daniel Couch, who positively identified
McDonald as the man who held him up
and robbed him and afterwards held
young Hayden when the latter was killed up.
