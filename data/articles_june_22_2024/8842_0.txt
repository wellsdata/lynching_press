assaulted the daughter of Police.
man Vance, of Paris, Texas, and
afterwards murdered her by tearing
her body in twain, was captured
near Hope, Ark., and taken to
the scene of his terrible crime. Ten
thousand people gathered to witness
his execution by Judge Lynch.

tared by red hot irons; then oil was
poured over his body and the torch
