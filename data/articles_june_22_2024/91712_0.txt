Anti- Chinese Mob in New Mexico.
NEW YORK, May 16th.- The Telegram has
the following special from Loredo, Tex .: Information
has just been received here that a
railroad mob has taken forcible possession of
the railroad at Crittenden, N. M., the cause
of the riot being the presence of a number of
Chinese laborers. They drove all the China-
Jewsen from the camp, and then seizing Scott,
the Superintendent of construction, hung him
to telegraph pole until he promised to hire
no a more Chinamen.
