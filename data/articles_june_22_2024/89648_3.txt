Burned to Death.
Al Batson, pressman at the Ar-
kansas Cotton Oil Company's mill
in Helena, met a horrible death. With
another man Batson kept bach in
a small house near his work. During
the night the house caught fire
and Batson, who was alone, his partner
being away for the night, was
burned to death.
Andy Miears, one of the oldest
citizens of Little Rock, died on the
4th inst., aged 63 years.
Sid Moore, colored, was killed by
officers at Arkansas City while resisting
arrest. The negro attempted
shoot two officers with a Winches-
to ter, but they were too quick for him.
