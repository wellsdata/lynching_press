A Negro Boy Lynched.
NASHVILLE, Tenn., June 30.- Late at
night a mob took Tom Lilliard, a negro
boy 15 years old, from the jail at Wood-
bury, marched him to the edge of town
and hanged him to the bridge over
Stones river. The boy was charged
with criminal assault on Miss Mc
Knight, an inmate of the poor house,
and of not very mind. He confessed
his guilt. sound
