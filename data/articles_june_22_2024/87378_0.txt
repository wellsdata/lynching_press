Chicago. Oct. 19.- Mayor S. S. King
of Rome, Ga., wires the Associated
Press tonight that the report handled
last night that two negroes were lynched
for arson near Summerville, Ga., was
an error. The negroes were taken from
officers last Friday night, and an at
tempt made to extort a confession, but
they were later and are now at
their homes. released
