A Murderer Lynched.
HOPKINSVILLE, Ky., November 24.-
Information has been received to the
effect that Joseph A. Smith, the man
who killed W. T. Williams, the Town
Marshal of Trenton, two weeks ago, was
taken from jail at Elkton, the county
seat of Todd county, last night, by a
mob, and hanged to a tree in the
house yard. court-
