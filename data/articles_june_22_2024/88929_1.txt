One Negro Only Slightly Wounded and
Escapes Enraged Citizens.
Atlanta, Ga., June 29.- Specials say
that seven negroes and one white man
were lynched near Watkinsville, Ga.,
last night. A mob, supposed to be
composed of citizens of Watkinsville,
and vicinity, formed about midnight,
marched to the jail, and compelled the
jailor to deliver the keys. The mob
forced the jailer to accompany it with
the victims to the scene of the lynching
. Four of the prisoners were reported
to be implicated in the murder
of and Mrs. Holbrook, several
weeks Mr. ago.
