A Murderer Lynched.
NEW ORLEANS, May 16th.- Monday last,
near Breaux Bridge, St. Martin parish, Jos.
Jenkins shot and killed his brother- in- law,
Rayshel Castille. He claimed that it was a
case of mistaken identity. Castille was
wealthy and respected. Eventually the citizens
became much incensed, and yesterday
collected two hundred strong, with masks,
rode to the village where Jenkins was con-
, and, taking him from the officers,
fined hanged him where Castille fell.
