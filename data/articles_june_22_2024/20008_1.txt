Sentenced.
Lynchers
WHITEHALL, Wis .. March 14.- At ten
o'clock this morning Charles Johnson,
Ole Sletto, Ole Jansen and Bertha Olsen
were sentenced to life imprisonment
and three days' solitary confinement
each year for the lynching of old man
Hans Jacob Olsen last fall. The rest of
the lynchers let off with fines,
which aggregate were$ 3,000.
