Negro Lynched.
Mobile, Ala., Aug. 18.- Rufus Les
seur, a negro, was shot to death out
side the calaboose of Thomaston, Ma
rengo county. He had been loafing
around the place several days and had
committed two robberies. The second
time he entered the home of Mrs. J.
P. Hollis, she was awakened, and the
negro was frightened away. He dropped
his hat, which led to his arrest.
