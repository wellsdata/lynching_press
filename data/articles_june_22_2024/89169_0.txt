Reports from Rockwood state that Joe
Blanton, charged with being the leader
of the mob which lynched George Cook
at Kingston jail last winter, had been
bound over to the grand jury and committed
to jail without bail at a prelim-
inary hearing. Blanton was arrested at
Memphis last Sunday, the police having
identified him by a scar on his head,
made by a knife in the hands Cook
on the night of the lynching. of
