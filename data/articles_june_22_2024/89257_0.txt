Walter Diehl on Trial for Participating
In Hanging of Etherington.
Newark, O., Oct. 25.- The case of
Walter Diehl, charged with first degree
murder in connection with the
lynching of Carl Etherington, of Wil-
lisburg, Ky., in July of last year,
go to the jury today. will
The state claims to have established
the fact that Diehl was not only implicated
in the hanging of the Ken-
tuckian but that he actually struck the
latter over the head with a hammer
just preceeding the lynching. Nine
other men are awaiting trial on
charges first degree murder growing
out of the of same incident.
