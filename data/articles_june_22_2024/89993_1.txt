An Attempted Lynching Frustrated by the
Sheriff.
CINCINNATI, Jan. 24.- A dispatch to the
Commercial Gazette from Shoals, Martin
County, Indiana, says that an attempt was
made about midnight last night to lynch
Thomas Archer and Martin Archer, as the
prisoners were being taken to Washington
for safety, but it was frustrated by the
prompt action of Sheriff Padgett. John
Archer and John Lynch, who has been indicted
for murder with the first named, were
brought here to- day, and threats lynching
are made. of
