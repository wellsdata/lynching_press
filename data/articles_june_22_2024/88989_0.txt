MOB LYNCHES WHITE MAN.
Condemned Slayer of Louisiana Town
Marshal Had Appealed.
Rayville, La.- Aroused by the fact
that an appeal to the Supreme Court
had been taken following his condemnation
to death here on the
charge of having murdered Town
Marshal C. C. Chenault, a mob battered
down the doors of the parish
jail at Rayville and, dragging J. D.
Freeman, a white man, from his cell,
hanged him on the same spot
