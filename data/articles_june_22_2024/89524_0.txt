Rugg Confesses.
HUNTER'S POINT, L. I., Jan. 28.- Charles A.
Rugg, confined in jail, charged with an assault
on Selah Sprague on Friday morning, has confessed
that he committed the crime. The preliminary
examination takes place at Hicksville,
on Tuesday. A large number of deputy sheriffs
and constables have been detailed to prevent
the lynching of Rugg
Ex- Governor.
