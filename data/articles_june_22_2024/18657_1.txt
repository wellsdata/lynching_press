BAY MINETTE, Ala.- For the
first time in the history of Alabama
convictions and pleas of guilty were
entered in a lynching case today
when in the Baldwin County Court
two men were convicted of complicity
in the murder and given Penitentiary
sentences, while 28 other members of
the same band entered pleas of
guilty. Ten of those pleading guilty
were given fines or sentences to
work. hard
