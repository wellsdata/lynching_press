A Kentucky Lad Disemboweled.
OWENSBURG, Ky., Dec. 19.- An outrage was
perpetrated here last night on Will Girvin, an
eighteen- year- old boy, by two tramps. The boy
was seized in the railroad yards and a coat thrown
over his head. He was carried into a car and
chloroformed. The villians used a knife, cutting
Into the bowels. He was found by his brother.
If caught, the villains will be lynched. One
