Negro Murderer Lynched.
NEW MADRID, Mo., Dec. 1.- Another
lynching occurred here yesterday, the victim
being the second of the two negroes
who killed Alex. Loin, in Madrid Bend, Ky.,
last Friday. The negro was caught at Dy-
ersburg, Tenn. The friends and relatives of
the murdered man heard of the capture, a
mob was organized, the jail stormed and
the prisoner taken out and lynched near the
scene of the crime with which he was
charged. The unfortunate black man made
a confession, saying that he killed Loin in
order to get$ which he understood he
had on his person 100.
