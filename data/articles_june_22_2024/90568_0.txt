NEGRO RAPIST LYNCHED
Man Who Defends Him Is Shot to
Death.
Lake Charles, La., Feb. 21.- Thomas
Vital, the negro who criminally assaulted
the thirteen- year- old daughter
of Estieve Miller, was taken from his
home near Fenton last night by a
mob and lynched. Samuel Maddox
who attempted to defend Vital, was
shot to death. Vital had been previously
shot in leg by the girl's
father and was not the yet under arrest.
