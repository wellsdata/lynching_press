ALVA, O. T., Sept. 29.- Two surveyors ..
John Allen and his son, yesterday ordered
a cowboy settler off a claim which they
said belonged to a Bobemian, who accompanied
them. The cowboy shot and killed
both Allens, arrested the Bohemian and
took possession of the claim. Last
