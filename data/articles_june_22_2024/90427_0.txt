LYNCHED. MURDERER
OGDEN, Utah, Dec. 13.- Negro refugees
from Green river, Wyo., arriving
here today declared that all colored
men, women and children had
been ordered to leave the that town
following the lynching of Edward
Woodson, a negro charged with killing
a railroad switchman and wounding
another. Nearly all left their possessions
in Green river having been
given only until last night, to leave.
