Here a scene occurred which bafes description.
The alarm having become general that the murderer
was captured, the whole force in pursuit came
hurrying up yelling and cheering like so many
crazy men; each one, too, wanted to inflict a blow
on the monster. The cry was," Hang him up to
the tree,"" Despatch the villain,"" Let's look at
him,"" Let's see what he looks like,"" Take his
head off," said another," Burn the son of"
exclaimed another,& c. Officer Dowling protested
against any such steps being taken, and was actually
compelled to stand guard over the accused with
a loaded revolver, to prevent the excited
from carrying the lynch law into effect. people
