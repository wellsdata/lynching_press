Lynched the Wrong Negro.
BRYAN, Tex., Nov. 22.- Rev. J. E.
Horne of Madison county, Tex., brought
news of the lynching of a negro in a remote
part of that county last Tuesday
night. He was accused of riding over a
little white girl in the road, inflioting serious
injuries on her. Later developments
go to show that the mob the wrong
negro, and the guilty one made got his escape.
