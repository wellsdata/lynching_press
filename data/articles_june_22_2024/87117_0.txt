City Marshal Killed.
LEXINGTON, July 10.- Joseph Beard, the City Marshal
of Lexington, Ky., in attempting to arrest a
man named Barker, who was engaged in a fight, was
killed by Barker. So great an exeitement did this
cause among the citizens of Lexington that they immediately
arrested Barker, and hung him, according
to Lynch law
. Domestic Markets.
