BILBO IGNORED NEGROES
Two weeks ago Governor Bilbo received
two or three telegrams from an association
of New York negroes demanding
to know what he was going to do about
the lynching of four negroes at Shubuta.
The governor finally decided he would
just ignore the telegrams, but he said
he felt like replying as Mr. Hoover did
to the Germans who demanded assistance
.-
