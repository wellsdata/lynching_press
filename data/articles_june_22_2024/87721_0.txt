BALTIMORE, Aug. 13.
The Prometheus arrived at New York
on Wednesday with San Juan dates to the
4th inst., and San Francisco to the 15th
July. She brings 300 passengers, and
$ 200,000 in gold on freight, and$ 400,000
in the hands of passengers, who express
great satisfaction at the Nicaragua route.
At San Francisco a desperado named
James Stuart had been hung by Lynch law
on the 10th July, before five thousand people
. The citizens in other towns were
adopting measures to themselves,
the laws being inadequate protect for that purpose.
