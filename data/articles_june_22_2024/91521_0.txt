NEGROES CONDONE
LYNCHING OF ONE
OF THEIR BLOOD
Athens, Ga., Sept. 23- Negroes at
a mass meeting near Lexington, Ga.,
have endorsed the action of whites
in lynching Obe Cox, negro, accused
of attacking and murdering the wife
of a white farmer.
Resolutions adopted at the meeting
condemned in strong language the
crime which aroused one thousand
men and sent them on a 24 hour
search of swamps concluding with the
shooting to death of the negro and
burning of the body. At the time of
the crime the negro was under indictment
