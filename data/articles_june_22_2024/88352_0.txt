Louisiana Mob Lynched White Man.
Monroe, La., May 30.- A mob en
gaged a special train here Monday
night and went to Tallulah, La., where
they forced the jail and hanged R. L.
Rogers, a white man, the alleged murderer
of Jesse Brown, a prominent
merchant at Girard, La. Brown was
killed February 19, 1904. Rogers was
tried and found guilty. He got a
change of venue and a mistrial was
ordered. He was discharged on a
of former jeopardy. plea
