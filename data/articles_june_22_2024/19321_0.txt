A Negro Assaulter Lynched.
ABERDEEN, Aug. 13.- Monday night a
negro named Bowen, employed on a farm
nine miles from this place, entered the
room occupied by Mrs. Moore and her
daughter and a niece of Bowen and at-
tempted to assault the niece, but her
screams awoke the other occupants of the
room and he fled. Before noon he was ar-
rested and handed over to the justice of the
peace. The entire neighborhood congregated
and took Bowen from the custody of
the officers and strung him up by the neck
on the public road near where the
was made. assault
