UNKNOWN NEGRO LYNCHED.
He Was Charged With Shooting and
Fatally Wounding Mrs. Craft in
South Carolina.
Columbia, S. C., Nov. 25.- Late Sun
day night word was received of the
lynching in Anderson county Sunday
of an unknown Negro.
Saturday afternoon the man went
to the house of Mrs. Perry Craft and
asked for something to eat. When
she turned to get him a plate of food
the Negro shot her through the back
inflicting a fatal wound. The man
fled, but was pursued by hundreds of
men with bloodhounds and captured.
There are no details of the manner
his death. of
