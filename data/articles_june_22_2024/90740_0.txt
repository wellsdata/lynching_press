A Wife- Murderer in Danger of Lynching.
BLOOMINGTON, ILL., March 25.- The
Pantagraph's Petersburg, Ill., special
as: At 11 a. m. the State Militia on
duty around the jail to prevent the lynching
of the wife- murderer, Houlden, has
been relieved and the jail is without a
guard. The Sheriff anticipates trouble if
the weather is not stormy to- night. He
will be on the alert. Public feeling
Houlden is running very high. against
