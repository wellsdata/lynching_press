Dying Man Accuses Black
TERRE HAUTE, Ind., July 25.-
George M. Lintz, dying in a hospital
from a bullet fired last night by a
burglar, has identified Albert McGowan,
a negro, as the man who shot him.
After the arrest and identification Mc-
Gowan was taken to jail and there is a
fear that an attempt may be made to
lynch him Men are gathering and the
sheriff is. preparing to defend the jail.
