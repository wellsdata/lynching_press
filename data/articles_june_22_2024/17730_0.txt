A NEGRO LYNCHED.
A Mob Shoots and Burns Bill Hillard for
Assaulting a Girl- Traced to
His Home.,
Troy, Ala., Sept. 4.- Bill Fourney,
alias Bill Hilliard, a negro, charged
with assaulting Miss Wilson at Chest
nut Grove, was shot and his body
burned by a mob near the scene of his
crime yesterday. The posse tracked
the negro to his home, where he was
found in bed. Miss Wilson is in precarious
condition. a
