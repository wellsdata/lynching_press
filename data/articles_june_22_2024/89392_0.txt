Struck With Gun
Pine Bluff, Ark., December 25.- Roy
Porter, negro, was brought to Pine
Bluff tonight to prevent anticipated
mob violence at the village of Youngs-
taown, where he was arrested this afternoon
charged with the wounding,
probably fatally, of Hal Conrod. Porter,
it is declared, attempted to force an
entrance into the Conrod home in search
of a negro servant and struck Conrod
over the head with a shotgun. He was
badly stabbed about the face and.
