Mob Seizes and Hangs Negro Suspect.
Vicksburg, Miss. Louis Andrew,
negro, was taken from a train at Tal-
lulah, La., and hanged by a mob. according
to a dispatch received here.
Andrew was accused of complicity in
the murder of Key Oakes, a young
planter, several weeks ago. He was
captured at Rayville, and was being
brought here for trial. When the
train arrived the mob seized the
and hanged him to a telegraph negro pole.
