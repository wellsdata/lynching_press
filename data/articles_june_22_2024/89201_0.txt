Mr. Kunkle is the step- father of the
notorious LaSalle boys, one of whom
a Polk City mob threatened to lynch
when he was captured a month ago.
This one, Fred LaSalle, is now in jail,
having been bound over to the grand
jury on three charges of stealing from
Polk City neighbors. His brother,
Wm. LaSalle, is in jail on a five- year
, imposed by Judge Howe at
sentence the last term of court.
