NEGRO MINSTREL LYNCHED.
Sequel to a Row at a Minstrel Showing
New Madrid, Mo.,
Saturday Night.
New Madrid, Mo., Feb. 18.- Louis
Wright, a negro minstrel belonging
to the Richard& Pringle troupe,
which showed here Saturday night
was taken from jail, Sunday night,
by a crowd of masked
men and
hanged. This was the sequel to a
row at the show, Saturday night during
which several shots were
and a number of person wounded fired.
