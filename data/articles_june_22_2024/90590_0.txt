Says Man Killed
Train Conductor
Lincoln, Neb., Sept. 30.- Leon Darling
, the Negro accused of killing Conductor
Massey of the Union Pacific,
was sought by a posses of lynchers,
but Warden Fenton of the state penitentiary
foiled them at the point of
his Winchester. Darling denied the
murder and repudiates an alleged confession
. Feeling in Grand Island is
high, the authorities are determined
but to perform their duty.
