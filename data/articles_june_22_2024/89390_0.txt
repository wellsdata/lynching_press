NEGRO LYNCHED
FOR ASSAULT
Meridian, Miss., November 7 .-" Judge"
Moseley, a negro, was taken from deputy
sheriff Brown near Lockhart this morning
by a mob or about 400 persons and lynched
. The negro's body was riddled with
bullets. Moseley assaulted J. H. Cov
ington, white, with a stick at
yesterday. Lockhart
