GETS AFTER LYNCHERS.
GUTHRIE, Okla., July 18.- For the
first time in history the Oklahoma government
will prosecute lynchers.
United States Marshal John A. Bern-
athy and two assistant United States
attorneys are in Osage tonight to secure
evidence against members of the
mob that Tuesday night hanged the
negro, Frank Bailey, for assaulting the
brakeman, Frank Kelly. Charges of
murder will be preferred against the
men and boys. Osage is in the Indian
reservation and the authorities
have no jurisdiction territorial.
