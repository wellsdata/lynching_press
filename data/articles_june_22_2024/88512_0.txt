Gov. Johnston Makes Statement
Governor Johnston, who is at his home in
this city, stated last night that he would
exert every effort to punish the men who
were in the mob that lynched Clark at
Huntsville yesterday. He did all in his
power to prevent the lynching of the negro
and had directed that court should be convened
at once to try him. Had the negro
not been lynched he would, in all
