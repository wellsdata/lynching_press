HANG NEGRO
PREACHER
( By United Press Leased Wire)
JACKSON, Ga., Dec, 15- Hanged
in a theatre before an audience of
his relatives and others, the Rev.
Wm. Turner, a negro preacher, is
dead here today. He was executed
for having incited a race riot in
which Jesse Singley, a prominent
planter, was killed.
The sheriff erected the gallows on
the stage, and the negro's confession
was read from there while a
mob outside clamored for a
