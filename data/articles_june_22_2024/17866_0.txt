the leadership of Elder W. G. Ellis, of
Salt Lake City, Utah, are at work in
the vicinity of Beaver Dam Station,
Hanover County, Va.

desperado, was lynched in Lexington
County, S. C., on the 17th. He tried to
cremate his wife and two children.
Whites and blacks captured him, and
