Negro Lynched in Alabama.
BIRMINGHAM, Ala., June 6.- Near
Strasburg, the body of Jim Powell, a
Negro, was found hanging to a limb.
Monday night Powell entered the residence
of John Bussy, a well known
farmer near there, and assaulted a
young daughter of the farmer. Pursuit
was given the Negro by a posse,
and he was captured near Calera,
teen away, and was carried before
the miles girl, who identified him.
