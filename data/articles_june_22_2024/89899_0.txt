TEXANS INTRODUCE
NEW STYLE OF LYNCHING
( By Associated Negro Press)
Woodville, Tex., June 3.- Members
of the mob that whiped a Negro to
death between here and Kountze last
week have conveyed the news to the
local authorities that they did not intend
to kill the man and were very
sorry that he died from the whipping.
He is alleged to have threatened the
life of a white.
