Lynch a Murderer.
Casper, Wyo., March 29.- Charles
Francis Woodward, the condemned
murderer of Sheriff W. C. Ricker, of
this county, was hanged in his night
clothes at one o'clock Friday morning
by 24 masked and armed men on the
scaffold erected for his legal execution
. He was to have been hanged
Friday but had been granted a stay
of execution, by the supreme court.
