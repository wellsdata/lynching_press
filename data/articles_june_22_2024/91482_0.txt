Negro Nearly Lynched.
OTSEGO, Mich., Aug. 20.- Sam Sheeler,
a negro from Fort Wayne, narrowly escaped
being lynched here. He robbed a woman
and tried to assault her, but was captured
and jailed. Later a crowd entered
the jail and dragged the negro out with and
rope around his neck. He confessed his
crimes and told where his
could be found, and his life was confederates spared.
