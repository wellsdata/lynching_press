Attempt at Lynch- Law in Indiana.
Louisville, Ky., November 4.- Last
night, a mob collected about the jail in
Jeffersonville, Indiana, with the intention
of lynching a negro, named John Washing-
ton, who had attempted an outrage on a
little white girl, whose father died from excitement
produced by the outrage.
The prisoner was slipped out by the
back way and conveyed to the State prison;
learning which, the mob dispersed.
