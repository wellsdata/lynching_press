Negro Murderer Lynched.
Greenwood, Miss., Oct. 21.- Yesterday a
white man named J. H. Rook was shot
and killed by a negro on the place of
Rook, near Sunnyside, Leflore county. Mr.
Rook, as far as is now known, was reprimanding
a negro woman at her home when
this negro interfered and the shooting
commenced, the negro in the house and
Mr. Rook on the outside. Rook was shot
down and killed. night a posse took
the negro and hung Last him.
