A Negro Rape Fiend Lynched.
MACON, Ga., Oct. 18.- William Singleton
, a negro hackman, was taken from
jail last night by an armed mob and
hanged to a tree. Five weeks ago Singleton
committed rape upon a young
lady whose name is suppressed because
of her social prominence. She is a
daughter of the late Chief Justice
grand Lumpkin, of this state.
