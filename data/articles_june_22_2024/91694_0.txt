A Murderer Lynched- Challenge to Ride.
DENVER( Col.), July 23d.- Charles Norton
shot policeman O'Neil at Kokoma this afternoon
. Norton was intoxicated and raising a
disturbance in a saloon. The policeman attempted
to arrest him, and was shot dead.
Norton was taken to jail, and several Deputy
Sheriffs placed on guard. A crowd soon
gathered, overpowered the guard and lynched
the prisoner. He confessed to having committed
other murders, and said he ought to
have been hanged long ago.
Miss Minnie Pienoto of Gally has challenged
the winner of the twenty- mile ride at
Minneapolis to ride ten, fifteen or twenty
in Denver, St. Louis, Kansas City,
miles Minneapolis or Chicago, for$ 200 a side.
