NEGRO BOY LYNCHED.
Union Springs, Ala., April 2- While
state militiamen were mustering in at
Montgomery to hurry here to protect
a negro prisoner this afternoon, a mob
that had taken him from the jail carried
him to the outskirts of the town
and lynched him. The victim was a
boy named Johnson, who was accused
assaulting Mrs. J. J. Butts of Goshen
of last month.
