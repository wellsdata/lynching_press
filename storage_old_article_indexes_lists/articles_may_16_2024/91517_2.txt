Tarred and Feathered by Mob.
Chicago, April 5.- Frederick Wil-
helm Gustave Ehlen, a German, of
Minneapolis, was turned over to federal
authorities today for questioning
He was tarred and feathered in Flint,
Mich., last Tuesday and left there
Wednesday. When Ehlen was met
by detectives on arriving here a suit
case he was carrying contained clippings
of German raids and
