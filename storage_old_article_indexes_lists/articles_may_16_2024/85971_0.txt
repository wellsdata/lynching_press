STRUNG HER UP BY THE THUMBS.
Brutal Treatment of a Wife by a
Husband Who May Be Lynched.
IBY ASSOCIATED PRESS.]
SPRINGFIELD, O., Nov. 26.- At Wellston, a
mining town on the Ohio Southern Railroad,
Julius Boswicker, a miner, tied his wife's
thumbs together with a string, fastened a
rope to her thumbs and hoisted her up by a
pulley in the ceiling.
The poor woman nearly died of fright and
pain before she was rescued, and is still in a
a precarious condition. There are threats
lynching. of
