Omaha, Neb., Sept. 29.- After a
night of mob rule, during which
a negro was lynched, an attempt
made to hang the mayor of Omaha,
Edward P. Smith, the county court
house burned, one man, said to be
an ex- soldier shot and killed and
perhaps forty others injured, the
city was quiet today under patrol
federal troops ordered from Fort
of Omaha and Fort Crook.
