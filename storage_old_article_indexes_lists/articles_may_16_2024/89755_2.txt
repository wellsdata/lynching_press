Negro Murderer Lynched.
BRYAN ,, Tex., Nov. 18.- Last night at the
Allen farm, near here, while gambling for
pecans, a negro named General Cheatham
was stabbed twice and killed. Another
negro, Tom Sweat, was arrested for the
crime and while being conveyed to Millican
by a posse was taken from his guards and
up to the limb of a tree. The vigilantes
strung are said to have been negroes.
