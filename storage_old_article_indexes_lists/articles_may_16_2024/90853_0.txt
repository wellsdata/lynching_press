ST. LOUIS, Nov. 29.- The great excitement
at Potosi. Mo., is over. The murderers
of the Saphire family who were arrested
were confined in jail there. On Saturday
the mob attempted to lynch the prisoners
The Sheniff's posse were compelled to fire
upon the mob, and killed one. The crowd
then dispersed
