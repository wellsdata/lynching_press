LYNCH LAW Three Men Hanged.- We
learn from the St. Louis Republican that three
men were hanged in Clay county, Missouri, on
the 10th inst. Their names were Wm. and John
Shackleford, and Callaway. They were accused
of horse stealing, and in resisting the arrest
, killed a man by the name of Ross. After
commission of the homicide they were hung
by the the populace.
