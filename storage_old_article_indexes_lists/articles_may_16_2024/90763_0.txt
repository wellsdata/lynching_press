ARKANSAS NEGRO LYNCHED
MONTICELLO, Ark., March 22- Phil Slater, a negro, 50, who tonight
confessed he attacked a white woman near Wilmar last week was taken from
the jail tonight and lynched. In making his confession he said" I did it,
but please here give me a trial." The mob hanged the negro to a telephone pole
