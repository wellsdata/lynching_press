THIS NEGRO LYNCHED HIMSELF.
Macon, Ga., Oct. 24 .-- The body of
Henry Crawford, the negro who was
lynched at Wellston night before last
was carried through Macon yesterday,
afternoon en route to Atlanta, where
it will be presented to the medical college
for dissection. There are many
versions to how his death was
brought about as. The story given out at
Wellston was that the negro heard
a
mob coming, and in his fright placed
a rope about his, climbed a tree,
tied the rope to a neck limb and jumped off.
