An Alabama Negro Pays the Usual
Penalty for Assault on a Woman.
Brantley, Ala., Ang. 23.- Charles
Hurt, a negro, was lynched here last
night. He had made two attempts to
assault Mrs. Stephen Battle, a widow.
He was arrested late yesterday afternoon
and confined in a little frame cal.
aboose. During the night a mob secured
the prisoner, marched him to a
swamp a mile from town and tied him
to a tree. He confessed the crime. The
mob, every member of which was arm.
ed, stepped back a few paces and each
man opened fire and continued to shoot
bullets into Hurt until almost every
inch of him was pierced
