Negro Murderer Lynched."
Vicksburg, Miss., March 5.- The body
of Dr. William B. Oliphant, who was
shot today by a half- crazy negro at
Helms station, arrived here today.
From the same train the negro who
was responsible for the death of Dr.
Oliphant was taken from the deputy at
Elizabeth station by a masked body of
men, and reports that reach here are to
the effect that he promptly
