A NEGRO LYNCHED.
When Arrested for Wife Beating He
Felled the Officer With an Ax.
St. Louis, Mo., Oct. 11.- A special to
the Republic from New Orleans says:
" Dave Jackson, colored, was lynched in
front of the Covington jail yesterday.
Jackson, who lived at Abita, was charged
with wife beating. He defied arrest and
when the officers tried to capture him he
struck one of them over the head with
ax and seriously injured him." an
