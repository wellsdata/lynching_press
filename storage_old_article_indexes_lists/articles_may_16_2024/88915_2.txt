Wilmington, Del., June 26.- After the
hearing before Magistrate Hollis today
Arthur Cornell, arrested on the
charge of murder in connection with
the White lynching, was dismissed,
there being no evidence against him.
Leander Moore and Joseph Shockley,
negroes arrested for shooting Policemen
McDermott and Green during the
disturbance last night, were adjudged
guilty of inciting riot. Moore was giv-
a year in jail and Shockley was
en fined$ 50.
