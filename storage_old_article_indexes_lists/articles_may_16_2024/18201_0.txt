Alabama.
EFFORTS TO BREAK UP A REPUBLICAN MEETING
ONE MAN SHOT.
MONTGOMERY, Sept. 28.
At Wilson station, a disturbance occurred on
Saturday between two factions of the Republican
party. While W. G. Cox, colored, was
speaking, an attempt was made to break up
the meeting. A pistol shot was fired, and one
of the assailing party, colored, fell dead. The
mob then tried to Lynch Cox, but he was pro
