A Prospective Hanging.
CHARLESTON, W. Va., May 2.- The
Sheriff of Mason County, from Point
Pleasant, telegraphed here this morning
for cartridges. There is a prospect of a
mob lynching James Kirby, who killed a
man at Clifton and who was fined$ 100
and imprisoned for thirty days. The people
