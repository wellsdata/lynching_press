A Firebug Lynched
Montgomery, Ala., December 28.
George Fuller, a negro, was lynched
last night near Marion, Perry county.
Wednesday night the barn of Dennis
Cummings was burned, together with
several hundred bushels of corn, a large
amount of forage and seven mules. Fuller
had been a tenant of Cummings and
had made threats concerning a settlement
. He was arrested and while on
the way to Marion, a body of masked
men took him from the and
hanged him to a tree. officers
