ANOTHER NEGRO LYNCHED.
Accused of Having Entered a
White Woman's House.
Vicksburg. Miss., April 19.- Advices from
Bolton state that Henry McAfee, a negro
accused of an attempted assault upon a
Mrs. Saunders in Hinds County, was hanged
Ly a mob in front of the Brownsville
schoolhouse.
He entered Mrs. Saunders's room, but
was frightened off and was captured a few
hours by citizens who went in pursuit.
The lynching later followed.
