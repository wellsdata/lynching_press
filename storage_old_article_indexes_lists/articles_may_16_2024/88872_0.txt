LYNCHING. THREATEN
Special to Times- Republican.
Oskaloosa, Feb. 14.- William Galla-
gher's daughter, aged 12, living two
miles northeast of Eddyville, was crim-
inally assaulted last night on a public
highway and may die. She accused
Ed Davidson, of Pekay, and he was arrested
. News of the affair spread in the
vicinity and a mob hastily gathered
near the jail, preparations being made
for a lynching. But Davidson was
slipped out of the back door and hurried
to Ottumwa
