Two Negroes Lynched
Columbus, Miss., July 15 .-( P) -Two
young Negro farmers, Bert Moore and
Dooley Morton, each about 26, were
lynched Monday 8 miles south of
Columbus. A mob of white citizens
wrested the prisoners from a deputy
sheriff hanged them to an oak
tree. and
The Negroes were arrested Sunday
after two alleged attempts to attack
a white woman, the wife of an Ark
ansas lumberman, who resided about
mile from Zion Negro church, the
a site of the double lynching.
Hands of the victims were tied behind
their backs, ropes placed around
necks and they were dropped
