THE brothers William and Bradford
Courts quarreled with James and John
Smith, near Fairview, Ky., the other day,
and Bradford used his pistol with fatal effect
. Officers and citizens pursued the
slayer to his residence, where they found a
large quantity of stolen goods. The brothers
were arrested and taken to Brookville
for examination. On the night of the 26th
mob overpowered the jailer and hanged
the a prisoners to a tree.
