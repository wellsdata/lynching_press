Beaten to Death.
John Sterling, a disreputable character
who had been working the Gar-
field mine near Rimini, was brought
into Helena on Saturday by the sherift
and placed in jail. On the night o
the 6th inst. Sterling, who is a man
about 32 years of age, cruelly beat and
kicked his younger brother in a fit of
passion, inflicting injuries that resulted
in the death of the lad on Sunday
afternoon, the 8th inst. The murdered
boy was 18 years old and had recently
arrived from the east on a visit
to his brother. The affair created
great excitement in and about Rimini
and there were threats of lynching the
brutal Sterling, but the officers suc-
in capturing him and bringing
ceeded him safely into the city.- Herald.
