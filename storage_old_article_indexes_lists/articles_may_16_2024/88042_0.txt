HIRED NEGRO TO KILL WIFE.
Then an Alabaman Led the Mob
That Lynched the Slayer.
Andalusa, Ala., July 19.- Sam Spicer
, Jr., was found guilty today
murdering his wife, Mrs. Nobie
Spicer, and his punishment was
fixed at life imprisonment.
Spicer, who is 30 years old, was
charged with having hired Joe
Green, his negro chauffeur, to kill
Mrs. Spicer, and it was testified that
Spicer, a few days later, led
