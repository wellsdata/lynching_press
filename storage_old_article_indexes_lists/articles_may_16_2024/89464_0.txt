NEGRO FIEND LYNCHED
CHEYENNE, Wyo., Oct. 2.- Frank
Wigfall, a negro who assaulted Mrs.
Julia Higgins, aged 76, at Rawlins,
Monday night, was lynched by the convicts
in the state penitentiary at Raw-
lins today.
Wigfall was captured last night and
placed in the county jail. A mob sur-
rounded the jail threatening to lynch
Wigfall, but was dispersed. The convicts
took a rope and lynched Wig-
fall.
Wigfall chopped down the door of
the Higgins' home and overpowered
the aged woman and assaulted her.
