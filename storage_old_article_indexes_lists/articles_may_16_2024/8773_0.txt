Alabama Negro Lynched.
BIRMINGHAM, Ala., Sept. 21- Johr
Fitch, colored, was lynched last night
near Chapel Hill, Charleston county.
He had broken into the room of Miss
Harrington, daughter of Judge Har-
rington, a few evenings ago, but was
driven away. He was arrested, and
taken by a masked mob and
lynched was while en route to jail.
