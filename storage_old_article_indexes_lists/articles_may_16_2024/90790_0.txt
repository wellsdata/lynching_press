A Negro Thief Lynched by a Mob in
Maryland.
BALTIMORE, MD., November 28 .-- A report
has reached here that George Bris-
toe, colored, was lynched Wednesday
night at Newbridge, in Anne Arundel
County, this State. He was charged with
cobbery, and when delivered to the Constable
to be taken to jail at Annapolis, a
Browd waylaid the officer, took Briscoe
And hung him. He had previously been
warned leave the county because of
irequent to robberies in the neighborhood.
