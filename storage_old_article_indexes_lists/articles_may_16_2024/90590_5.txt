New Jersey Mob Mad;
Lynching Planned
Merchantville, N. J., Oct. 1.- A mob
of blood thirsty citizens of Merchants-
ville are scouring the outlying district
for a colored man accused of attacking
Mrs. Notsey, white.
The sheriff of Burlington county is
also leading a posse in search of the
alleged criminal. Citizens avow that
the man will be lynched but the authorities
declare that no cruelty will
be tolerated.
