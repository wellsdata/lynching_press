LYNCHING AHEAD OF A LOTHARIO.
Mrs. Philyag Writes a Letter of
Warning to Her Lover.
Athens, Ga., Dec. 7.- Mrs. J. D. Philyaw,
the Mississippi woman who ran away from
her husband with John T. Alexander, and
who was stopped at this place, has written
back from her home in Mississippi to
Alexander, who is now here in jail, telling
him to prepare to die and meet his
God, as a mob is ready to lynch him when
he is brought back to Mississippi. Mrs.
Philyaw is still infatuated with Alexan
der, and writes to him in an endearing
manner. An attempt will be made here
next Wednesday to have Alexander
