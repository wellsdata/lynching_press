A Narrow Escape.
Huntington, W. Va., May 23.- Charles
Ringo, who was arrested at Point
Pleasant yesterday for the murder of
his two children here, was brought
back here today. The train was met
in the suburbs by a closed carriage
and Ringo was rushed to the jail in
disguise. Two thousand people had
assembled at the depot and a lynching
is anticipated tonight The jail is
strongly guarded by deputy. sheriffs.
