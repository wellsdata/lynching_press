MOB LYNCH A SUBSTITUTE
Trouble Over Dog Results in Killing of
Deputy Sheriff- Posse Mob
Murderer's Brother.
Portland, Ark.- A posse of citizens
lynched Joe Blakeley, a negro, broth
er of Sam Blakeley, who shot and
killed Deputy Sheriff Cain near
here. Unable to catch the alleged
murderer of Cain, the posse lynched
the brother after he declared that he
intended to" get" Bud Harper, who
had killed the Blakeley brothers' dog.
Trouble the dog resulted in Cain's
death and over the lynching followed.
