ATTEMPTED LYNCHING ARFAIR.- On Sunday
afternoon, the 22d instant, while Andrew Porche
,
an ex- policeman, was sitting in a coffee house, at
New Orleans, George Foster and two other men
entered, and forcibly slipping a rope, noosed for
the purpose, over his head, strung him up to the
ceiling. Bystanders interfered and cut Porche
down, when he was nearly dead. The cause of
the attempt was a suspicion on the part of Foster,
that Porche had reported him for selling liquor to
slaves. Foster was arrested, but his
escaped. accomplices
