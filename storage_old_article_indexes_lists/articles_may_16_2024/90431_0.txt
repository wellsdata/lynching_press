Roanoke, Va., March 2.-R. D.
Troynham, who was arrested here
last night, charged with the murder
of his father, died suddenly on the
train which was taking him to Lynch-
burg for safe keeping today. An
autopsy showed that Troynham had
died from fright. Threats were made
by a mob here to lynch him. Troyn-
ham's young wife, who has been prostrated
since his arrest, gave birth
a child today. to
