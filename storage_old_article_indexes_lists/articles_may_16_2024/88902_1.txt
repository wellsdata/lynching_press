The Widow of Another Victim of the
New Orleans Riot Sues for a Big Sum.
NEW ORLEANS, March 15.- The tenth
damage suit by relatives of the Italians
lynched in New Orleans has been filed,
making a total of$ 270,000. The
widow of Politz brings the suit.
Politz, it will be remembered,
was taken from the parish prison
and hanged. This suit differs from the
other nine, as it makes Parkerson,
Dengre, Baldwin, Houston, Railey,
McLean, Couturie, Venables, Mather,
Walmsley and thirty other residents of
New Orleans the defendants, and is
also brought for$ 100,000 instead
$ 30,000, as are the claims of the others of.
