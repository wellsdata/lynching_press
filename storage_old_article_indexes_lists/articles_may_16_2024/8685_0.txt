LYNCH LAW IN NEBRASKA.
CHICAGO, Dec. 10.- A Tribune Omaha
special says that the jury at Nebraska
City found Harvey Jackson, Henry Martin
and Wm. S. Givens guilty of murder
in the second degree. for killing Charles
Slocumb and outraging his wife. They
were sentenced to the penitentiary for
life. At two o'clock this morning Mar-

a mob and hung, half a mile from the
house. Givens was taken to the
