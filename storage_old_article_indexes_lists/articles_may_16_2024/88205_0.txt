WIFE MURDERER LYNCHED
.
An Iowa Mob of 1,000 Mea Break into
Jail to Get Him.
Waterloo, Iowa, Jan. 9.- A crowd of
more than 1,000 men to- night battered
through the walls of the county jail
at Charles City, Iowa, with railroad
irons, tore the hinges from the doors and
took James Cullen out and lynched him
for murder and the murder of his
stepson wife yesterday morning.
