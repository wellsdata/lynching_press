NEGRO MURDERS
AND IS MURDERED
Wouldn't Work Out a Debt and Lynchers
Get Him.
MACON, Ga., June 8.- Banjo Peavy, a
negro, was hanged by a mob tonight for
the shooting today of W. Cope Winslow
Jr. Peavy owed Winslow a small amount
of money and was asked to work out the
debt. He refused, and shot Winslow
through the forehead. Peavy was captured
and turned over to the sheriff.
Tonight negro was lynohed and his
body was the riddled with bullets.
