SAYS TAFT IS WEAK.
Boston, Mass., Aug. 29.- In denouncing
at tonight's session of the
National Independent Political League
the lynching of negroes, bishop Alex-
ander Walters declared President Taft
was" one of the weakest presidents
the country had ever had in view of
the fact that he had remained silent
the face of continued reports of
in lynchings about the country.
