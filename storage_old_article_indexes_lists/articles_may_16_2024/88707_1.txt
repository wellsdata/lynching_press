NEGRO REPORTED LYNCHED.
Man Who Shot Dr. C. C. McMillan Was
Taken Away From Sheriff.
Vicksburg, January 14.- News was received
here today that" Butch" Riley, the
negro who shot Dr. C. C. McMillan near
Tallulah, Tuesday night, was arrested
and placed in jail at Tallulah but the
sheriff was relieved of the negro by a
strong mob last night and the negro
lynched. Particulars cannot be learned.
McMillan was prominent and there is but
slight hopes for his recovery. It is reported
that negro's body was cut down
this afternoon the.
