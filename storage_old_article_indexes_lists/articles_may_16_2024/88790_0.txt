A Blood- Thirsty Mob Forms to Lynch a
BURLINGTON, Feb. 11.- A mob gath-
ered here for the purpose of lynching
A. B. Storms, murderer of Mrs. Rath-
burn and her daughter. The authorities
learned of their intentions and
quietly removed Storms from the jail
to a private house and later conveyed
him to Mediapolis, where a train was
taken for Anamosa. The mob scoured
the town for Storms, who had left the
jail twenty minutes when the
would but- be lynchers arrived.
