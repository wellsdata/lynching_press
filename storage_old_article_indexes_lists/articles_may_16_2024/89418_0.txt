Negro Lynched by Negroes.
Guthrie, Ky., Jan. 25.- Lewis Rad-
ford, a negro, was lynched here by a
mob of from 30 to 40 negroes. He
was arrested this morning, accused of
killing Priscilla Frozell, also a negro,
last night. Radford congessed to having
assaulted woman, but denied
to the last the the act of killing her.
