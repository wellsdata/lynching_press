GRAND JURY BALKS
ON LYNCHING PROBE
Westchester, Pa., September 10.- Declaring
inability to get sufficient evidence
to warrant indictment of additional
persons in connection with the
recent lynching of the negro Zach Walker
at Coatesville, the grand jury in-
vestigating the case asked to be ex-
eused today. Judge Butler ordered the
to get back to work. He said the
jury lynchers must be brought to justice.
