Lynched Fiend. Negro
BELAIR, Md., March 26.- Lewis Harris
( colored), who was arrested here last
night for committing a felonious assault
on Miss Annie Mcllvaine, a recluse, was
lynched to- night. Sheriff Kinart and his
deputy fought to protect their prisoner,
and fired into mob, wounding two of
them. They were the overpowered.
