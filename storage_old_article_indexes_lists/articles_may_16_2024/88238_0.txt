FLEMING'S BODY
BROUGHT HERE
Negro Lynched at McGehee Yesterday
Will Be Buried Here This After
noon- Member Local Colored
Lodge.
The body of Sam Fleming, the Pine
Bluff negro who was lynched by a party
of masked men yesterday morning
at McGehee, Desha county, was
brought to this city last night for interment
. The local negro Knights of
Pythias lodge, of which Fleming was
a member, took charge of the body
and will attend this afternoon from
St. Paul's church. Fleming's wife
lives here He also had a number of
other relatives. in this city.
