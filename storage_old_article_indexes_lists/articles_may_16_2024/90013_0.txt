Negro Murderer Lynched By an Infuriated
Mob.
CINCINNATI, Jan. 14.- Henry Corbin,
the negro who murdered Mrs. Georgetta
Horner at Oxford, O., last Tuesday night,
was lynched there at 10 o'clock tonight.
He had been hiding ever since Tuesday
in a shed within a few hundred feet of the
place where he had committed the murder.
Growinfi hungry he had sent a negro boy
up town to get him something to eat, and
the boy told that he was getting food for
Corbin and told where the murderer was
