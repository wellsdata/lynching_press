Murderer Lynched on the Spot.
CETTINJE, July 7.- Colonel Martinovitz,
commandant of the body guard of Prince
Nicholas, ruler of Montenegro, and brother
of the Prince, was murdered in the market
place this morning. The murder was the
result of private vengeance. The murderer
immediately seized by the people in
the was market place and lynched on the spot.
