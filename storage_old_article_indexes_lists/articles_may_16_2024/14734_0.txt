Mob Lynches Four White ien.
Ada, Okla., April 19- At 3 o'clock
this morning a mob of 200 men
stormed the county jail here and
after overpowering the sheriff
lynched four white men. The men
lynched are: J. B. Miller, of Fort
Worth, Tex., charged with the murder
of A. A. Bobbitt, a wealthy cattleman
of this section; D. B. Bur-
rell Jesse West and Joseph Allen,
alleged, accomplices.
