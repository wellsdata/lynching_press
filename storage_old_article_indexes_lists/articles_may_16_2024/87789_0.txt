TURMOIL IN FLORIDA ENDED
TAMPA, June 3.( PP) -The city is quiet today
and apparently the seige of the Hillsborough Jail
is at an end after three nights of riot and disturbance
. Five persons are dead from the results of
wounds received in the clash between the mob and
the National Guards. One thousand special deputies
are on special guard duty. Thirty alleged
members of the mob which attempted to enter the
jail to take a confessed murderer out and lynch
him, are under arrest awaiting the action of
