MOB LYNCHED NEGRO
AT LAKE VILLAGE.
Doors of Jail Battered Down at Noon
day by Lynchers- Negroes Had
Killed Frank Anderson and
Wounded Vinson.
Lake Village, Nov. 3.- Henry John
son, colored, was taken from jail here
at noon today by a mob and hanged
in the center of town. The lynching
was the result of a shooting affray
which
occurred between 1 and 2
o'clock this morning in Joe Frame's
saloon between negroes on one side
and whites on the other. One white
man, Frank Anderson, was killed and
another, Baldy Vinson, a well known
lawyer of Little Rock, was wounded,
but not seriously. others
