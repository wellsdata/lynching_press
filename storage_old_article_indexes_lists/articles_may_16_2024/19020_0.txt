NEGRO IS LYNCHED BY A
MOB AT GREEN RIVER.
WYOMING
James Woodson, a negro, was taken
from jail at Green River, Wyo., this
afternoon by a mob of five hundred
men, mostly railroad employes, and
lynched,
The negro this morning shot and
killed a railroad switchman and
wounded another man.
The shooting occurred in the Union
Pacific station. The switchman, whose
name was given as Miller, and an
other railroad employe, were standing
in the station when they heard the negro
make an insulting remark to a
young white woman employed at the
station restaurant. A fight followed
