Mob Lynches Two Negroes.
A double lynching, in which Jim Redd
and Alex Johnson, negroes, were the victims
, occurred at Monticello, Ark. A mob
broke down the doors of the jail and entering
the cellroom poured a volley of
shots into the cages where the men were
confined. Johnson is dead and Redd is
fatally wounded. They were convicted of
killing W. F. Skipper, a rich planter and
merchant of Baxter, and were sentenced
hang. Appeal to the Supreme Court
to had granted them a new trial.
