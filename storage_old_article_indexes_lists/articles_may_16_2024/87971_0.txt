BLACK FIEND LYNCHED.
Confessed to Murder of Two White
Women.
Will Clifford, a negro, arrested at Tip-
tonville, Tenn., has confessed to assaulting
Mrs. Mary Cowan( white) and throwing her
body into the river and to killing another
woman a year ago. Constable Burrus secreted
Clifford in a barn to avoid a mob
that threatened the jail. Burrus finally
started with Clifford for another place and
at Maple, Ky., a posse caught up with
them The constable was overpowered and
the negro. strung up.
