The bogus baronet had, besides the drafts
in his pockets, a passage- ticket to Liverpool;
and had he not been captured thus early, in a
day or two he would have been on the broad
Atlantic, on his way to Liverpool.
Sir Exeter was brought back to Edmondson
County, and lodged in the county jail. His
deed soon became known to the Kentucky lads

jail, forced their way into the same, and
lynched the prisoner. The mob took him into
