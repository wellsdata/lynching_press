BLOODY EXECUTION OF LYNCH LAW.- The New
Orleans Bee of the 3d, gives an account of the cold
blooded butchery of Mr. Adams and brother, of
Covington, by a band of 20 armed men. Adams
had succeeded in a law suit against some of them.
He was shot deliberately in the day time, and then
his brother. The wife and children escaped. Another
brother had called the Governor for aid to
