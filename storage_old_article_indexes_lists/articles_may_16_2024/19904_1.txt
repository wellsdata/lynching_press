A Child Ravisher Lynched.
LITTLE ROCK, Ark., May 14- Henry
James, colored, brutally assaulted Mag-
gie Doxey, five years old, Wednesday.
Friday afternoon he was arrested and
placed in jail. Friday evening a mob
overpowered the jailer, took the prison
out and hanged him. The cadaver
er was then riddled with bullets.
