Saved From Lynching.
Fort Smith, Ark., Aug. 27.- Louis
Smith the 55- year- old negro whom a
,
mob tried to reach in the county jail
here at midnight, was saved from
lynching through the pleading of the
father of Lucy Watson, the little
white girl assaulted by Smith, and by
the prompt action of Chief of Police
John Fuller. Smith was finally re
moved from the flimsy to the federal
prison. jail
