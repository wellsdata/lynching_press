Shot by a Doctor.
VAN WERT, Ohio, Feb. 11.- Yester.
day George Bell, a young man, died
from the effects of a gunshot wound
caused by the carelessness of Dr. Joseph
W. Dunfee. There was a fox hunt last
Saturday, in which Dr. Dunfee carelessly
discharged his shotgun, mortally
wounding young Bell. The rage
against the doctor was such that
was talk of lynching him. there
