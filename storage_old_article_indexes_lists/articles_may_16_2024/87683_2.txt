DIDN'T LIKE THE VERDICT.
Citizens of Williamsburg, Ky., Take a
Convicted Prisoner From Jail
and Hang Him ..
Williamsburg, Ky .. Aug. 22.- Elcany Sul-
livan, convicted yesterday of the rape of
Sarah Lawson and sentenced to twenty
years in the penitentiary, was taken from
jail last night by a mob and hung on the
timbers of the county bridge across the
Cumberland river The lynching occurred
at 1 o'clock this morning..
