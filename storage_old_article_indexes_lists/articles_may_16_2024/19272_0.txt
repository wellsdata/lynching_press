Negro Lynched.
CHARLESTON, Ill., June 26- Saturday
night Mary A. Bingardiner, a German girl,
sixteen years old, living near Effingham,
came to this cit. She inquired at the depot
for a lodging house.and William Moore,
colored, offered to escort her to one.
On the way he overpowered her and
criminally assaulted her. She complained
to the police and Moore was arrested
, convicted and committed to jail
About midnight a band of masked men
from Mattoon, Shelbyville and Effingham
broke into the jail overpowered the guards

tank, hanged him. The crowd disperse C
