Negro Who Attacked
Woman Must Die
Knoxville, Tenn., Oct. 18.- -Maurice
F. Mayes, negro, who slew Mrs. Ber-
tie Lindsey, when he invaded her
home at night, August 29, the crime
resulting in the storming of the Knox
county jail by a mob bent upon lynching
him and subsequent race riots,
was today sentenced to electrocution
on November 28, within the prison
walls at Nashville, his motion for a
new trial having been overruled this
morning by Judge Nelson, of
criminal court. the
