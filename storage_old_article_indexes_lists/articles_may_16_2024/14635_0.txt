Negro Lynched.
St. Louis, Sept. 21.- A special to the
Post- Dispatch from Luxora, Ark., says:
Negroes Friday night overpowered the
sheriff here, took out a negro named Hel-
len and hanged him to a water tank,
where his body was left dangling until
Saturday morning. Hellem was charged
criminally assaulting two little
negro with girl, aged five and ten.
