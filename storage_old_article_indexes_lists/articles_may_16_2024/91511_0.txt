MURDERER LYNCHED.
Omaha, Neb, Sept 6.- A Kemmerer,
Wyo, special says that it is reported
there that John W. Keffer, the condemned
murderer, who was to have
been executed yesterday, but who obtained
a stay of execution, was taken
from the jail at Lander and lynched
last night, but no confirmation can be
obtained, because of the distance
tween the two points. be-
