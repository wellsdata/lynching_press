NO MORE LYNCHING
CENTRALIA, Nov. 12.- Judge Dy
sart this afternoon in an address
said:" There will be no more lynching
, boys." He was listened to by
citizens and members of the Bar Asing
sociation who agreed to let the law
take its course.
Handbills were posted throughout
the city by officials asking citizens to
report all knowledge of the shooting
and all knowledge of the activities
of the reds, in order to secure evidence
.
The I. W. W. member lynched was
not named" Britt" Smith, as first
announced, but was known as" Brick"
Smith, according to many making the
identification of the body, befores it
dropped to the stream. No
will be made to recover the attempt body.
