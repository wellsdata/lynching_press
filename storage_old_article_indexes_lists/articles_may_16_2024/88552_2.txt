TEN MEN INDICTED
IN ELMORE COUNTY
Charged With Being Members
of a Mob Who Lynched
a Negro
Wetumpka, August 23 .-( Special.)- The
grand jury at a special term of the Circuit
Court, convened by Judge Denson to
investigate the recent lynching of a negro
in this county, charged with assault
to murder, has found indictments against
ten of thirty members constituting the
mob and are still investigating the case.
Several who refused to testify when
called as witnesses were placed in jail
for contempt of court.
