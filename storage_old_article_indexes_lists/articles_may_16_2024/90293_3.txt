PROTESTED HIS INNOCENCE.
But That Did Not Prevent a Maryland Mob
from Hanging a Negro.
ROCKVILLE, Md., July 6.- Sidney
Randolph, a negro, charged with the
murder of little Sadie Buxton, and
with assault with intent to commit
murder on the girl's father and mother
and elder sister, during the night of
May 25, at the home of the Buxtons,
near Gaithersburg, was lynched before
daylight on Saturday by a mob of determined
men, who overcame the
sheriff and forced him to surrender the
keys of the jail The negro protested
his innocence of. the crime to the last.
