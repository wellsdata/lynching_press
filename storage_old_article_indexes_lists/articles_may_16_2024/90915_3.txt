Mass Meeting at Institutional Church.
There will be a meeting of the Colored
Citizens of Chicago at the Institutional
church, 38th and Dearborn
streets, Sunday, June 14th, at 4
o'clock p. m., to take action on the
recent lynching of W. David Wyatt
at Belleville, Illinois. All are invited.
