Mob Dances at Lynching.
The negro was caught in a saloon
and dragged to the street. Then a rope
was swung around his neck and the
other end of the rope was thrown over
the limb of a tree. With a cry of satisfaction
the negro was swung high off
the ground. He was dead in a few moments
. After riddling the body with
bullets the mob commenced to dance
around the tree. At this point the company
from Decatur arrived and company
