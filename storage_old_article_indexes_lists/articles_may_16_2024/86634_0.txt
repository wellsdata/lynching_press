A NEGRO LYNCHED- The Baltimore
American of Nov. 3, has a letter from Dal-
ten, Caroline county in that state, giving
an account of the lynching of a negro under
arrest for the outrage and murder of
little girl, daughter of Edgar Plummer,
on Saturday night A large number of
excited people came into town, surrounded
the jail, forced open the doors, took
the prisoner and hung him. While suspended
fifteen bullets perforated his body.
He was cut down and his throat cut by
the infuriated mob. The body was dragged
through the streeets, and finally taken
to the front of negro church where it
was cut to pieces the and burned.
