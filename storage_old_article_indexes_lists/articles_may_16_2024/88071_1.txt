ACCUSED MURDERER LYNCHED
Taken From Carolina Jail and Hanged
to Nearby Tree.
Wadsboro, N. C., May 28.- A mob of
200 last night lynched J. V. Johnson,
accused of murdering his brother- in-
law. Johnson was taken from the jail
and hanged to a nearby tree. The
sheriff was knocked senseless by the
mob, who made the raid on the jail.
