Professors Chase Negro.
CHICAGO, IL .. Aug. 2.- Hyde Park
and the academic atmosphere of the University
of Chicago were superheated yesterday
afternoon as a result of the chase
of G. Marbel, a negro, who attacked a
janitress at the Windermere hotel, resisted
arrest, shot a detective and a banana
peddler, and finally escaped across the
university campus.
Automobiles, three patrol wagons, thirty
policemen, several professors, scores
of students and merchants took part in
the chase. Several classes were broken
Shouts of" String him up!" and" Hang
him!" mingled with" Stop thief!" sounded
by the employes and residents of the
hotel were taken up by pedestrians
