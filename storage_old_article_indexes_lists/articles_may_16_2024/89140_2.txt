Montgomery Mob Seeks Prisoner.
Montgomery, October 21.- A mob of 50
to 100 went toward No. 4 state prison, nine
miles out today, looking for Herman
Thompson, the negro who assaulted little
Callie Cain Friday. The main part of
the crowd remained out of sight, while
two of the men went and were allowed to
examine the cells and prisoners. They
left satisfied that the negro was not
there. The whereabouts of the negro
