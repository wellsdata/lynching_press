MOB ATTACKS JAIL TO
LIBERATE MURDERERS
Speedy Texas Justice Incites Mexi-
cans to Riot
[ ASSOCIATED PRESS DISPATCH]
SAN ANTONIO, Dec. 29. Fifty
American citizens repelled the attack
by a mob of 150 Mexicans at Oak-
ville, Live Oak county, tonight when
an attempt was made to liberate
Ysidro Gonzales, a Mexican, convict-
and sentenced to be hanged, and
the
Frederico Sanchez, indicted for
murder of Jailer Hinton four days
ago.
Gonzales, Sanchez and a third man
were captured by mobs recently when
charged with the murder of the jailer
, who was killed while he slept.
The prisoners in jail then escaped.
A mob lynched one of the men captured
and turned the other two over
to the authorities, after Judge Cham-
blis pledged his honor the
men
would be given speedy trials. Gon-
zales was put on trial this morning
and by night had been convicted and
sentenced to be hanged.
