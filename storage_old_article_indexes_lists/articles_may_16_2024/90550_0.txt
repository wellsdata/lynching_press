The Terrible Crime
of a Louisiana Negro.
NEW ORLEANS, La., Feb. 18.- Thomas Jackson, a negro called at
the home of Alexander Burgeois near the town of St. Peter twenty
miles north of here, presented a decoy message which lured Bourgeois
from his home and stabbed him in the back. Returning Jackson
butchered Mrs. Bourgeois and. her two babies and ransacked the
house. Two boys visiting the escaped and gave the alarm. A
mob caught the negro, hung him place and riddled him with bullets.
