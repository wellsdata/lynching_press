MOB OF 1,000 HANGS NEGRO.
Parents Of Woman Begged Lynchers
To Stop.
Shreveport, La.- A mob of 1,000 took
Jess Hammet, a negro, from the jail at
Vivian, 20 miles north of here, and
hanged him to a telegraph pole. He
was identified by a white woman as
the man who attempted an assault
upon her, armed with a butcher knife.

among
