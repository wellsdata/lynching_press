DE PRIEST PAYS
OGDEN TRIBUTE
ON MOB STAND
G. O. P. Absolved of Blame
for Marion Killings by
Negro Congressman.
Oscar De Priest, Negro Republican
congressman from Chicago,
absolved the Republican party of
blame in lynching of two Negroes at
Marion Aug. 7, at a reception in nis
honor at Phyllis Wheatly branch
of the Y. W. C. A. Friday.
Two hundred Negro
heard De Priest. Republicans
