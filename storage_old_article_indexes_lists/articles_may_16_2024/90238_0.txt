Texas Mob Tries to Lynch Negro- One
Lyncher Dead.
Henderson, Tex., Oct. 7.- Late last
night a party of young white men went
to the home of Bob Willis, a negro,
for the purpose of flogging him for
some offense, not clearly stated.
The negro warned them not to approach
but they continued to advance,
when the negro fired, killing Chas.
Brown, a young man recently from
Georgia and wounding a young man
named Gossett.
