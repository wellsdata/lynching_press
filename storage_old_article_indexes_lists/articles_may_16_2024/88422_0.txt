Unnecessarily Alarmed.
Knoxville, Tenn., April 25.- After
spending twenty- four hours of anxious
waiting Sheriff Grower's men emerged
from behind the perfect barricade of
arms at the Knox county jail to find that
no mob had shown up to lynch the murderers
of Mrs. Givens nor had any been
formed. Last night's suspense, terror
and preparations have availed nothing
but an appetite for slumber in official
reportorial circles. and
