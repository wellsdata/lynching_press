MOB HANGS NEGRO.
Man Accused of Assault Quietly Lynched
in Alabama.
Prattsville, Ala., May 7.- News
reached this city this morning that
Gainer Hall, the negro who assaulted
Mrs. Josiah Owens yesterday, was
caught at Kingston yesterday after-
noon by a possee, who took him to the
scene of the crime and quietly hanged
him to a tree. The body was then riddled
with bullets.
It is said that the negroes refuse to
take down the body and bury it. It
is also reported today that Mrs. Owens
is in a critica! condition. The town
