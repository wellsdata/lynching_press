MOB REPULSED BY
A HIDDEN OFFICER
Attempt to Lynch Two Ken-
tucky Murderers Proved
Unsuccessful
Lebanon, Ky., August 23.- An unsuccessful
attempt was made early this
morning to lynch Dan Hotten and Charles
Shipp, who are confined in the Marion
county jail charged with murder. The
mob called at the house of Jailer J. Clay-
ton, but his wife refused them the keys
to the jail. They went to the jail and be.
gan to batter the doors, when Officer
Brent, who was secreted in a coalshed
nearby, fired upon them. The mob re-
the fire, but no one was hit. They
turned then dispersed.
