Negroes Lynched.
MARSHALL, Tex, Feb. 15- A mob
lynched George Sanders and Mary Jack-
son, a negro and a negress, hanging
them to the same tree just across the
Panola county line, It was learned here
today. The negroes had lived in the
same house with Jennie Sneed, a young
negress who shot and killed Paul
Strange,, a white man. an January 29.
