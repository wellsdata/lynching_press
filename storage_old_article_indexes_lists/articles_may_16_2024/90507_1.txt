Boy Escapes a Lynching.
Anna, the 14- year- old daughter of Richyard
ard Rust, who lives one mile south of
Divide, Colo., was attacked by a boy
afterward identified as Clyde Gray. Gray
later confessed, but escaped from Deputy
Sheriff Fenton, who had him in charge.
Before his escape a crowd formed to
lynch him but were held back by the
father of the, girl. Gray 17 years old.
