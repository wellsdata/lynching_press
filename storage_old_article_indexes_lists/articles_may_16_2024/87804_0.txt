MOB ATTEMPTS TO LYNCH POL
ICEMAN WHO SHOT PROMINENT
CITIZEN
PANAMA, Aug. 166.( PP) -Eight per
sons are reported killed and many injured
in an attempt to storm the jail
at Buena Ventura. The dispatches
said the mob sought to lynch a police
man whom it held responsible for the
shooting to death of a prominent citizen
. Unable to obtain arms the mob
used rocks and sticks freely. The
police and a handful of soldiers tried
to them back but reinforcements
had force to be called from a nearby town.
