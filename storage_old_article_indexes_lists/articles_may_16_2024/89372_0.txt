COUPLE OF GEORGIA
NEGROES LYNCHED
Social Circle Is Scene of
Actions of Infuriated
Mob
Monroe, Ga, June 27.- Two negroes
were lynched in this county today. Tom
Alen, accused of attacking a white
woman several weeks ago, was taken
from a train at Social Circle, near here,
tied to a telegraph pole and shot to
death. He was in charge of three
officers, who were overpowered by
members of the mob. Nearly 12 hours
later, in broad daylight, men, who are
thought to have composed the first
mob, stormed the jail here, secured Joe
Watte, another negro held for acting
suspiciously around the home of
Haw, and put him to death. Bud
