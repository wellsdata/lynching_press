Mayor Shot By Negro.
Marked Tree, Ark., Oct. 30.- Mayor
N. J. Hazel was shot and seriously
wounded by" Little Rock" Norton, a
negro, and a posse of citizens is
searching the surrounding territory in
an effort to capture the negro, with
the avowed intention of lynching him.
A negro resident of this place had
appealed to Mayor Hazel because of
the alleged attentions of Norton to
the complainant's wife, and when Nor-
ton saw the mayor and the negro approaching
he opened fire, the bullet
striking Mayor Hazel and inflicting
dangerous wound. a
