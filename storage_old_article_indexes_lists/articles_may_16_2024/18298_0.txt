NEGRO LYNCHED.
Pine Bluff, Ark., July 18.- John Cot
ton, a negro seventeen years old, at
tempted to outrage Mrs. John Tucker, a
white woman at Cornersville, twenty
eight miles south of here, but his design
was frustrated by the timely arrival of
the woman's husband. The negro was
captured and hanged to a tree by a mob,
and his dangling body riddled with
lets. bul
