Wanted to Lynch Him.
MARTINSVILLE, Ind .. Sept. 1.- At a
late hour last night while attempting
to arrest a desperado named Dell Core,
City Marshal William Cox was seriously
if not fatally wounded by a knife
thrust in the left side. For a short
time threats of lynching were indulged
in, but the would- be murderer was
hurriedly taken the county jail be
fore the mob had to time to organize.
