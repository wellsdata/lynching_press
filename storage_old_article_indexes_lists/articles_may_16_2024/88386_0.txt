TWO NEGROES
ARE LYNCHED
BY GEORGIANS
Blacks Recently Convicted
of Assault Were Being
Taken to Savannah for
Safe Keeping.
( By The United Press)
Jessup, Ga., July 1- Two
negroes who were recently convicted
of criminal assault and
were being taken to Savannah
for safe keeping by Sheriff
Rogers of Wayne county, were
seized by a mob and lynched
early today. The negroes yesterday
were granted respite
by the governor. a
