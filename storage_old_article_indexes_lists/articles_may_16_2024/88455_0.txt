Driggers Escapes Lynching.
Jacksonville, Fla., July 30 .-- M. M.
Driggers, who shot R. T. Dowling in
Bardford county last Thursday, was
brought to this city today to escape a
mob at Starke, which was bent on lynching
the murderer. Driggers will remain
in jail in city until his trial comes
up at the next this term of court.
