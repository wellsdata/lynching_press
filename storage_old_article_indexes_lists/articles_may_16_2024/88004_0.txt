ALLEGED LYNCHER ARRESTED.
Maryland Justice of the Peace Held
for a Crime Committed Three
Years Ago.
ELLICOTT CITY, MD., June 27.- James
F. Melvin, editor of the Ellicott City Democrat
, and a justice of the peace, was held
here to- day to await the action of a grand
jury upon the charge of having taken part
in the lynching of a negro murderer about
three years ago. Mr. Melvin was arrested
yesterday, and the testimony at the preliminary
hearing to- day was to the effect
that he had discussed the matter with ex-
Sheriff Oldfield, giving details of the affair
and admitting his presence there, and that
he had filed with the telegraph operator at
Ellicott City for an out of town newspaper
a story of the lynching within a short time,
