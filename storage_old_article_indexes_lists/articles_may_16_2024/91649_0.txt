FARMER SHOT WHO NEGRO
GEORGIA MOB LYNCHES
Prisoner Is Taken From Jail
and Hanged and Shot
LINCOLNTON, Ga., May 24.- Albert
Aiken, a negro, who seriously wounded
John Spiers, a white farmer, near
here last Thursday, was hanged by a
posse of about 100 men today.
The posse broke into the jail, seized
the negro and carried him into the
country. His body was found marked
by bullet holes and bearing this placard
:" Notice.- This is what will happen
to all negroes in Lincoln county
similar circumstances.
under" REGULARS."
