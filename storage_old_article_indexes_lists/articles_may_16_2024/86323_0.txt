TWO NEGROES ARE LYNCHED
To the Music of Church Bells They
Are Swung Into Eternity.
[ The Citizen Special Service.l
AUGUSTA, Ga., Feb. 26.- As the
church bells were ringing this morn
were taken from
ing two negroes
the jail at Warrensburg and lynched
by a mob. Very little resistance
was made by the jailors, and the
mob went about its work in a systematic
manner. dispersing quietly
after the negroes had been hanged.
Both of negroes were charged
with having the murdered white men.
