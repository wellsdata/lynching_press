Murderer's Brother le Lynched.
Portland, Ark., May 30.- A posse of
citizens today lynched Joe Blakely,
Negro, brother of Sam Blakely, who
shot and killed Deputy Sheriff Cain
near here yesterday. Unable to catch
the alleged murderer of Cain, the
posse lynched the brother after he
declared that he intended to" get"
Bud Harper.

man who is charged with shooting
Deputy Sheriff Cain, is what they call
