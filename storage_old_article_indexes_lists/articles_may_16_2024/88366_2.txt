SLATON EXCORIATES MOB.
San Francisco, Aug. 17- Former
Gov. John M. Slaton of Georgia,
who commuted the sentenced of
Leo M. Frank and who is here
visiting the exposition, was informed
this morning that Frank had
been lynched.
" The act was a consummate outrage
,' said Mr. Slaton," and every
man engaged in the lynching
should be hanged for he is an assassin
. Such an act is contrary to
the civilization of Georgia and one
which every good citizen will condemn
" I.
