First Will Cannot Be Found.
Boston, June 6.- The last will of the
late J. Montgomery Sears, Boston's largest
tax payer, which disposes of an estate
of about$ 25,000,000, has disappeared.
Mr. Sears' turned the document
attorney
over to him, it is stated, several months
ago, after it had been executed and attested
, but though a previous will, exe-
cuted many years ago, has been found,
family has been unable to discover
the the slightest trace of the document.
