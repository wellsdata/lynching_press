Sheriff Shot by Negro.
Atlanta, Ga.- Sheriff W. W. Beard
was shot and fatally injured by a negro
he attempted to arrest in Fort Gaines,
Ga., according to a dispatch from that
city this afternoon. The
negro was
suspected of being implicated in the assassination
of the Pius brothers at
last and when
Sheriff Beard approached him to make
an arrest the man opened fire.
A posse is in pursuit of the negro
and many threats are being made that
the lynch law will be applied if he
captured. is
