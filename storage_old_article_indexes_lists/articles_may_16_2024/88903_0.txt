FIVE MEN LYNCHED.
Father, Three Sons and a Companion
Hanged by a Mob.
Bieber, Cal., June 1.- Incomplete details
have been received here of the
lynching of five men at Lookout, Modoc
county, early yesterday morning. The
lynched men were Calvin Hall, aged 72
years, his three half- breed sons, Frank,
James and Martin, aged 26, 19 and 16
and B. D. Yatis, aged 27.,
As reported here, the men lynched had
been suspected of petty stealing for
some time. Last Saturday they were
arrested for stealing barb- wire, pieces
of harness and some hay forks.
