CENTRALIA, Wash., Nov. 12.- A
mob lynched Brick Smith, secretary
of the I. W. W. organization here,
who killed Dale Hubbard, a Cen
tralia lumber man, one of the former
soldiers who were wounded, who died
this morning as the result of the
shooting yesterday, when the reds
fired on the Armistics parade from
the shelter of roofs.
Nineteen reds have been placed in
jail. F, National Guards,
from Tacoma Company, forms a street patrol.
