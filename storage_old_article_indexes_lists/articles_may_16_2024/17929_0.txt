TWO NEGROES LYNCHED.
Taken From an Officer and Hanged- Had
Beaten a White Man.
SILVER CITY, Miss., Sept. 3.- Arthur and
Talcum Woodard, negro brothers, were
taken from a train last night by a masked
mob and hanged to a tree half a mile out
of town ..
They were in custody of City Marshal
W. B. Jackson of Silver City, who was
carrying them to the county farm to serve
sentences of sixty days, imposed for assaulting
Andrew White with a club and
drawing pistol on Sam Dugan, White's
neighbor, a who came to his assistence.
