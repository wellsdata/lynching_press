Alleged Negro Murderer Captured
New Orleans, August 23.- Walter Cade,
the alleged negro slayer of Policeman
Charles Smith at Slidell, La., after eluding
a posses bent upon lynching was arrested
here today. Cade was identified
by a negro who was sent here by the
Slidell authorities. Because of the feeling
against the negro at Slidell, he will be
kept in the parish prison here for severa
days. Officer Smith was shot down at a
dance last Monday night. Smith
negro
is said to have reprimanded Cade, where-
the negro extinguished the lights
and upon shot the officer down.
