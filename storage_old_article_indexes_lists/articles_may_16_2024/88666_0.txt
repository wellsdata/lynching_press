Not to Send Representative
Montgomery, January 4 .-( Special) -Em-
mett G. Thigpen, attorney general, does
not believe it will be necessary for his
department to have a representative at
Tuscumbia when the Colbert county
grand jury and court meet next Monday.
It had been reported in the city the attorney
general would send a representative
into Colbert county to assist the
solicitor in preparing cases against the
men who are under indictment for assisting
in the lynching of two
there recently. negroes
