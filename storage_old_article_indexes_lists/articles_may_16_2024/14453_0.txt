Another Negro Lynched.
LOUISVILLE( Ky.), April 29.- A special
to the Courier- Journal from Greenville,
Ala., says: The sixth negro was lynched
in Butler County yesterday for the murder
of young Watts Murphy. Sheriff
Bargaines found his body hanging to a
tree in the neighborhood where the other
five men were lynched last Sunday. He
appeared to have been dead for at least a
day. The last victim is believed to have
been the man who struck the blow that
killed Murphy. His name unknown
