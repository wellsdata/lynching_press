Chicago Negro Is
Lynched in Missouri
Moberly, Mo., Nov. 17.- This city
remained quiet during the early part
of today following the lynching of a
negro here yesterday morning. The
three negroes who escaped from the
mob at the time of the lynching have
been recaptured, but their place of
incarceration has been made a secret
, which the sheriff refuses to disclose
, even though all signs of mob
spirit have disappeared.
The slain negro is believed to be
George Adams, 18 years, of 2938 Federal
street, Chicago
