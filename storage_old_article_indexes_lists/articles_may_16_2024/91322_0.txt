that ever disgraced this State was perpetrated
in Rutherfordton. In the midst of a
drenching rain, near midaight, a band of men
in disguise, numbering about sixty, entered the
village, proceeded to the residence of J. M. Justice
, Esq., Republican member of the Legislature
from that county, broke open his door
with axes, and carried him out to the suburbs
