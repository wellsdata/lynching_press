HANGING SCENE. A most disgraceful scene
was enacted in Gochland county, Va., on Friday,
28th ult., on the occasion of the execution there
of two colored men. Under instructions from
the court in which the culprits were convicted,
the sheriff was about to perform the duties within
the jail enclosure: but the crowd that had assembled
without to witness the execution were
determined not to be disappointed, and, by violent
threats, compelled the sheriff to comply with
their wishes. The doomed men were accordingly
executed on the jail wall, in presence of
