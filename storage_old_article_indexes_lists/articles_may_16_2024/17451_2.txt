De Lome Hanged In Effigy.
TROY, N. Y., March 2.- Spain's former
minister to the United States.
Senor Dupuy de Lome, was hanged in
effigy in this city, and the police had to
be called upon to disperse a crowd of
about 100 people who had assembled to
hurl harsh epithets at the object of
their scorn. Placards on the effigy were
thus inscribed:" Hang De Lome. V.
H. R.," and" Sucker De Lome. V. H. R.
club."
The police took the effigy and
carried it to the police amid
cries of derision. station
