" Doc" Jones Lynched.

alias" Doc" Jones, a negro and a self-
confessed murderer and moonshiner, was
lynched in the court yard here last midnight
by a mob composed of prominent
citizens of this town and surounding country
. The direct cause of the lynching was
the murder of a merchant near here
