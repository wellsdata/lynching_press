The Lynch Law.
A special session of Judge Lynch's Court
was lately held at Clinton, la. The account
states that a young man attacked a
little girl in the woods, and frightened her
by his lewd attempts. The girl's father
and about forty friends, took the fellow
to the the river, and hung him by the hands
across
a tree, and gave him one
hundred lashes with hickory withes, raising
large blisters him, besides drawing
blood, and then on bade him travel.
