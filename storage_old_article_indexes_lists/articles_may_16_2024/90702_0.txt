9 Colored Ex- Soldiers
Lynched in 1919.
The National Association for the
Advancement of Colored People, 70
Fifth Avenue, New York, today published
a statement showing that 9
colored ex- soldiers had been lynched
in the United State during 1919. Of
the nine, two were burned to death,
two were hanged, four were shot and
one was beaten.

shot to death because he did not turn
out of the road soon enough for pass-
