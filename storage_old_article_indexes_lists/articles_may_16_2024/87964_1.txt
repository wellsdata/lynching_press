Gov. Cummins Offers$ 500 for Arrests
and Convictions.
Des Moines, Jan. 15.- Declaring that
he believes every man who assists in
a lynching is guilty of murder and
should be punished as much as any
other man who commits murder, Gov.
A. B. Cummins offered a reward of
$ 500 for the arrest and conviction of
any man or woman who was implicated
in the lynching of James
