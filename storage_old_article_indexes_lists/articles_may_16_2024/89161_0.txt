NEGROES SPIRITED AWAY.
Mob Tries to Lynch Them for Murderous
Assault.
Seneca, Kan., September 12.- Jim and
Ollie Johnson, negroes, fatally stabbe
Sam Murphy, a white man, at Centralia,
near here, last night and an angry mob
secured a rope and there were cries of
" Lynch them; hang them!"
The negroes were spirited out of town
and brought safely to the Seneca jail.
The negroes had attacked a white boy
and when interferred they stabbed
and beat Murphy him.
