Father's Vengence.
MEMPHIS, Aug. 13.- William Nersh-
bred, a negro charged with committing
a criminal assault upon an 8- year- old
girl near Rossville, Tenn., was taken
from a deputy sheriff by S. A. Crook,
the father of the child, and shot to death.
A mob was gathering to lynch Nersh-
bred when Mr. Crook arrived on the
and empitied both barrels of a
scene shotgun into the negro's body.
