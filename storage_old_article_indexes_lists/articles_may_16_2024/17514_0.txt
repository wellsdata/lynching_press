A Fierce Battle.
LAREDO, Nov. 8.- News reached the
commander of Mexican troops at Nuevo
Laredo of serious trouble at Mier, Mexico
. Last Sunday a merchant named
Guteries was killed by two customs guards.
Citizens raised a mob and lynched the
guards. A body of forty or fifty Mexi-
cans troops and fifteen or twenty customs
guards came upon the scene and a battle
ensued in which ten or fifteen were killed
