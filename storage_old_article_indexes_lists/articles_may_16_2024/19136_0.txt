NEGRO IS LYNCHED
Rawlings, Wye., Oct. 2.- Frank Wigfall, a negro ex- conviet, was. taken
from his cell in the state penitentiary this morning, where he had been
placed for safe keeping, and lynched by a party of convicts who had previously
overpowered the keepers. Wig fall had assaulted an aged white woman
in Rawlins and had been placed in the penitentiary by the posse which
