Negro Preacher Lynched.
" Parson" Wallace, a nergo preacher
, was lynched by a mob at Greenwood
, Miss., which sought revenge for
the serious wounding of Patrolman J.
W. Slack, by the negro. The negro
was badly wounded in his fight with
the officer and when he fell into the
hands of the mob it was necessary to
drag him to the telegraph pole
which he was hanged. to
