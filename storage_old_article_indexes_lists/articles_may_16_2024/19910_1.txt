Mardered by Texas Bill.
ST. LOUIS, August 13.- A dispatch from$
Hutchinson, Ke, says Mannard Emerson, but
generally known as Texas Bill, shot and
killed a baker named Denger, about noon
yesterday. Emerson was arrested, and about
midnight last night sixty masked men went
to the jail, hammered down the doors, and
were just about taking the murderer out to
hang him, when the sheriff with a large posse
arrived and drove them off. Emerson is
known ss a desperado, and is said to have
killed several men, and his act yesterday is
characterized cold- blooded and unprovoked
