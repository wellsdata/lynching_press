A Lynching Bee.
NASHVILLE, Tenn., May 25.- A special
to the American from Franklin, Ky ..
says:" At.10 o'clock to- night, a mob of
seventy- five or a hundred men quietly
took Wes Hicks and Jerry Taylor, who
were charged with burning James
Wheeler's house and barn some time
ago, placed them on horses and s, arted
for the country. It is supposed they
will be hanging on a limb in the
morning found."
