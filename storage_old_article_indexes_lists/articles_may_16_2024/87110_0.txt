An infuriated mob hung and
burned three negroes in Spring-
field, Mo., one day last week.
Two of them for assaulting a
white woman and the other was
in jail for murder. The lady
that was assaulted says that they
were not the negroes that assault-
ed her and there were doubts as
to the guilt of the one charged
with murder. This only shows
that race prejudice is noted
