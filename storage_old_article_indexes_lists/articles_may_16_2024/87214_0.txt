A Murderer Lynched.
CHICAGO, March 1, 1876.
A special from Kuoxville, Tennessee,
states that the murderer Burn Beg.
garly was taken from his cell Sun
day night, and hanged. Fifty unmasked
men rode up to the jail and
demanded the keys, which were re
fused. The men placed a revolver at
the jailor's head, and his wife, who
thought that her husband was going to
be killed, delivered up the keys. Beggarly
was taken from his cell, placed
on a horse, and the mob galloped away.
