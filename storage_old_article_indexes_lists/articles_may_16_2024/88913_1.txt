Wilmington, Del., June 23.- All is
quiet in this community today, following
the horrible lynching of George
White, Helen Bishop's confessed slayer,
early this mining. Of a mob of nearly
5,000, identity of only one was known.
That person is little Peter Smith, aged
12, who fell sericisly wounded when
the workhouse guards fired a volley into
the ranks of the attacking mob.
