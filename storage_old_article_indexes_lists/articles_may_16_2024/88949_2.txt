Bloomington Mob Disappointed.
Bloomington, Ind., Oct. 8.- Notwith-
standing Edward Jones, a negro, who
shot and killed Alfred Stephens, a
, lunch wagon man, Saturday night, was
spirited from Bloomington shortly
after midnight and safely placed in
the Jeffersonville penitentiary, a mob
that clamored for a lynching did not
disperse till after daylight. Mutterings
against the blacks in general are
still heard. The crowd did not dispense
till committees had thoroughly
searched two the jail.
