Judge Curtis Will
Hold Special Session
Colbert Circuit Court
Montgomery, January 4 .-( Special.)
Judge J. J. Curtis, circuit judge of the
Jasper district, late Saturday was designated
by the chief justice, John C. An-
derson, of the supreme court, to conduct
the special session of the Colbert circuit
court at Tuscumbia. The special ses-
sion convenes Monday. The court is
called to conduct a searching probe of
the lynching of two negroes in Colbert
county, and also to try several prominent
persons who are under indictment
for either assisting in the lynchings or
being indirectly connected with them.
