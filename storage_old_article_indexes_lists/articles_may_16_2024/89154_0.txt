THINKS HE KNOWS
MEMBERS OF MOB
Chattanooga, Tenn., June 11.- There was
but one session of the second day's hearing
of the contempt case agafnst Sheriff
J. F. Shipp. While none of the members
of the alleged.mob that hanged Ed John-
son was by any means positively identified
, A. J. Ware, former justice of the
peace, testified that he thought he recognized
several of them at the time. Mr.
Ware was positive in stating that he
would not swear that these actually were
the men, but that was merely his impression
at the time. Luther Williams, Nick
Nolan and" Sheenie Warner were the
three defendants that" he thought he saw.
