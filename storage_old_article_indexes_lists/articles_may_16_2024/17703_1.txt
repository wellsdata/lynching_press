Confessed Murderer Lynched.
Birmingham, Ala., Aug. 2.- With a rope
around his neck and death before him,
Charley Bentley, a negro, confessed to the
murder of Jim Vann, alias Williams, a
white man, and was hanged by a mob in
Leeds, St. Clair County, Ala., to- day.
