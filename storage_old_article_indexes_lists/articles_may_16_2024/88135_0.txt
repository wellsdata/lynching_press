NEGRO LYNCHED FOR
ASSAULTING WHITE WOMAN
Shreveport, La., Aug. 7.- Accused of
criminal assault upon the wife of
well known attorney of Bastrop, La.,
Bubber Hall, a young negro was
lynched at Bastrop this afternoon
about 4:30 o'clock, according to word
received here,
The negro was taken from Sheriff
B. H. Spear while enroute to the
woman's home to be identified. He
was carried to the Bastrop Merrouge
roadside where he was strung up
