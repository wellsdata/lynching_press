TO PROBE LYNCHING
OF VAN ASHMORE
PHOENIX, Ariz., May, 11.-
Attorney General Wiley Jones
advised County Attorneys Laney
and Richardson of Maricopa and
Pinal counties that he expected
them to act promptly to investigate
the violation of the law in
the lynching of Starr Daley,
alias Ashmore, Sunday morning.
The lynching occurred in Pinal
county but the mob came from
Maricopa, county, it is said.
