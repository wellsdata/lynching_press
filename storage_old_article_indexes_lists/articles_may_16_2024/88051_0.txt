THOUGHTFUL OF FRIENDS.
Mob Held Negro Five Hours for Arrival
of Friends.
Pensacola, Fla., July 7.- So that a
mob could arrive from Bombay, 100
miles distant, and take part in the
lynching of an unidentified negro,
the victim was held for five hours
by a mob at Milton, near here, this
morning before he was strung up
to a telegraph pole and his body
riddled with bullets.
