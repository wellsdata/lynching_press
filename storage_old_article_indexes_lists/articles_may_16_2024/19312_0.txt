The Skeleton of a Missing Negro Found
Hanging to a Tree.
CALERA, Ala., April 7.- Near Shelby
Springs this morning an old colored woman
found the skeleton of a man suspended from
the limb of a tree by a rope around the neck.
The skeleton was later in the day identified by
the clothing as that of Cass Avery, a negro,
who has been missing since last January. The
Coroner impanelled a jury, and began an investigation
. which will be continued to- morrow
, there being evidences of foul play. It is
that the negro was lynched, but for
supposed what crime is unknown.
