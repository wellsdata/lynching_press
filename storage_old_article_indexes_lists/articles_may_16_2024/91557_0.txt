HANGED, AND SHOT RIDDLED
NEGRO STOLEN FROM JAIL,
LAWRENCEVILLE, Ga., April 7.-
Shortly after midnight a mob of 200
masked men stormed jail here and
got a negro, Charles the Hale, arrested
earlier in the night for assaulting Mrs.
C. C. Williams.
