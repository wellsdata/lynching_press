MOB LYNCHES A NEGRO
WHILE SOLDIERS TALK
Dallas, Tex., October 1.- A special to
the News from Marshall, Tex., says:
Tonight a mob of several hundred men
forced their way through the brick wall
of the jail with the aid of a telephone
pole and with sledge hammers and crowbars
, took out Walter Davis, a negro,
and marched him to the west side of
town, where he was hanged to a willow
tree.
