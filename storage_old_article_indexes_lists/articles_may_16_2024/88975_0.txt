NEGRO IS LYNCHED IN ALABAMA
Hanged to Tree Previously Used in
Execution of Two Blacks.
Pritchard Station, Ala., Sept. 23.-
was lynched
Mose Dossett, a negro,
here for an attempted criminal assault
upon Mrs. J. Breeder, a white woman,
9 years old. Dossett was hanged from
the same tree from which two negroes,
Jim Robinson and Will Thompson
lynched a year ago for assault
were upon small white girls.
