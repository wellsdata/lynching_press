A telegram from Tupelo, Miss.,
60 miles southeast of Memphis, says
that yesterday morning at 2 o'clock a
young negro about 18 years old was
taken from jail by a mob and lynched
in front of the courthouse. The mob
went to the sheriff's home, overpowered
him with shotguns, marched
him to the jail and forced the keys
from him. They wore masks and
their identity is unknown. The
prisoner attempted to rape a white
lady at Plantersville two weeks ago
A dispatch.
