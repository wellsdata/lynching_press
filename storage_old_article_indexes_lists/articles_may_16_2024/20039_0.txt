Murdered His Divorced Wife.
MORRIS, Ills., Oct. 7.- Near Waupon
sea station, ten miles from here, Mon
day, W. L. Barrett, a Chicago bookkeeper
, shot and killed his wife, who
had separated from him, and then set
fire to the residence and attempted to
drown himself in a small stream. Failing
in this he sent a bullet through his
body, but the wound is not fatal and
murderer may yet be lynched. The
fire the was extinguished by the neighbors,
