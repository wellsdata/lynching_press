A Mississippi Negro Lynched.
William Stern, alias William John-
son, colored, was mobbed near Rose-
meath, Miss. He attempted to murder
Mr. John Hord, a prominent
planter, last May, striking him in the
head with a nail- puller. He made his
escape and was recently captured in
Alabama by Mr. Hord, and was taken
to Rosemeath, the scene of his crime,
where a number of determined
hanged him in broad daylight. citizens
