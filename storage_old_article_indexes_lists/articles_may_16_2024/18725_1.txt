Atlanta, Ga.- According to dispatches
received in this city today Geo. Thomas,
the negro who shot Sheriff W. W. Beard
at Fort Gaines, Ga., yesterday, was
lynched by a mob in the Central of Georgia
railroad station after the shooting.
First reports said Thomas was killed by
the Sheriff, but the news dispatches to
day bring out the fact that he escaped
after shooting the sheriff and took refuge
in the railroad station.
Here a crowd of infuriated citizens located
him and when the police arrived
found Thomas' body literally rid
they dled with bullets.
