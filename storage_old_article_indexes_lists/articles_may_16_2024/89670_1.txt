Tramp Saved from Lynching.
BATAVIA, O., June 9.- The quiet little
village of Williamsburg was thrown into' a
state of excitement to- day by an attempt
made by a tramp to assault Mrs. Emma
Curtis. She is in a very critical condition.
A posse was immediately organized and
started in pursuit of the tramp. He was
captured at Budd's brickyard and Constable
Bucker had all he could do to prevent
the frenzied mob from lynching him. The
tramp had to be removed secretly to
