Bold Warning to Assaulters Pasted
Upon His Body.
Calhoun, La., Aug. 27. Alfred
Schaufneit, a negro, was lynched here
for attacking Miss Olive Chambers.
Schaufneit was capturd by a posse a
few miles out of town and confessed.
The following bold notice was posted
apon the negro's body:
This is a warning to all negroes
who would attempt to force
to the apartments of white women entrance."
