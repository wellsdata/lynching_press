NEGRO LYNCHED BY
AQUEDUCT LABORERS
Colored Man Said to Have
Assaulted 6- Year- Old
Child of Homesteader
BAKERSFIELD, Calif., Sept. 20.-
The lynching of a negro on the desert
thirty miles east of Mojave by men
alleged to be employed by the Los
Angeles acqueduct was reported here
tonight.
It is said that the negro assaulted
the six year old child of a woman
who is homesteading a tract of land
near the aqueduct camp in Jawbone
Canyon. The men formed a posse
which pursued the negro and surrounded
him in a gulch several miles
distant from the scene of the alleged
crime. They riddled his body with
bullets.
The deputy coroner and a deputy
sheriff left tonight in an automobile
to make here an investigation.
