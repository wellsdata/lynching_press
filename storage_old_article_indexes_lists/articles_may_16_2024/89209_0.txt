Nebraska Murderer Lynched.
Omaha, Neb., Aug. 26.- Loris Hig-
gins, who murdered W. L. Copple and
wife, farmers near Rosalie, Neb., May
12, was lynched at Bancroft. Sheriff
Young, of Thurston county, came to
Omaha at night to get his prisoner,
who had been in the Douglas county
jail here since his capture and took
him to Bancroft on a train, which arrived
there about 8 o'clock in
