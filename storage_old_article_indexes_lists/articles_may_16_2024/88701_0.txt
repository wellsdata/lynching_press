NEGRO LYNCHED IN TEXAS.
Mob from Carthage Hangs Him to a
Telephone Pole in Public Square.
Longview, Tex., April 26.- News reached
here today of the lynching at Carthage
last night of a negro named Hensley
Johnson, who criminally assaulted a 4-
year- old white girl of that place last
Friday night. The negro was captured
by the officers of Panola county and
placed in jail, but was released on bond
yesterday.
He left for the country, where he was
captured last night by a mob from Carth-
age and hanged on the public square,
where his body was found this morning
suspended to a telephone pole. The mob
was quiet and the people of Carthage
nothing of the affair until the
knew body was found this morning.
