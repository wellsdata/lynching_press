Mob Just Faded Away.
Jamestown, N. D., Oct. 19.- An angry
mob made an attempt to lynch E.
E. Travis, who is alleged to have shot
and killed Gottlieb Anhern, a 16- year-
old boy whom he suspected of stealing
a bundle of lath. The sheriff gave it
out straight that he would protect the
prisoner at all hazards, and the
faded away mob
