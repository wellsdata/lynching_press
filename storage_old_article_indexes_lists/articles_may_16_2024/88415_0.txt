LYNCHED FOR MURDER.
NATCHEZ, Miss., Sept. 13.- There was a
murder and lynching in the lower portion
of Concordia parish last Monday. A negro
named Bob Williams, employed as a fore.
man on the Excelsior place, started after
two of the hands on the place who had absconded
. He overtook them and in an
altercation that followed Williams shot
and killed one of the parties, whose name
is not known. Williams was arrested, and
while still in the custody of the officers he
was taken by a mub and hanged, after be.
ing brutally maltreated. The affair created
much excitement in the lower part of the
parish, but news it only reached this city
today. All the parties of were negroes.
