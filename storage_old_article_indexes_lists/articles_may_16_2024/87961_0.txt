NEGRO SAILOR NEARLY LYNCHED.
Starts Fight After Being Thrown Out of
California Dance Hall.
REDONDO, Cal., Nov. 25 .-- There was
an incipient race riot among the sailors
of Admiral Dayton's fleet that nearly
terminated in a lynching. A negro sailor
named Epps presented himself at a local
hall where a dance was in progress. He
was refused admission and started a
fight. In leaving the place Epps knocked
down and seriously injured a white
woman on the sidewalk. Immediately
the negro was seized by a number of
sailors, and, after being given a severe
beating. was escorted up the street with
shouts of" Lynch him!"
rescued Epps and took him
to Policemen jail.
