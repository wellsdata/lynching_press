THREE MORE LYNCHED.
A Telephone Message From Carrolton Declares
This Report Untrue.
( By the Associated Press.)
New Orleans, La., August 2.- Three
more negroes were lynched at Carroll
ton, Miss., this morning.
THE REPORT DECLARED FALSE.
Memphis, Tenn., August -A telephone
message from Carrollton, Miss., at 8:50
p. m., states that the reported lynching
of three negroes there this morning is
untrue. Three negroes were lynched there
yesterday but there has been
no disturbance afternoon of any kind since then.
