NEGROES ARE INDICTED.
Men Whom Mob Sought to Lynch
Held for Murder. Are
CHESTERTOWN, Mo., Dec. 30.-
Indictments charging murder in the
first degree were returned today by
the Kent County Grand Jury against
Norman Mabel and James Paraway,
negroes, for the killing of James R.
Coleman, a well- to- do farmer, last
Tuesday night.
Both the negroes had confessed
their guilt, robbery being the motive
for the crime. Chief Judge Constable
instructed the jury to investigate the
storming of the jail on Saturday
night a mob in an endeavor to
lynch by the negroes.
