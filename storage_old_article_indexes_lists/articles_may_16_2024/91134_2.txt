Dumas, Ark ,, Sept. 27.- A mob
of 100 men, overpowering those in
charge, broke into the county jail
here and took Charles Malpass, a
white man, who married a colored
woman, to a water tank and lynched
him today. The lynching followed
a battle late yesterday at
the home of Malpass, in which
Sheriff Preston, Deputy Sheriff
Barney and two mulatto sons of
Malpass were killed and Malpass
wounded. The wife of Malpass
and younger son escaped during
the his fighting.
