THAT TYLER LYNCHING.
Jackson County Grand Jury Said to
Be Investigating It.
Special to the Indianapolis Journal.
SEYMOUR, Ind., Nov. 27.- The excitement
which attended the Tyler lynching trial at
Indianapolis last week is about to be transferred
to this county. The grand jury is
now in session in Jackson county, and it is
reported on good authority that it is investigating
the charges made in the Federal
Court that the mob of lynchers organized in-
this county before proceeding to Scottsburg
to lynch Marion Tyler. If the evidence
given at Indianapolis is true the grand jury
would have the right to investigate the matter
. Another rumor to- night is that the
grand jury returned indictments against two
