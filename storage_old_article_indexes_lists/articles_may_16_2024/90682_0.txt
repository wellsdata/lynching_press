A LOUISIANA INCIDENT.
Negro Lynched in Conformity With
the Custom.
Calhoun, La., Aug. 26.- Alfred
Schaufniet, negro, was lynched here
today by a mob for attempting to assault
Miss Olive Chambers, early this
morning. Schaufniet was frightened
by screams of his intended victim and
fled, but was captured by a posse. After
confessing his guilt he was hanged
| to a telegraph pole.
