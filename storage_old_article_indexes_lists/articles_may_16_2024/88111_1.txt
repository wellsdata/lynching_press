LYNCHED BY NEGROES.
Black Brute Captured by Mob of His
Own Race and Strung Up.
Lake Butler, Fla., July 20.- A negro
tramp named Adams, who assaulted
a negro woman near this place a day
or so ago, was captured Friday night
by a mob of negroes and lynched in
Santa Fe swamp. His body was cut
into shreds by the mob and severely
disfigured. lynching was carried
out by negroes The entirely.
