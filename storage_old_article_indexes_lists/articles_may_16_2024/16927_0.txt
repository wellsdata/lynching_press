Good order in general prevailed both at the Bay
and Mines. Capt. Grayson was robbed on the 5th
of June of$ 15,000 by a Chilian servant, in whom
he reposed confidence. He would undoubtedly be
hung as soon as apprehended. Another robbery of
$ 6000 occurred a few days after. As a general
thing property is respected, evil doers being kept
in check by of Lynch law.
