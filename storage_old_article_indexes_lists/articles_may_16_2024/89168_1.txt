NEGROES ARE HELD
TO GRAND JURY
Selma, October 12 .-( Special.)- Today before
Commissioner E. S. Starr, Norman
Vesketter and Virgil Henderson, two
negroes, were bound over in the sum of
$ 1000 to appear before the United States
grand jury. The negroes were arrested.
in Demopolis a few days ago charged
with sending obscene letters through the
mail to the wife of a well- known citizen.
There were ugly threats of lynching when
they were captured and they were hurried
to Selma. Marshal Monier and several
other witnesses from Demopolis attended
the trial and United States Marshal G.
Dean came up from Mobile. B.
