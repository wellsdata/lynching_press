American Mobs Lynched
4,713 Victims In 64 Years
By Associated Negro Press Associated Negro Press
TUSKEGEE, Ala-Following on the heels of the Monroe,
Ga., lynching, the department of records and research, Tuskegee
Institute, released a statistical record of lynching in the United
States covering the period 1882 to 1946. The record shows that
1,291 whites and 3,422 Negroes were lynching during the 64
year period covered, making a grand total of 4,713 mob vic-
tims.
