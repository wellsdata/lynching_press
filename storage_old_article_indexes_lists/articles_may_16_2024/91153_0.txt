Louisiana, Mo., Sept. 13.- A
new form of mob execution-
drowning -- was the fate met by
Love Rudd, a Negro who was taken
from a constable by a mob from
Clarksville, Mo., several days ago.
This became apparent when Rudd's
body with a big rock tied to the
feet was found in the Mississippi
river tonight. Rudd was taken
from the sheriff after his arrest
