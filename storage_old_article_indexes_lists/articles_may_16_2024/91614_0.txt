A Negro Fiend Lynched,
NASHVILLE, June 23.- A dispatch
to the" Banner" from Double Springs,
Tenn., states that Charles Washing
ton, colored, who recently assaulted
Mrs. Ward near Mine Lick, Putnam
County, was lynched at Mine Lick today
. He was captured near Dayton
and lodged in jail at Cookeville, but
was taken from prison by the mob
and hanged. The negro said he had escaped
from the New Jersey penitentiary
after serving only a short time.
He confessed his. The lynching
was witnessed by guilt 3,000 people.
