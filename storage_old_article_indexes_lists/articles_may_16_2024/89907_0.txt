NEGRO LYNCHED BY MOB
Body Riddled With Bullets by Party
of Fifty Men.
By United Press
MERIDIAN, Miss., July 19 .-- Har.
ry Shelton, young negro, was taken
from jail at Scoeba, 45 miles from
here, by a mob of approximately
fifty unidentified men and swung to
a tree.
The body was riddled with bullets.
The lynching followed an alleged
attepted attack on a white woman.
Shelton was arrested at De Kalb,
but authorities fearing for his safety,
took him to Scoeba for safe
Friday. keeping
