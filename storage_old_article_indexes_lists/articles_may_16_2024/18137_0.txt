Whites and Blacks Crash With Disastrous
Results.
Tulsa, Okla.- Mannford, a town of
6,000 persons, twenty miles from here,
resembles an armed camp, in fear of a
race war following a series of events
in which one white man, Fred A.
Evans, was killed; another, Rolly
Johnson, was seriously injured, one
negro," Ned" Walker, was lynched
and another seriously injured.,
