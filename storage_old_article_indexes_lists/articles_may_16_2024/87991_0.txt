Mob Listens to His Speech but insists
on Lynching Negro.
Columbia, S. C., Aug. 17.- Bob Da-
Fis, the negro who on Monday murderously
assaulted Miss Jennie Brooks
with intent to commit assault and
who afterwards outraged a fourteen-
year- old negro girl, was lynched at
Greenwood about 7:30 p. m. A
Woman fired the first shot at Davis negro.
