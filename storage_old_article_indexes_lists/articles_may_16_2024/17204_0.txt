Lynch law is a dangerous experiment in all
neighborhoods, and often leads to serious consequences
, but if any people in the world are justifiable
in resorting to it, they are those whose
slaves are being corrupted and ruined by cross
road groggeries, where stolen goods are paid for in
bad whiskey, and where the negro is taught to
believe that to rob his owner is a virtue. We
have heard of unprincipled men amassing fortunes
] by traffic with slaves, but if any of them
can succeed in doing so on Church Hill, they may
expect to travel a rough road whilst carrying
their hazardous enterprise. out
