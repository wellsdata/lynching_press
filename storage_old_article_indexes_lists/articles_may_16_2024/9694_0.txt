Negro Lynched on Suspicion.
ALBANY, Ga., April 11.- Henry Smith-
ers, a Negro, was lynched near Donald-
sonville, in Miller county. He was
hanged to a tree and his body riddled
with bullets He was arrested on suspicion
of entering a room occupied by
four ladies Their screams caused him
fly. He was captured and on the
to way to jail an armed mob lynched him.
