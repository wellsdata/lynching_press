MISSOURI LYNCHING
CLARK PREVENTS
BOWLING GREEN, Sept. 2-
Champ Clark, speaker of the house
of representatives, prevented B
lynching near here last night, it
was learned today. Aroused from
his bed by the chugging of six automobiles
carrying farmers from
Clarksville, bent on lynching Har
rison Rose, negro, he exhorted
the men to return a to their homes.
