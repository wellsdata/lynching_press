( Special to The Daily Times)
FAIRBANKS. Sept. 9.- Sam Cote,
a naturalized foreigner and a farmer
at Hot Springs, evidently insane,
yesterday shot and killed Deputy
United States Marshal J. A. Anders,
who was trying to serve a peace
I warrant on him.
A posse of eight Hot Springs men
started in pursuit of Cote after the
shooting, and Cote was killed when
he refused to surrender to the posse.
Anders was considered one of the
best deputies on the force. He
