TWO NEGROES LYNCHED
[ Republican A. P. Leased Wire]
ANDERSON, S. C., Jan. 3.-
Johnson of Hart county, Georgia Sheriff, tele-
phoned here late today that two neg-
roes were killed on the South Carolina
side of the Savannah river, ene of
them being shot down and the other
bound by a mob, taken into a wood and
there lynched. The attack on the neg-
roes was said to have result from one
of them speaking to a white
who passed on a country road woman in a
buggy
