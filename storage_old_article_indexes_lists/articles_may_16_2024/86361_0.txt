NEGRO LYNCHED FOR FIGHTING.
JENNINGS, La., Aug. 27.- James
Comeaux, a negro was lynched here
early today. He was taken from
his cell shortly after midnight, shot
to death and
his body left lying
at the jail door where it was found
later by a night watchman.
Comeaux had been arrested for assaulting
A. W. Joseph, an Italian
merchant, who had accidentally
swept dirt on the negro's shoes
while he was passing the Italian's
store. Joseph's relatives have been
arrested in connection the
