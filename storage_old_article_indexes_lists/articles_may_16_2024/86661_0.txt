LYNCHING FEARED
IN A TOWN OF
ILLINOIS
A CLASH EXPECTED AT VIENNA.
GOVERNOR DENEEN GIVES MILITARY
ORDERS TO PREVENT
TROUBLE AT ANY COST.
Springfield, Ill., Jan. 12.- Everything
is quiet at Vienna this afternoon, but
a clash is feared between the military
and mob who are bent on lynching
the murderers of a prominent
citizen of this place.
The mobs formed last night to
avenge the murder of Allen Clark
( white) by three negroes on a passenger
train near New Burnside, have
arrived in Vienna, but troops are on
the scene, prepared to prevent violence
, which is expected to break out
at any time.
Governor Deneen has given the
commanding officer orders prevent
a lynching, no matter at what to cost.
