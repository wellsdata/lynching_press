COATESVILLE HOSPITAL.
Infuriated beyond measure and filled with the spirit of lynch law, a
screaming, howling mob of more than one thousand men and boys fought
their way into the Coatesville( Pa.) Hospital last Sunday, and, overpowering
the police and physicians on guard, dragged wounded Zachariah Walker, a
negro, who killed a special watchman, Edgar Rice, along with his cot to a
farm quarter of a mile distant and burned him to death on a pile of fence
