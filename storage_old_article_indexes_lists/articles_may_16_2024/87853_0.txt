The shots apparently came from the roofs of buildings
standing near a hall occupied by the I. W. W. organization
. Momentarily stunned by the unexpectedness
of the attack, the citizens quickly recovered and a mob of
several thousand persons rushed the I. W. W. hall and
into the nearby buildings. Sixteen radicals were arrested
and rifles and revolvers were taken from them. All
of the weapons had apparently been freshly fired. The
mob tried to lynch the ringleaders and did secure one of
