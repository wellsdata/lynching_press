Lynching Feared.
WOODLAWN, Wn., Oct. 12 .-- Victor
Beebe, a young man, was killed Friday
night by a man named Day in a quarrel
about some cows which had destroyed
Beebe's corn. Day fled after the shooting
but was captured next day and a
mob was organized to lynch him. He
was taken Kalama but it is feared
that he will to yet be lynched.
