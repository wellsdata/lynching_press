The Fate of a
Brute Negro in Kentucky.
PARIS, Ky., Feb 11.- George Carter, a negro who assaulted the
wife of W. Ebord was taken from jail at 2 o'clock this morning. by a
mob and hanged at the entrance of the courthouse.
No word was spoken during the entire affair and only the participants
knew what was taken place. After the lynching the mob
quietly dispersed having first pinned a placard on the negro's breast
reading:
" This will be the fate of all negroes who assault white women.
