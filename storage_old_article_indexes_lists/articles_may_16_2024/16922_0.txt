A GEORGIA HANGING.
The Victim a Negro Charged With
Assault.
Laurenceville, Ga., April 7.- Shortly
after midnight a mob of 200 hundred
masked men stormed the jail here and
secured a negro, Charles Hale, arrested
earlier in the night for assaulting Mrs.
C.C. Williams. The mob hanged the
negro a tree and riddled his body
