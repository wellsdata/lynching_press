Negro Taken Away
From Second Mob
Winchester, Va., May 25.Wm. H.
Turner, the negro who has confessed
to killing Thomas H. Moore, of Alex-
andria, Va., and attacking Miss Pearl
Clarke, Mr. Moore's fiancee, near
Washington, D. C., last Saturday
night, was brought here today to escape
a second mob seeking to lynch
him.
Turner was moved from Alexandria
yesterday to Leesburg, Va., for safekeeping
, but mob gathered and the
negro was away.
