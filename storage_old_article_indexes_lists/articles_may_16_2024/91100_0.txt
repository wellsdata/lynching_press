Calhoun, La., Aug. 26.- Alfred
Schaufneit, a Negro, was lynched
here today by a mob for attempting
to assault Miss Olive Chambers
early this morning. Schaufneit
was frightened by the screams of
his intended victim and fled, but
was captured by a posse. After
confessing his guilt he was hanged
to a telegraph pole. The following
notice was posted upon the
Negro's body:" This is a warning
to all Negroes who would attempt
