Wounded Negro Lynched.
FULTON, Ky., May 10.- One of the three
negroes implicated in the cutting of City
Marshal Walker here Saturday, was arrested
by the telegraph operator at Arling-
ton, Ky., a few miles north of Fulton, this
morning. In attempting to arrest the negro
the operator shot him just above the left
lung. The wounded man was brought to Ful-
