DARLINGTON, Wis., Sept. 21.- At noon
today an infuriated mob took from the
county jail Anton Seiboldt, who brutally
murdered James Meighan last
Wednesday, and hanged him to a tree
in the courthouse yard. Seiboldt was a
desperate character. The city is in a
state of excitement, and the principal
streets are crowded with people from
surrounding country. Sieboldt was
a the hand on Meighan's farm.
