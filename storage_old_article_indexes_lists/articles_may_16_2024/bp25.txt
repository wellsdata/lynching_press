Governor Offers $400
for Lynchers' Arrest |
Raleigh, N. C., July 23.-Governor
Bickett has offered a reward of $400
each for tho apprehension of members)
of the mob which lynched Ed Roach
near Roxboro two weeks ago. Roach
was accused of attucking a white girl.
It later developed that he was inno-
cent of the crime and that the girl
had been attacked by a man of her
race. A white contractor for whom
Roach worked declared that the latter
was busily engaged at the time the at-
tack is alleged to have been commit-
ted. It is said that the sheriff is well
tequainted with some of the men who
took part in the lynching, but as yet
has failed to make an arrest.
