( By Associated Press.)
Augusta, Ga., August 15.- Two ne-
groes in jail at Statesboro, charged
with murdering the Hodges family,
are said to have confessed, implicating
thirteen others. A report by train is
that a mob this morning lynched the
entire thirteen. Wires from States-
boro are cut and efforts are being
made to confirm the report.
Atlanta, August 15.- Governor Ter-
rell said at noon today that he had
not heard of any lynching at States-
boro. The wires that were cut were
repaired before noon There are
a thousand soldiers at. Statesboro.
