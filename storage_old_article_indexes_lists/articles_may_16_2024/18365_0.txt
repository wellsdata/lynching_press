CHASED CRIMINAL BY STEAM.
Locomotive Used to Catch a Murderer
, Who May Be Lynched.
CHILLICOTHE, Ohio, May 23.- At 3 p.
m. yesterday, near Austin Station, Ethel
Long, thirteen years old, went to a spring
for a bucket of water, when she was assaulted
by a negro. The negro beat her
head in with a sharp instrument, after
which he cut her throat.
The crime was discovered soon after its
commission. A crowd started
in pursuit
of the negro. An officer from Sabina was
passing at the time. He mounted a loco
motive, gave pursuit, and succeeded
