Negro Is Lynched By
North Carolina Mob
By Associated Press
Franklin, N. C., December 27.- A negro
named Green, charged with the murder
here today of R. M. Brown, was lynched
near here by a mob tonight which took
him from officers en route to Raleigh in
an automobile, where it was intended to
hold the negro for safe keeping. The
negro was taken from the officers only
after a fight between deputies and members
of the mob, and during the sruggle
Green broke away but was recaptured.
A rope was tied around the negro's
neck and he was dragged two miles behind
an automobile, then hanged to
tree. a
