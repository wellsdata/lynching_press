BARBECUE IN PROSPECT.
Negro Vagabond Pursued by Population
for Shooting a White Girl.
Stephenville, Tex., May 25.- Miss
Bird, a young lady living a few miles
north of town, while coming to town,
was accosted by a negro. Miss Bird
did not stop, and the negro fired at
her, producing a flesh wound in her
arm.
Since the news was reported every
man in town who could muster a gun
has been scouting for the criminal,
and if he is found the consequences
will be serious. Excitement is
at present. high
