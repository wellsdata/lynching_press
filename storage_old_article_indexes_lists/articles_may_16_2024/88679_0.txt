NEGRO LYNCHED IN ARKANSAS.
Assaulted Two Negro Women and
Pays the Usual Penalty.
Little Rock, Ark .. March 10.- A Gazette
special from Forman, Little River
county, says:
About 3:30 o'clock Sunday afternoon a
negro giving his name as Porid McCoy
assaulted a negress. She was badly cut
about the head and shoulders in the
struggle, but succeeded in getting away.
The negro some time after the assault
met another negro whom he also assaulted
. The negro also cut Mrs. Lemon
dangerously about the head, neck and
shoulders. The
negro was captured
about dark and at 11 o'clock last
was taken out by a mob and lynched night.
