A Murderer Lynehed.
CHEYENNE, WY., May 21.- Two men arrived
here last night from Sabille County, eighty
five miles north, with the body of George Met
calf in their possession. Metcalf, while at
dinner, got into a dispute with William Ma
loney, and the latter shot him dead. Maloney
was arrested and on, the way here was
