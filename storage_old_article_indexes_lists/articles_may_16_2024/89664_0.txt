Arrest in Tyler Lynching Case.
SCOTTSBURG, Ind., Dec. 23.- The first and
long- expected arrest in the Marion Tyler
lynching case nas occurred. Freeman
Moon was placed under arrest on a warrant
charging him with aiding, abetting
and assisting the mob in hanging Marion
Tyler on the morning of Dec. 24 last. The
warrant was issued by Justice of the Peace
Edwin F. Cranford upon an affidavit
sworn out by Deputy Sheriff E. C. Gobin.
The arrest is not generally known here tonight
, and will cause a sensation. Moon
I was in town to- day and retained his lawyers
. It is that other arrests are to
follow immediately said.
