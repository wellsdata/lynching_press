MURDERERS FOUR
MOB TODAY HANGED
[ By Associated Press.]
Ada, Okla., April 19.- At 3 o'clock
this morning a mob of two hundred
men stormed the county jail here and
overpowered the sheriff and lynched
four white men.
The victims of the mob are J. B.
Miller of Fort Worth, charged with
the murder of A. A. Bobbit, a wealthy
cattleman of this section; F. E.
Brown, Jesse West and Joseph Allen,
alleged accomplices. The electric
wires were cut by the mob prior to
hanging and the city was thrown
the into complete darkness.
