MOB IS FORMING.
Governor Heard of Louisiana Attempts
to Prevent Lynching.
Baton Rouge, La., May 16.- Governor
Heard received a telegram from Sheriff
W. N. Traylor at Rayville stating that
a mob was forming to lynch a prisoner.
No mention was made of the prisoner or
crime, but the governor immediately wired
the sheriff to protect the prisoner at
all hazards. He also ordered Captain
Stubb's company of militia Monroe to
go at once by special train to Rayville
to maintain the law and prevent mob
violence and he wired Sheriff Taylor
this action. of
