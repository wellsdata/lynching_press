NEGROES MUST RESIST.
Bishop Alexadner Walters, President
of the Afro- American Council,
addressed a large audience in Jersey
City N. J., a few days ago on the
lynching and burning of Sam
Hose, and the death of Rev.
Strickland by the mob, who was innocent
of committing any crime.
In the course of the Bishop's remarks
he said," This cry we must
protect our Southern women, is
leading us on to another bloody
struggle; it is the Southern white
man's subterfuge to satiate his implacable
hatred for the negro. I have
came to the conclusion that nothing
but manly resistance on the part
of the Afro- Americans themselves
will stop these atrocities.
