TROOPS MAY BE NEEDED.
Capital City Guards May Go to Oxford,
Miss.
Jackson, Miss., January 7.- Governor
Longino has order the Capital City Guard
of this city to hold themselves in readiness
to go to Oxford.
The sheriff there is apprehensive that
a mob will tonight try to lynch Will
Mathis, Orlando Lester, Will Jackson
and Whit Owens, in jail there. Three of
men have recently been convicted
these of the murder of the two Montgomerys.
