Cairo, Ill., March 24 .-( Special.)
The special grand jury that has been
investigating for the last eight days
the work of the mob that attacked
the county jail on the night of Feb.
17, and tried to take out and lynch
the Negro, John Pratt, the purse
snatcher, found indictments against
twelve persons this afternoon, charg
them with forming a mob.
