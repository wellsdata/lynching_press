GEORGIA MOB
LYNCHES BLACK;
SEEKS ANOTHER
Negroes Who Assaulted
Mrs. Elizabeth Kitchens
Feel the Hand of Mob
Rule.
( By The United Press)
Davisboro, Ga., May 19.-
Sheriff's posses and mobs are
hunting John Tarver, negro,
who has been implicated in the
murder of Mrs. Elizabeth Kitchens
, rural mail carrier, yesterday
. The mob last night lynched
Charles Atkins, negro, 15,
for participation in the murder.
The boy made a statement in
which he named Tarver as
