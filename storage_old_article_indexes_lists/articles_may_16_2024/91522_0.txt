TWO NEGROES
ARE LYNCHED AT
COLUMBUS, GA.
Columbus, Ga., Oct. 17- The cause
of race troubles in the county between
here and Buena Vista, Ga.,
which were said to have resulted in
the lynching of two negroes and the
burning of a negro house yesterday,
had not been learned definitely here
early today. The Scene of the
trouble was about seven miles north
of Buena Vista. The house burned
was said to have been that of" Doc"
Smith, a negro accused of intimacy
with a white woman who disappeared,
