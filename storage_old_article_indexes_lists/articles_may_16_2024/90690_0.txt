Mob Lynches Negro
In Wauchula, Florida
[ Republican A. P. Leased Wire]
WAUCHULA, Fla., Feb. 1- The
lynching here last night of Ben Camp-
bell, a negro, was reported today. He
was taken from the city jail by a mob,
hanged to a telephone pole and his
body riddled with bullets. He
was
accused of attempting to attack
