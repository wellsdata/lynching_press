PRIVATE LYNCHING BEE.
Georgia Man Disposes of Two Negroes
Without Ceremony.
Macon, Ga., Oct. 24.- It was learned
here today that two negroes James
,
Grier and James Colloway, were
lynched by a white farmer at Liberty
Hill. While out hunting they shot
recklessly into farmer's house,
frightening a young the white woman.
