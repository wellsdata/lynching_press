MEMPHIS, Sept. 2- Criminal Court
Judge Cooper is determined to punish
the mob that lynched the six alleged
negro incendiaries near Millington.
From facts brought out today it appears
that Detective Richardson knew
an attempt would be made to lynch
the prisoners and deliberately led
