MEMPHIS, Jan. 5.- Florence Bolan, the
5- year- old daughter of Pandy Bolan of the
Memphis Baseball club, was assaulted by
" Yally" Taylor, a negro boy, in Chelsea, a
suburb. The black boy narrowly escaped
reformlynching, and Bolan tried to kill him, but
Taylor hurried off to the police station
in a patrol was wagon.
