CLOSE AFTER HIS VICTIM.
An Arkansas Prisoner Lynched for Murdering
a Deputy Sheriff.
MORRILLTON, Ark., May 24.- Charles
Stewart, prisoner confined in the Perry
county jail, was lynched this morning.
Last night Stewart seized an iron
poker and struck
Deputy Sheriff
Holmes across the head, dashing out
his brains. When the news of the murder
spread through the streets this
morning an armed body of men was
quickly organized. They marched to
the jail, and, taking Stewart from
his cell, hanged him to the
tree. nearest
