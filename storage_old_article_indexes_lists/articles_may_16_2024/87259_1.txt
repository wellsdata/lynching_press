Attempt by a Pennsylvania Mob to Cremate
a Colored Fainly.
NEWCASTLE, April 28 .-- At 3 o'clock this
morning a second attempt was made to cremate
the family of David Simpson, a colored
man, whose wife is an important witness
against young Skelly, arrested for arson.
Investigation led to the discovery of a woman's
foot- marks leading to the Skelly
House. Arrests will follow. The first attempt
was made to burn the house of Simp.
On early last Saturday morning. The old
barely escaped death before the fire
couple was extinguished, bring almost auffocated.
