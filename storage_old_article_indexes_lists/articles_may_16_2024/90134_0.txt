PENNSYLVANIA MOB
BEING GATHERED IN
Eight of Men Who Burned Negro Under
Arrest.
Coatsville, Pa., Aug. 19- With eight
persons under arrest in connection with
lynching a negro here last Sunday
night, the Chester county authorities
are still pushing the investigation vigorously
, and interesting developments
are promised.
It is reported that at least three of
the arrested men have made confessions
implicating the leaders of the
mob which burned Walker. The
confessions
are said to involve leading citizens
. One of the five arrested last
night, it is said, will later have to answer
to the charge of murder in
