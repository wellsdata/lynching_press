AN ATTEMPTED ASSAULT
Promptly Followed by a Lynching at
Kesley, Georgia
Albany, Ga., Sept. 6.- John Towns,
the negro arrested at Kesley yesterday
morning and placed in the town calaboose
, was taken out last night by a
mob of a hundred men, hung to the
limb of a tree near the track of the
Georgia, Florida and Alabama rail
road and his body literally shot to
pieces. Towns entered the home of a
white farmer, Joseph Wheeler, Friday
night and had his hands Mrs.
