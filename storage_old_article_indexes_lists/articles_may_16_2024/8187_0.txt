LYNCHING AS AN EPIDEMIC.
The Colored Population of Louisiana Being
Properly Decreased.
SHREVEPORT, La., Jan. 14- News was
received last night of the lynching by a
mob of fifty men of the negro Nathan
Andrews, who yesterday morning shot
and wounded William Driscoll, manager
of the Cash Point plantation in
Caddo parish. The shooting of Driscoll
occurred in the same neighborhood in
which about three weeks ago the negro
Patterson Kad two white men, fits
wife and a man, for which he was
subsequently negro lynched.
