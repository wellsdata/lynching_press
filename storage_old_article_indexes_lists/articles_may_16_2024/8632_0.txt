A New Departure.
ATLANTA, Ga., Sept. 6 .-- A delegation
of citizens called on Governor Gordon
yesterday and asked him to take action
concerning the whipping of a lot of ne-
groes by unknown white men Thursday
night at East Point, near Atlanta. The
affair grew out of the lynching of a negro
boy on Wednesday, The negroes held a
mass meeting and the citizens becoming
scared sent for the police from Atlanta.
The presence of the officers prevented
further trouble, but after they had gone
a lot of white men went to different
cabins and whipped the negroes The
.
Governor has offered a reward of$ 100 for
person convicted of having taken
any part in the affair.
