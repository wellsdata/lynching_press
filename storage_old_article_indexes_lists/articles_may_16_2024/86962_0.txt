A Black Jack.
OCOLA, Fla., May 4.- A number of
women have been attacked recently by a
tramp negro, who cut them with a knife
after the manner of" Jack, the Ripper."
It is reported the body of a colored girl
was found in the woods last night, slashed
horribly. The negroes are in arms search
for the murderer. He will be
if found. lynched
