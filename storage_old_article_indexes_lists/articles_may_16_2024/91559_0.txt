LEAVENWORTH, Kans .. Jan. 13.-
Judge Moore to- day issued a warrant
against Fred Alexander, the negro who
attempted to assault Miss Roth yesterday
, and to- night the Sheriff went to
Lansing to serve it. Many
persons
thought that Alexander would be brought
back here to- night and a large crowd of
determined men armed with guns and
ropes gathered at the jail. But the
prisoner was left at Lansing, where he
will remain until the officials deem it
to bring him here. safe
