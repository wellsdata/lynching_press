Mob Hangs Negro.
Wewoka, Okla., Nov. 5.- John Cudjo,
negro, accused of killing deputy sher-
iff John Dennis, was captured by a
posse and brought to Wewoka, where
he was seized by a mob and hanged to
a telephone pole in front of the court
house. Cudjo, it is said, killed the
deputy sheriff as the officer was
ing to arrest him. try-
