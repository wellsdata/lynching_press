German Workers
Present Scottsboro
Protest to U. S.
BERLIN, Oct., 24.- A delegation
sent by a mass meeting of three
thousand workers to the American
Embassy to protest against the Scotts
boro lynch veridct found the embassy
building heavily guarded by police.
The delegation was at first refused
admission to present their protest.
Finally, however, as a result of the
gathering of hundreds of workers in
the streets, two of members were
permitted to enter in the presence of
detectives. They vehemently protest-
ed against the presence of the detectives
and secured their withdrawal.
They then presented the protest resolution
of the meeting to the Sec-
retary of the Embassy. The secretary
promised to forward it to the
proper quarters. During the course
of the morning additional delegations
arrived from other workers
and organizations. meetings
