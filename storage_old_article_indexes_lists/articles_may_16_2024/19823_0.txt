LYNCHED FOR CRIME
Two Negroes Make Full Confession
To a Mob in Ala

MALCOLM, Ala., Sept. 3.- John and
Lewis Balm, negroes, were lynched late
last night near Jackson, Ala., for the
murder of Sheriff J. L. Wainwright, according
to a long distance telephone
message received here today. The
tims made a full confession. vic
