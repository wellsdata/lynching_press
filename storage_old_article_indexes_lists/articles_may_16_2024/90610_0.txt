Memphis, Tenn., March 3.- Earle
Trice, a young colored boy barely
escaped being lynched here day before
yesterday. Trice was accused of
attacking the ten year daughter of
Patrolman J. A. Cruse on Hernando
Road. The alleged attempt was not
verified and if the facts are known
it will appear that the girl was merely
frightened by Trice, who was
ing from his work. return-
