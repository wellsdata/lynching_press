A Murderous Young Desperado.
LYNCHURG, Va, Dec. 9.- Charlie B. Palmer,
a gambler and low character of Chatham, Pitt-
sylvania county, while on a carouse, yesterday,
at Sago, that county, accosted William Akers,
with whom he had an altercation, and commenced
shooting. Several shots took effect, and
Akers soon died. Palmer then beat an inoffensive
negro nearly to death, and fled. A
posse of citizens has been searching for him all
day, but without success. They are greatly incensed
, and their purpose is to lynch him if he is
caught. Though only eighteen. Palmer has become
notorious throughout that section as
swindler and desperado. a
