Five Negroes Lynched.
Gainsville, Fla, Five negroes,
three men and two women, were taken
from the jail at Newberry, Fla.,
and hanged by a mob and another negro
was shot and killed by deputy
sheriffs near Jonesville, Fla., as the
result of the killing of Constable S.
G. Wynne, the shooting of Dr. L.
G. Harris by and Boisey Long, a negro.
