Mob Forms to Lynch a Negro.
Alexandria, La., May 31.- A tele-
phone message from Bijou, a small
town west of here, tonight, said that a
mob was forming to lynch a negro
accused of criminal assault. Assistance
was requested, and the sheriff
and one deputy left Alexandria on
horseback for Bijou. The town is on
railroad, but no train starts for that
place a for several hours.
