CHILDREN KIDNAPED.
Enticed From Their Homes at Peters
burg, Ind., by Unknown Parties
Petersburg, Ind., Feb. 21.- Leonard
Duwese and Mattie Barnes, 12 and 14
years old, children of wealthy farmers
living five miles south of town, were kidnaped
today. Officers are scouring the
country for the kidnapers and lynching
is talked of if they are captured. The
children were enticed away from their
early this morning by a man and
homes woman in a buggy.
