MOB LYNCHES NEGRO
AFTER CONVICTION.
Poplarville, Miss., April 18. Cland
Singleton, a negro convicted last night
of the murder last March of E. J.
Bounds, a white railroad worker, was
taken from the county jail here early
today and hanged to a tree near the
outskirts of the town.
A mob of
about a hundred persons previously
forced the sheriff to turn over the
keys to the jail.
Singleton was arrested about a week
ago, His trial opened yesterday mora-
ing and he was convicted and sen-
tenced to life imprisonment before
adjournment was taken last night.
The coroner's jury tonight brought
in a verdict to the effect that Singleton
came to death at the hands
