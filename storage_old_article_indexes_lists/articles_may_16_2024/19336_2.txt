He Had Killed His Man.
BIRMINGHAM, Ala, January 28 .-[ Special.]
Jim Seams, the negro who killed Deputy Sheriff
Autry, of Tuscaloosa county, last Tuesday,
was brought to this city for sale keeping. An
effort to lynch him was made at Tuscaloosa,
and the sheriff brought him here under a
strong guard. The negro talked freely about
the crime today, and said he guessed he would
be suspended where he could kick at the
United States, but it all right, as he had
