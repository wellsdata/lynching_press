Executed in Virginia for Attempted
Assault on a White Woman.
Richmond, Va., Sept. 2 .-- Anderson
Finch, colored, was hanged at Boydton
today for an attempted assault on Mrs.
C. A. Geoghegan of Chase City. Doc
Bacon, another negro, had been condemned
to die with Finch for complicity
in the crime, but was reprieved by
the governor today until Friday to permit
further examination into his case.
The negroes narrowesly escaped
ing at the time of the crime. lynch
