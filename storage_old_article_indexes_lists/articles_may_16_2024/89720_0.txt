WINSTON, N. C., Aug. 12.- It was
announced in the colored churches
' yesterday that a mob would lynch
Arthur Tuttle, colored, on trial for
the murder of a policeman. One
hundred and fifty negroes marched
to the jail armed with shotguns.
The sheriff ordered out the Forsythe
riflemen, and the negroes fired on
them, wounding several. The rifle
men fired several volleys at the
negroes, who dispersed, carrying off
wounded. Fourteen have been
the arrested.
