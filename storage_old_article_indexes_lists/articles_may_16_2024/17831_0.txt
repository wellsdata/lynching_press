Negro Lynched Por
( By the Associated Press.)
Richmond, Va., March 22.- A rumor,
which reached here last night of a lynching
in Halifax county, has been confirmed.
A negro sent on for court by a magistrate
on the charge of burning the stables
of a Mr. De Jarnette, was taken by fifty
unknown men from a constable who was
taking him to the county seat and shot
death. None of the mob could be
identified to.
