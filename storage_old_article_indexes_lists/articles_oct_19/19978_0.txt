AN INDIANA MOB BENT ON A
LYNCHING
INDIANAPOLIS, Ind., Dec. 2.- The
county jail, situated in the center of
the city, was surrounded by a mob of
three hundred infuriated people short-
ly before midnight last night thirsting
for the blood of Charles Birsow, who is
contined within its walls. The man is
an all- around criminal, and his latest
erime was an outrageous assault on a
little girl named Blanche Staton, 7
years of age, living in the northeastern
part of the city.
A few men gathered in the rear of a
Falcon in the same portion of the city,
and this was soon added to. A descent
was made upon the jail by fifty sturdy
fellows, and in the march a force of 200
men joined.
The jail. a flimsy old structure, was
easily entered, but the mob got no
farther than the strong doars in the
office. At one time they were upon the
point of going into the jail with the
blows of sledgehammers and prying of
crowbars, but the sheriff and a posse of
officers cleared the room by a
