MOB LYNCHES NEGRO
WHO ADMITS ATTACK
Monticello, March 22.- Phil Slater,
negro, 50 years old, who tonight con
fessed that he had assaulted a white
woman near Wilmar last week, to
night was taken from the jail here
by force and lynched. In making his
confession he said:" I did it, but
please give me a trial." Already in
the hands of the mob, he was strung
from a telephone
cable by a rope
