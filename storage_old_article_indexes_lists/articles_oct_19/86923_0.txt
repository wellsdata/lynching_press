POSSE LYNCHES NEGROES
One of Blacks Caught in Girl's Room
and Infamous Murder Plan
Is Disclosed.
Athens, Ga., Sept. 7.- Caught in an
attempt to commit arson, assault and
murder, five negroes were lynched in
an isolated part of Clarke county by
neighbors of the family against whom
the blacks had plotted.

daughter of J. W. Huff, a planter,
