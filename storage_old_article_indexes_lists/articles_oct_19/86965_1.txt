Would- Be Lynchers Repulsed.
COLUMBIA, S. C., March 10.- A mob
of several hundred men procured a small
cannon and rode into the town of Spar-
tansburg today for the avowed purpose of
attacking the jail and taking there from
George Sumon, who shot and killed his
brother- in law, Edward Fisher, on Friday.
When the mob arrived at the public
square Mayor Henniman, at the head of
the police force, and with a pistol in his
hand, repulsed the mob, spiked the can
non and locked it up.
