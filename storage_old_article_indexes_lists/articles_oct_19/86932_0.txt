NEGRO SLAYER IS LYNCHED
Mob in Pursuit of Accomplice in Mur
der of Mississippi White
Man.
Meridian, Miss., Aug. 6.- One negro
was lynched and a mob is in pursuit
of another near Hall's Station, Ala., 40
miles east of here, according to passengers
arriving here.
Two are said to have murdered
a negroes white man named Tutt.
