Lynching at Tullahoma.
Will Lewis, a negro boy 19 years
of ag, a was taken from the calaboose
at Tullahoma last Monday night by
seven or eight masked men, and
hanged to a tree near by until dead.
Lewis was frequently drunk and disorderly
, and was impudent and
troublesome while under the influence
of liquor, but aside from this
had a very fair reputation. A large
public gathering of citizens of Tulla
homa was held on Tuesday, to ex
press their indignation over
