Government Officials Condemn Collinsville Hanging.
WASHINGTON, April 5.- Attorney General Gregory took the report of
the lynching of Praeger at Collinsville, Illinois, to the cabinet meeting today
and it is expected that the government will denounce the lawless action of the
mob and express a hope that there will be no repetition of the unlawful offense
elsewhere the nation. Government officials universally condemn the tyne
ing as a shame in to the American nation.
