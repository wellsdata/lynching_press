LYNCHING SUSPECTED.
Newnan Excited Over Rumors in the
Howard Case.
NEWNAN, GA., June 4.- The whole city
is in a high state of excitement over the
reported lynching of Ben Scraggs, the negro
fiend who assaulted Mrs. Ida Howard on
yesterday. It is hard to get reliable news,
as the current rumors are conglomerated to
a great extent. It would necessarily be
very difficult for the lady to identify the
negro, as his face was concealed with a
handkerchief, leaving nothing but his eyes
exposed to view. During the interview
had with her yesterday, she said that she
would know the voice of the negro, and it
is probable and believed by the greater
portion of the people that he was done
away with in some manner unknown to
