FASHIONABLE SPORT.- Some boys in
Petersburg the other day followed the
example of" Old America" by indulging in
the sport of lynching a little negro. They
took the negro boy, and after flogging him,
concluded to hang him, which resolution
they proceeded to carry into effect by
swinging him up; but fortunately a gentle
man passing by relieved the little darkey
from his unpleasant situation. Young
America is hard on the heels of the" old
ones" in modern practices of rascality
