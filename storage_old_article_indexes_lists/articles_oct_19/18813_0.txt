SCOTT ESCAPED THE MOB
Officials Took the Accused Negro from
Fort Scott to the Girard Jail for.
Safe Keeping.
Fort Scott, Kan., Aug. 24.- Jasper
Scott, the negro who brutally assaulted
and robbed Mrs. Taylor, an
elderly lady at her home in this city
Thursday afternoon, and who last
night confessed, has temporarily es
caped mob violence by being taken
to the Crawford county jail at Girard.
As far as can be learned there is no
talk of going to the Crawford county
jail after him. The jail here was
surrounded nearly all 1st night
a mob, thirsting for him. by
