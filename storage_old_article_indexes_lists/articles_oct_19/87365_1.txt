Vengeance on a Brutal Negro.
Nashville, Tenn, July 22.- A special
from Florence, Ala., to the Banner,
says: A report from Riverton this
morning says that the negro caught
yesterday started for the United States
prison, guarded by a party. At Cherokee
he was met by a mob and hanged.
Another report, which is not credited,
says he was tied to a stake and burned
to death. He has certainly been punished
for his crime. His identity was
thoroughly established by his victim,
Vaughn. Mrs. Vaughn's condition
is Mrs. serious.
