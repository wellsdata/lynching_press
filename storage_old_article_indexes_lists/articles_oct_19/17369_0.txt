San Francisco has been again visited by a tremendous
and destructive fire, which has destroyed property
to the amount of$ 15,000,000.
Amongst the buildings destroyed are the Custom
House, Union, National, Delmonico's, Exchange, City
and New World Hotels. Much shipping in the harbour
has likewise been consumed.
The greatest distress prevails.
The news from the mines is favourable,
