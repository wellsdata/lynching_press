LYNCHING IN MINNESOTA.
National Associated Press to the Star.
ST. PAUL, MINN., Nov. 11.- Michael and
John Walde, living in Todd County, con
fessed to the cowardly murder of a man
named Coldale and his friend. John was
taken from the jail at Long Prairie Saturday
night by a mob and hanged to a tree,
it is thought that Michael, who was
and afterwards arrested shared the same fate.
