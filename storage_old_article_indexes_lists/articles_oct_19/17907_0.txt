NEGRO LYNCHED
IN MISSISSIPPI
By Associated Press.
Meridian, Miss. June 25.- Information
received to- day states that an un
known negro was quickly lynched by
an unknown mob at DeKalb, Kemper
county, for an attempt to criminally
assault Mrs. James Young, the proprietor
of a store. The negro darted
behind the counter and rushed to
wara Mrs. Young, when she drew a
pistol The negro escaped but was
captured. by the mob and lynched.
