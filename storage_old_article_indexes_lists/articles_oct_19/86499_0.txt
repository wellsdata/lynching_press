Mob Hangs Colored Boy
For Killing Police Chief
WOODBURY, Ga., April 4.- Beach
Trash, 15 year old negro, was hanged
by a mob yesterday afternoon.
He was employed by a bank and
Albert Wells, Chief of Police caught
him taking money from the till.
The boy grabbed a gun and shot
Wells through the head. The hang-
followed Wells died last night.
