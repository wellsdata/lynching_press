A Lynching Party Failed.
SULLIVAN, Ind., Jan. 27.- A mob endeavored
to lynch Grant Atterbury, who
is under arrest on a charge of assaulting
his sister- in- law, but was driven off by
the sheriff. The door of the jail was bro
ken down and an entrance forced. The
sheriff confronted the mob and threatened
to kill the first man who came up stairs to
ward the cellroom. The mob halted and
finally withdrew, but threatened to return
within 24 hours. Atterbury strongly asserts
his innocence, and the only evidence
against him is that bloodhounds
