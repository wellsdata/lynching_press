Mob Deals Out Justice to a Quartette

NASHVILLE, Tenn., April 28.- A tele
phone message from Goodletsville,
small place some twelve miles from
this city, states that four
negro
men have been lynched at that
place and two other parties shot at.
The cause of the lynching was that the
negroes brutally assaulted two daughters
of a white family.
