Hollis' Friends Threaten Vengeance.
DEADWOOD, Dak., Aug. 28.- Great
excitement prevailed yesterday in
Sturgis, the scene of the lynching of
Ross Hollis, the colored soldier, assassin
of Dr. Lynch. A number of colored
soldiers of Fort Meade, members of the
Twenty- fifth United States Infantry,
armed themselves and proceeded to the
town of Sturgis, threatening to burn the
place. On being advised of the trouble,
General Sturgis, commanding at Fort
Meade, detailed a patrol of the Seventh
