NEGRO LYNCHED
REWARD PUT UP
FRANKFORT, Ky., March 14.- Gov.
Edwin P. Morrow today offered a re
ward of$ 500 each for the arrest and
conviction of each member of the mob
that early yesterday took from the
Woodford county jail and lynched
Richard James, negro. He also issued
a proclamation removing from office
John H. Edgar, jailer of
county. Woodford
