Arrested On a Serious Charge.
J. P. Bramblett, a wealthy bachelor
of Carlisle, was arrested yesterday at
that place on a warrant sworn out by a
Mrs. Shaw, aged about 25 years, charging
him with attempted rape. There
were numerous rumors on the streets
yesterday afternoon to the effect that a
mob was organizing to lynch the ac
cased, and that a race was on between
the Sheriff and his deputies and the
mob. The NEWS called up Carlise over
the telephone and learned that there
was nothing in the rumors save the
mere fact of his being arrested. Mr.
Bramblett is a very influential citizen
of Nicholas county and his friends
claim he will have no trouble of
his innocence. proving
