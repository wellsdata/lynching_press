LYNCHED A MURDERER.
Summary Justice Administered to a Fiend
in Louisiana.
NEW ORLEANS, April 23.- A county
visitor from Smithland brings news of
the murder and lynching in Pointe
Couple parish of a man named Free
man, who entered Cotton's store and
demanded the surrender of the cash on
penalty of death. Cotton refused and
Freeman put his threat into execution,
after which he fled. A posse pursued
captured and strung him up to a tree.,
