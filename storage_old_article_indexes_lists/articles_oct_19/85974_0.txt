Let the Prisoners Go to Escape Lyachers.
HUNTINGTON. W. Va., Aug. 30.- The authorities
of Lincoln county last evening arrested
John Peyton, his son Elisha, and Wilton Gross
on the charge of assassinating Albert Keyser on
Saturday night. While the prisoners were un

Adkins& mob appeared to lynch them. The
three men were turned loose and ran for their
lives into the mountains, escaping the mob or
they would have strung up. The greatest
