A Negro Rapist Lynched.
PHILADELPHIA, May 12- A dispatch
from Centerville, Md., says there is intense
excitement among the colored population
over the lynching, this morning, of
Green, the rapist. Tonight they are talking
of vengeance and a patrol of 100
wh tes is making the rounds of the
streets. When Green was taken from
jail early this morning he was dragged
with a rope several hundred yards to the
point where he was hanged, and being
clad only in his underclothing was terribly
injured. fact, he was perhaps dead
before being In hanged.
