Negro Assailant Lynched.
John Jones, the negro assailant of
Mrs. Banister, was lynched at Carters
ville, Ga., near the scene of his crime.
Judge A. W. Fite made a speech in an
attempt to restrain the crowd of about
200, but it was useless. The negro's
body was with bullets, niore than
500 shots riddled being fired.
