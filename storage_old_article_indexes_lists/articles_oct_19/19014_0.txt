Louisiana Negro
Is Lynched After
Slaying of Girl
Labadieville, La., Oct. 12 .-( AP)
A Negro arrested yesterday for questioning
in connection with the slay-
holding of Miss Louisiana Larose, 15- year-
old white girl whose body was found
in a field was taken from the Labadie-
ville jail carly today and lynched by
a mob of unidentified men.
The Negro, whose name was not
disclosed at the time of his arrest, was
seized quietly by the mob and hanged
from a girder of a bridge. When early
risers in the community saw the
body at dawn and officers cut it down
