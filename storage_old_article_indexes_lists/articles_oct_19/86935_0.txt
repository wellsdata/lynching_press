APPEAL SAVES DYING SLAYER
Would- be Lynchers Threaten to Re
turn for Intended Victim if
He Fails to Die.
Olive Branch, Miss., Nov. 29.- A
mob determined to lynch G. W. Tread-
way, the only survivor of the pitched
battle in which four men were killed,
visited the home where Treadway lies
desperately wounded.
The family physician addressed the
mob leaders and upon his representations
that Treadway was in a dying
conditon the would- be lynchers departed
.
