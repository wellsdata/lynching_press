Omaha Not Ashamed of
Mob Rule and Lynching
Even Women Boast of Part
Taken in Deed- Mayor
Furnishes Joke.
Omaha, Oct, 4.- Citizens show no indications
today that they are ashamed
of the actions of the mob which burned
the Douglas county million- dollar court
house in its efforts to lynch William
Brown, a negro. They laugh and joke
about the lynching and burning of the
negro. They even laugh at the attack
made upon Mayor Edward P. Smith be
cause that official would not order
