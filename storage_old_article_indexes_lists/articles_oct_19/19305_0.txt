The Beauty of Mob Law.
ELDORADO. Kan., Sept. 28- At Rosalia,
Kan, Mr. and Mrs Edwards were suspected
of of killing the 3 year- old child of
Henry Bloomer. A mob hung them to a
tree and frightened them in a confession
of the crime. They were placed in jail te
await trial and the sher.ff prevented their
death by lynching only by armed force.
After days confinement the child was
found two alive and well.
