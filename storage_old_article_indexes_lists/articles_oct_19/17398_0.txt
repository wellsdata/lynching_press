The tyranny of Slavery is well illustrated
in the case of proceedings of a meeting in
Sumner county Tennessee. Lynch law
was adopted by a solomen resolution, which
reads as follows:
" Resolved, That the various magistrates
of the county hand over the leaders of the
conspiracy to a jury of twelve slaveholders
of this county, appointed by this meeting.
which jury shall try said slaves and deal
with them as the interest of the community
may require: said jury to adopt such regulations
in regard to the trial as they
