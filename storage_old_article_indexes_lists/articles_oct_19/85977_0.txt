LOUISVILLE, August 17.- Logan Murphy,
a desperado and murderer, was hanged
near Mount Sterling this morning by an
armed mob of 300 men, who took him from
jail by force. He was taken to a railroad
trestle, a rope put about his neck, and
pushed off. In September, 1891, Murphy
killed his father, near Sawyersville, be
cause he objected to his cooking a stolen
turkey in his house. He was brought here
for safe keeping, and sentenced to hang
Jan. 8. He was granted a respite, pending
an application for a rehearing. The body,
