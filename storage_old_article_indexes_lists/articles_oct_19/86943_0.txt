Mob Seeks to Lynch Negro.
Bay Springs, Miss., July 10.- The
Jail here was in a state of siege. A
mob, seeking to lynch Mose Johnson,
a negro, who confessed to killing one
man and wounding two others in an
attempt to rob a pay car, was at the
doors. Sheriff Jones ordered 30 additional
deputies to reinforce his posse
of 13, and his captive to an in
ner room and moved barricaded the jail.
