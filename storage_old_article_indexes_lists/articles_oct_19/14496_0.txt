Mob Lynches Negroes.
Jessup, Ga.- James Harvey and
Joe Jordan, Negroes, convicted of
criminal assault and respited by Governor
Hardwick, were taken from a
deputy sheriff near Lanes Bridge, Ga.,
and lynched, a telephone message to
Sheriff Rogers here said. The Negroes
were being removed from
Wayne county to Savannah for safe
keeping. J. R. Tyre, deputy in charge
of the prisoners, reported the
