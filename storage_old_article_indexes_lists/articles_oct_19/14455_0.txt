They Cried" Lynch Him!"
N. J., noon
Hudson Green, a negro, entered the house
of Isaac F. Phillips, a bank teller of New
York, at 434 Maple avenue, and assaulted
Mrs. Phillips. The woman fought desperately
. Her screams brought Charles
W. Downes to her assistance. He grabbed
the negro, but the latter made a lounge at
him with a knife, nearly severing a finger.
The negro then fled, pursued by an infuriated
mob who cried" Lynch him!" He was
finally arrested. Mrs. Phillips is
pletely prostrated. com
