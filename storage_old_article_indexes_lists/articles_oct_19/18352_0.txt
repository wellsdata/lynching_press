Excitement Over a Shooting.
Nicholasville, Ky., Feb. 8.- The
town is somewhat excited over the
shooting of Newton Lane by one of
his work hands on the Fayette line.
A mob was organized here to lynch
Lane when he killed H. C. Roden
baugh and his son in Versailles about
four years ago. Rodenbaugh was
very popular here, where he had lived.
At the request of Mrs. Rodenbaugh
the mob dispersed. Lane was arrest
ed here about months ago for
using abusive language nine to a Negro.
