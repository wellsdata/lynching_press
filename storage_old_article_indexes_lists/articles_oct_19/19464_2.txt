SHOT IN WIFE'S SIGHT.
Richard Hone, Wealthy Floridian,
Killed From Ambush.
West Palm Beach, Fla., Oct. 21.-
Richard Hone, a well- to- do pineapple
grower, living three miles south of
this town, on Lake Worth, was shot
and instantly killed at 7 o'clock last
night, while sitting at a table with
Mrs. Hone, writing a letter.
Robbery is supposed to have been
the motive of the murderer, who has
so far not been found. A posse is on
trail, and if caught he will be
his
lynched. Telegrams have been sent to
different points for bloodhounds.
The affair has created intense excitement
owing to the prominence and
popularity, of the victim.
