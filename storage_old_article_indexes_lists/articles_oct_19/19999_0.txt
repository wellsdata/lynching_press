PRICES ON THEIR HEADS.
South Carolina's Governor Offers
Reward for the Barnwell Lynch
ers.
COLUMBIA, S. C., Dec. 30.- In view
of the lynching of eight negroes at
Barnwell on Saturday last, Gov. Rich
ardson has issued a proclamation offer
ing a reward of$ 200 for the apprehension
and conviction of each and every
of the parties concerned in the kill
one ing of the negroes referred to.
