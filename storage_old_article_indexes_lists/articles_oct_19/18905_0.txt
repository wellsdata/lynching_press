ALL A HOAX.- There has been a report in
circulation, and got into the papers of this State
as well as some of the Philadelphia papers,
that a certain Alfred Archer, a resident of Mil
ford for the last two years, and formerly of New
Jersey, left Milford and went down to Virgin
ia, and that the Postmaster in Milford wrote
to the Postmaster in Virginia that a live Abolitionist
was in their midst, and forth with the
mob took Archer and hung him. The whole
thing has turned out to be a hoax. Archer is
now in New Jersey, living with his father, and
has written a letter to his friends in Milford,
and was in Philadelphia last week, with his
friends there. Such men, to get up such
story, should be punished. W. a
