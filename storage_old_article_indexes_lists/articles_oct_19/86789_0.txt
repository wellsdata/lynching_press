Sandusky, September 8th, at a zens
meeting, held in the park to take action
in relation to bringing the lynchers of
Wm Taylor to justice, the resolutions to
that effect were hooted down bythe mob.
The better class of people endorsed the
resolutions, and a committee has been
appointed to employ counsel, bring the
matter before the grand jury, and take
such steps as will accomplish the
purpose. desired
