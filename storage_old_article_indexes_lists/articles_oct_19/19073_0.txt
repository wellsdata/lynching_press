TWO ARE STRUNG UP.
Lexington( Mo.) Mob Lynches Alleged
Murderers.
Lexington, Mo., Aug. 13.- At 1:30
a. m., a mob numbering some two or
three hundred masked men battered
down the doors of the jail, located in
the courtyard here, and secured there
from Charles Salyers, white, and Har
ry Gates, colored, who one week ago
murdered George W. Johnson, and
hanged them a tree a short distance
south of town to.
