New Orleans, Dec. 28.- Tilman Green,
the colored trusted carriage driver in a
prominent family living ten miles from
Columbia, in this state, was lynched yesterday
for an attempted outrage on his
mistress. He broke into her room at
night, while her husband was in attendance
at a Christmas party elsewhere.
She escaped by the rear of the house and
gave the alarm. Green was arrestet.
He confessed bis design and was lynched
by a mob, in which a number of
of the neighborhood participated. negroes
