A Murderer Shot and Lynched,
GALVESTON, June 26.- A News' special.
from Bryan, Texas, dated June 26, says:
Last night a large crowd of men took
Henry Cook, the assassin of J. P. Farmer,
out of jail, and shot and hung him inside
the prison yard. J. T. Nelson, his accomplice
, it is said, was told to leave the State
at once. The jail was guarded by forty
three men, but they were powerless to pre
vent the hanging Cook against the large
number of about 200 of men.
