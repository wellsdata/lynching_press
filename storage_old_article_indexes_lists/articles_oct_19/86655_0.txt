MOB TAKES MAN
FROM JAIL AND
STRINGS HIM UP
Slayer of Key West Citizen
Handled Roughly at Hands
of Masked Mob.
KEY WEST, Fla., Dec, 27.- Manuel
Head, coffee house owner, was taken
from jail here early this morning by
a masked mob and lynched as the result
of shooting and killing W. H.
Decker, prominent resident.
After Head had been taken from
the jail and given a severe flogging,
was hanged while unconscious to
he a telephone pole and his body riddled
with bullets.
Head early last evening stood off
a mob and was rescued by marines
and, hustled to jail. The marines
guarded the jail until relieved by
deputies who surrendered Head
to the mob. later
