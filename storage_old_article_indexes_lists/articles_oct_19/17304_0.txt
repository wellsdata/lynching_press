LYNCH LAW ON THE MISSISSIPPI.- On the steam"
ship Prometheus, from California for New Orleans,
two parties had taken passage; one of them, J.
Henry Martin, had been tried and acquitted in
California for stealing$ 1,000. His accusers were
also on the Prometheus, and on getting into the
river, they drew revolvers on him, and forced him
to give them the$ 1,000 which they alleged he had
stolen from them. One of the three was
wards arrested in New Orleans for the outrage after.
