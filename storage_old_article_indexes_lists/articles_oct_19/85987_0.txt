Another Negro Lynched.
LOUISVILLE, Sept. 6.
On Tuesday a negro named Lewis Otis
committed rape on a Miss Strange of Jes.
semine county The negro was arrested on
the following day and confined in the jail
at Nicholasville county seat. At an examining
trial this morning he plead guilty
and was remanded for the action of the
circuit court. Soon after the jailor was
overpowered by the mob headed by the
father of the girl and the negro taken
side of town and hanged to a tree. out
