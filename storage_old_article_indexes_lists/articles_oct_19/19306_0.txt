Troops Needed at Mayfield, Ky., to Pre
vent the Lynching of a Negro
MAYFIELD, Ky., Jan. 12.- There. is
great excitement here over the threat-
ened lynching of Dick Milner, a negro
arrested on Wednesday for assaulting a
white woman near Wingo. A mob at
tempted to board the train on which the
prisoner was brought to Mayfield, but
the sheriff succeeded in getting Milner
safely to jail. Last evening the mob was
greatly reinforced and seemed deter
mined to get the negro and hang him.
The authorities have barricaded the jail,
placed a strong guard inside and have
telegraphed the governor asking for
itary assistance. mil
