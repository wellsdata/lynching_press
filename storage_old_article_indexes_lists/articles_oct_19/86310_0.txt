ARTIST ADMITS
TAKING PART
IN LYNCHING
OMAHA, Oct. 15( by Associated
ress) .- Detectives say that Leonard
Webster, an advertising artist who is
under arrest, has confessed to taking
part in the lynching of a negro, the
burning of the court house and the
attempted lynching of Mayor Smith
recently.
It is stated that Webster admitted
having struck the mayor over the head
with a gun Webster is employed by
the World- Herald..
