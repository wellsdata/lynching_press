Two Mexicans, named Santos Peraltas and Joaquin of
Valenzuela, have been hanged by lynch law in Santa
Barbara county, the former for complicity in
Baratier murder, and the latter for other crimes. the

A negro was hanged by lynch law last week, near
Stockton, for having trespassed upon the bed of a
married white man. The woman had a colored child.
The father attempted to kill the negro by shooting
but was prevented from accomplishing his purpose.,
