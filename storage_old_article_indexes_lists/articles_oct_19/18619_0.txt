ST. LOUIS MOB FOILED.
Five Hundred Negroes Try to Lynch a
Black Murderer- Police Intervene.
ST. LOUIS, Mo., Dec. 2.- While
Alexander Royal, the negro who killed
Jessie Sims with a butcher knife Fri
day morning, was being taken from
the coroner's office to the morgue about
11 o'clock Saturday, 500 wildly excited
negroes made an effort to take him
from the police at the corner of
Eleventh street and Clarke avenue,
but reinforcements were quickly sent
from the Four Courts, close by, and
the mob vas beaten off and dispersed.
The design of the was to lynch
the murderer. crowd
