E. L. Thomas, of Whiting, said there
was not an open saloon in Nemaha county,
but plenty of joints. The county attorney,
however, was trying to do his duty, and
was arresting jointists right and left. The
speaker said he had been pushing the
fight himself, and that twice within a few
months a mob had been organized to lynch
him; but, he said, the leaders did not
