Negro Brute Narrowly Escapes Lynching.
HICKSVILLE, O., Oct. 22.- Charles
O'Neill, a negro, was arrested early
Sunday morning charged with outrag
ing Harriet Heckerman, the 3- year- old
daughter of Mrs. Daniel Heckerman.
The child was discovered unconscious
in a barn, and when she came to, she told
how she had been carried there by
O'Neill. The latter admitted the
charge, and for a time it was thought
he would be lynched. The law prevailed
, however, and O'Neill was
landed in jail. safely
