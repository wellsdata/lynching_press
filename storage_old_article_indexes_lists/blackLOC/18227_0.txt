( By The Associated Negro Press)
Cairo, Ill., Aug. 15.- Nine members
of the mob which sought to lynch three
suspects of the murder of a young
white woman have been indicted by
the Pulaski Circuit Court grand jury.
If convicted after trial they are subject
to penalties of one year in prison
or a$ 1000 fine. Two of the suspects
Hess Connors, 22, and Fred Hale, 19,
were tried and found guilty. Connors
